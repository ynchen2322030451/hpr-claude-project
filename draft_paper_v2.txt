摘要 / Abstract
================================================================================

We develop a probabilistic surrogate framework for coupled neutronics–thermal–structural
analysis of a heat-pipe-cooled reactor (HPR), enabling end-to-end
uncertainty-to-risk quantification at a fraction of the cost of high-fidelity
simulation.

本文针对热管冷却反应堆（HPR）的耦合中子–热–力分析开发了一套概率代理建模框架，可在远低于
高保真仿真计算成本的条件下，实现从材料不确定性到结构风险的端到端定量分析。

The surrogate is a heteroscedastic multilayer perceptron (HeteroMLP) — a deterministic
neural network outputting predicted mean and variance, not a Bayesian neural network —
trained on approximately 2900 coupled OpenMC–FEniCS simulations, predicting distributions
over 15 physics outputs from 8 material uncertainty inputs across two fidelity levels:
a decoupled single-pass prediction and a converged coupled steady-state.

1. 引言 / Introduction
================================================================================

Heat-pipe-cooled reactors (HPRs) are an emerging class of compact microreactors in which
the core integrates heat pipes, fuel rods, and a structural monolith into a tightly coupled
thermo-mechanical-neutronic system. Neutron transport in the fuel generates volumetric
heat, which is conducted through the SS316 stainless-steel monolith to the heat pipes and
ultimately extracted by the working fluid. The resulting temperature field drives thermal
expansion of the monolith, altering core geometry and material properties, which in turn
modifies neutron transport efficiency. This neutronics–thermal–structural feedback loop
creates a multi-physics system in which each physical domain is inseparable from the others
at steady state.

热管冷却反应堆（HPR）是一类新兴紧凑型微型反应堆，其堆芯将热管、燃料棒和结构单石紧密集成为
一个耦合的热–力–中子系统。燃料中的中子裂变产生体积热功率，热量经SS316不锈钢单石传导至热管
并由工质携带排出；所得温度场驱动单石热膨胀，改变堆芯几何形状和材料性质，进而影响中子输运效率，

Material properties of reactor structural components inevitably carry uncertainties arising
from manufacturing tolerances, material batch variability, and irradiation-induced degradation
over service life. In a coupled multi-physics system such as the HPR, these parameter
uncertainties do not propagate independently: a perturbation in thermal conductivity modifies
temperature gradients, which changes thermal stresses; a perturbation in thermal expansion
modifies core geometry, which shifts neutron multiplication and local power density, which
then feeds back into stress through altered thermal loading. Quantifying how these coupled
uncertainty pathways translate into structural failure risk — the probability of exceeding
the yield strength of the monolith — is a prerequisite for principled safety margin
characterisation and risk-informed design. 【这里讲的多物理耦合还有不确定性分析都太少不够，可以参考我的rpha论文但是要比那个写的多】*（已修改：前：该段仅一句话，不确定性描述极简；后：扩展为完整段落，阐明(1)不确定性来源（制造公差、材料批次、辐照退化）(2)耦合系统中不确定性传播的非独立性（热导→温度梯度→热应力，热膨胀→几何→keff→功率→应力）(3)定量表征的必要性；理由：为后续代理方法的必要性建立充分的物理动机。)

反应堆结构材料的性质不可避免地受到制造公差、材料批次变异和辐照退化的影响而具有不确定性。在
HPR这类多物理耦合系统中，这些参数不确定性并非独立传播：热导率扰动改变温度梯度从而影响热应力；

Existing surrogate approaches each face limitations in this context. Gaussian process (GP)

inference, but without explicit uncertainty calibration or physical consistency constraints,

2. 方法 / Methods

------------------------------------------------------------------------
2.1 材料不确定性模型 / Material Uncertainty Model
------------------------------------------------------------------------

Eight material parameters are treated as independent uncertain inputs spanning
the mechanical and thermal properties of the fuel–monolith–heat-pipe system.
The structural mechanics parameters are: the temperature-dependent Young's modulus
E(T) = E_slope·T + E_intercept (two uncertain components: slope E_slope and intercept
E_intercept), and Poisson's ratio ν. The thermal deformation parameter is the
temperature-dependent thermal expansion coefficient α(T) = α_base + α_slope·T
(two uncertain components). The SS316 stainless-steel thermal transport parameters are:
the reference temperature T_ref, the reference thermal conductivity k_ref, and the
thermal conductivity temperature slope k_slope (the parameter k_slope,SS316 in the code,
labelled SS316_alpha, is the coefficient in the linear model
k_SS316(T) = k_ref + k_slope·(T − T_ref), with units W/(m·K²)). All eight parameters
are assigned independent uniform prior distributions.

The coupled HPR system exhibits two distinct physical pathways through which
material-parameter uncertainty propagates to safety-critical outputs:

(i) Stress pathway: Young's modulus intercept E_intercept is the primary stiffness
parameter — higher values directly amplify thermal stress under the same temperature
gradient via σ ∝ E·α·T. Thermal conductivity k_ref,SS316 governs the temperature
gradient itself: higher conductivity reduces the fuel-to-heat-pipe temperature difference,
decreasing thermal stress; lower conductivity amplifies the gradient. This pathway is
primarily governed by elastic and thermal-transport properties, with relatively limited
direct coupling to the neutronics feedback at first order.

(ii) Reactivity pathway: Thermal expansion coefficient α_base controls core geometric
expansion under heating. In the coupled steady state, higher α_base causes greater
radial and axial expansion of the monolith, increasing the effective neutron-leakage
probability and thereby reducing keff; the resulting decrease in fission power partially
offsets the thermal expansion-driven stress increase. This expansion–leakage–power chain
constitutes a negative reactivity feedback.

2.3 物理规律正则化概率代理模型 / Physics-Regularized Probabilistic Surrogate

The surrogate is a heteroscedastic multilayer perceptron (HeteroMLP) — a deterministic
feedforward network, not a Bayesian neural network (BNN). Unlike BNNs, which place
distributions over network weights and propagate weight uncertainty to output predictions,
the HeteroMLP predicts a point estimate of mean μ(θ) and log-variance log σ²(θ) for each
output using a single deterministic forward pass.【不用说是BNN神经网络吗？】*（已修改：前：未说明HeteroMLP与BNN的关系；后：增加对比说明：HeteroMLP是确定性网络，输出均值+对数方差；BNN将分布置于权重上，推断成本高；两者都能输出不确定性估计，但机制不同。）
At test time: p(yⱼ|θ) = N(μⱼ(θ), σⱼ²(θ)).

【不用说是BNN神经网络吗？】参见上方英文注释。
代理模型为异方差多层感知机（HeteroMLP）——一种确定性前馈网络，而非贝叶斯神经网络（BNN）。
BNN在网络权重上维护概率分布，推断成本较高；HeteroMLP通过单次确定性前向传播同时输出预测均值μ(θ)
和对数方差log σ²(θ)，参数量与标准MLP相当，但自然表达异方差预测不确定性。测试时预测分布为
p(yⱼ|θ)=N(μⱼ(θ), σⱼ²(θ))。

The data-only baseline surrogate minimises the heteroscedastic Gaussian negative
log-likelihood (NLL):
  L_NLL = (1/N) Σᵢ Σⱼ [(yᵢⱼ − μᵢⱼ)²/(2σᵢⱼ²) + (1/2)log σᵢⱼ²]
This training objective jointly optimises predictive accuracy (residual term) and
uncertainty calibration (variance term), without any structural constraint on
input–output relationships.

The monotone-regularized surrogate augments L_NLL with a gradient-based monotonicity
penalty derived from the physical reasoning about input–output relationships.
For each identified monotone pair (input dimension i, output dimension j) with expected
sign s_ij ∈ {+1, −1}, the penalty penalises violations of the gradient sign:
  L_mono = w_mono · (1/|P|) Σ_(i,j,s)∈P  mean_batch[ReLU(−s_ij · ∂μⱼ/∂xᵢ)]
where P is the set of constrained pairs and w_mono is an Optuna-optimised weight.

The set P is determined from training data: Spearman rank correlations between each
input and output are computed on the training set; all pairs with |ρ| ≥ 0.25 are
retained, ranked by |ρ|, and the top-40 are included in P. This data-consistent
selection process yields monotone directions that align with physical expectations

hard-coding physical formulas.

2.4 前向不确定性传播 / Forward Uncertainty Propagation
------------------------------------------------------------------------

20,000 samples are drawn from the joint prior distribution by Latin hypercube sampling.
For each sample θᵢ, the surrogate returns the predictive mean μ(θᵢ). The empirical
distribution of these means constitutes the prior-predictive uncertainty propagation.
The exceedance probability at threshold τ is estimated as:
  P_exceed(τ) = (1/N) Σᵢ 1[μ_stress(θᵢ) > τ]
with τ = 131 MPa as the primary threshold; τ = 110 and 120 MPa are reported in the appendix.
Using only the surrogate mean (rather than sampling from the full predictive distribution)

2.5 全局敏感性分析 / Global Sensitivity Analysis
------------------------------------------------------------------------

Global variance-based sensitivity indices decompose the variance of a model output into
contributions from each input. The first-order Sobol index S₁ for input i measures the
fractional reduction in output variance achievable by learning the true value of xᵢ alone:
  S₁ᵢ = Var[E(Y|Xᵢ)] / Var[Y]
The total-order index S_T measures the total effect of xᵢ including all higher-order
interactions. Together, S₁ and S_T bound the contribution of each input.

Indices are estimated using Saltelli's quasi-random sampling scheme with the Jansen estimator,
which provides the variance of each index estimate and avoids underestimation of S_T common
in older estimators. Specifically, two independent quasi-random matrices A and B (each of
size N_S × 8, N_S = 4096) are constructed, and N_S(8+2) = 40,960 model evaluations are
performed per output. This is repeated for 50 independent replications; the mean and
standard deviation of S₁ across replications provide a normal-approximation 90% confidence
interval (CI): S̄₁ ± 1.645 · SE. The surrogate mean μ(θ) serves as the function evaluator,
reducing the cost of full Sobol analysis to a fraction of a second.

局限性。代理模型在单一HPR设计点的固定数据集上训练，几何或工况改变需重新训练。MCMC标定将
4个参数（E_slope、T_ref,SS316、SS316_k_ref、k_slope,SS316）视为已知固定，因此报告的后验不确定
性压缩量为下界——放开SS316_k_ref（第三灵敏因子）还需要原位热导率测量，但将允许后验进一步
收缩至热应力相关导热区域。此外，解耦keff（第一轮迭代）缺乏稳态物理含义，不应用于临界评估。
在OOD条件下，keff代理性能对两个模型均显著下降（附录OOD测试R²≈0.29），表明当前架构的
keff外推在训练范围之外不可靠。

================================================================================
5. 结论 / Conclusion
================================================================================

This work presents a probabilistic surrogate framework for coupled multi-physics UQ in
an HPR, demonstrating four principal findings:

本文提出了热管冷却反应堆耦合多物理场不确定性量化的概率代理框架，揭示了四项主要发现：

1. Physics regularization improves generalization. The monotone-regularized HeteroMLP
(Spearman-rank monotonicity constraint) matches in-distribution accuracy
(NLL 0.305, stress R² = 0.929) while substantially improving OOD performance for
α_base extrapolation (R²: 0.774 → 0.944, ΔR² = +0.170).

（1）物理规律正则化改善分布外泛化性。单调正则化HeteroMLP（物理规律一致的单调性约束）分布内
精度与基线相当（NLL 0.305，应力R²=0.929），同时大幅提升α_base外推的分布外性能
（R²：0.774→0.944，ΔR²=+0.170）。

2. Multi-physics coupling compresses uncertainty. 47% stress σ reduction and 183× keff
compression — direct consequences of neutronics–thermal negative feedback,
not capturable by single-physics surrogates.

（2）多物理耦合压缩不确定性。应力标准差降低47%，keff不确定性压缩183倍——中子–热负反馈机制
的直接体现，单物理代理模型无法捕捉。

3. Distinct sensitivity pathways exist. E_intercept dominates stress (S₁ = 0.598);
α_base dominates keff (S₁ = 0.775). The two pathways have limited cross-interaction,
enabling partially decoupled design strategies for stress and reactivity control.

（3）存在独特的敏感性传播路径。E_intercept主控应力（S₁=0.598），α_base主控keff（S₁=0.775），
两条路径交叉作用有限，支持应力控制与反应性控制的基本分离设计策略，简化工程安全分析。

4. Posterior calibration sharpens risk assessments. MCMC updating converges the
exceedance probability to 1.0 for all ten extreme-stress scenarios (prior: 0.84–0.96),
demonstrating reliable in-situ risk stratification from a single observation vector.
【我不喜欢这个量这是不确定性分析中通用的量吗？如果不是的话就换一个。】*（P_exceed（超阈概率/超阈失效概率）是概率安全评估（PSA/PRA）和可靠性工程中的标准量，广泛用于核、航空等安全关键系统。英文名称：probability of exceedance，或 failure probability P_f。如用户偏好更通用符号，可改为P_f并定义为P_f(τ)=P(σ>τ)，在正文首次出现时定义清楚即可。本节已去掉"P_exceed"表达式，改为汉语直述，减少读者的符号负担。）

（4）后验标定精化风险评估。MCMC参数更新使全部10个极端应力场景的超阈失效概率收敛至1.0
（先验值0.84–0.96），证明仅凭单次多物理观测向量即可可靠地完成原位风险分级。

The computational speedup (> 10⁷ over the coupled solver) makes this framework practical
for iterative design exploration, sensitivity-guided tolerance allocation, and real-time
risk monitoring in reactor systems with significant material uncertainty.

超过10⁷倍的计算加速比（相对于耦合高保真求解器）使该框架适用于反应堆系统的迭代设计探索、
敏感性导向的公差分配和实时风险监控，为核能系统概率安全评价提供了一条兼具物理可信性和计算
可行性的新途径。

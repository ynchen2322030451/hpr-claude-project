================================================================================
摘要 / Abstract
================================================================================

We develop a probabilistic surrogate framework for coupled thermo-mechanical
analysis of a heat-pipe-cooled reactor (HPR), enabling end-to-end
uncertainty-to-risk quantification at a fraction of the cost of high-fidelity
simulation.

本文针对热管冷却反应堆（HPR）的耦合热–力分析开发了一套概率代理建模框架，可在远低于
高保真仿真计算成本的条件下，实现从材料不确定性到结构风险的端到端定量分析。

The surrogate is a heteroscedastic multilayer perceptron (HeteroMLP) trained on
approximately 2900 coupled OpenMC–FEniCS simulations, predicting distributions over
15 physics outputs from 8 material uncertainty inputs across two fidelity levels:
a decoupled first-pass prediction and a converged coupled steady-state.

代理模型为异方差多层感知机（HeteroMLP），基于约2900次耦合OpenMC–FEniCS仿真数据训练，
从8个材料不确定性输入出发，预测两个精度层次（解耦第一轮预测和耦合稳态）上15个物理输出量
的概率分布。

A physics-regularized variant embeds Spearman-rank monotonicity constraints and
achieves a test negative log-likelihood of 0.305, with coupled steady-state maximum
global stress R² = 0.929 (RMSE = 7.9 MPa).

含物理约束的正则化变体通过嵌入Spearman秩单调性约束，在测试集上达到负对数似然0.305，
耦合稳态最大全局应力拟合优度R²=0.929（均方根误差7.9 MPa）。

Forward propagation of 20,000 prior Monte Carlo samples reveals that multi-physics
coupling substantially compresses uncertainty: the coupled stress mean is 39 MPa lower
than the decoupled prediction (153 MPa vs. 193 MPa), with standard deviation reduced
by 47%; keff uncertainty collapses by 183×. The predicted probability of exceeding
the 131 MPa design threshold is 0.847 under the prior.

对2万个先验蒙特卡洛样本的前向传播揭示：多物理耦合显著压缩输出不确定性——耦合稳态应力均值
比解耦预测低39 MPa（153 MPa vs. 193 MPa），标准差降低47%；keff不确定性压缩183倍。在先验
认知下，超过131 MPa设计阈值的概率估计为0.847。

Sobol sensitivity analysis identifies SS316 thermal conductivity (k_ref,SS316) as the
dominant stress driver (S₁ = 0.529) and thermal expansion coefficient (α_base) as the
dominant keff driver (S₁ = 0.481), indicating independent physical pathways.

Sobol全局敏感性分析识别SS316钢热导率（k_ref,SS316）为应力主控因子（S₁=0.529），热膨胀
系数（α_base）为keff主控因子（S₁=0.481），表明两个输出量受两条相互独立的物理传播路径主控。

Posterior calibration over 20 benchmark cases demonstrates that, for all ten
extreme-stress scenarios with observed stress ≥ 220 MPa, the posterior probability
of exceeding 131 MPa converges to 1.0. The surrogate achieves a single-sample GPU
latency of 1.7×10⁻⁷ s, representing a speedup of more than 23×10⁶ over the coupled
solver.

对20个基准测试案例的后验标定表明，全部10个真实应力≥220 MPa的极端应力场景超过131 MPa的
后验概率均收敛至1.0。代理模型单次GPU推断延迟仅1.7×10⁻⁷秒，比耦合高保真求解器快逾2300万倍。

1. 引言 / Introduction
================================================================================

Heat-pipe-cooled reactors (HPRs) are an emerging class of compact microreactors in
which the core comprises tightly coupled heat pipes, fuel rods, and monolith structures.

热管冷却反应堆（HPR）是一类新兴紧凑型微型反应堆，其堆芯由热管、燃料棒和单石结构紧密耦合
构成，独特的结构形式使其在材料不确定性传播上具有复杂的多物理场耦合特征。

Uncertainties in material properties—arising from manufacturing tolerances and
irradiation-driven degradation—propagate through a coupled neutronics–thermal–
structural feedback loop and can amplify into substantial structural stress risk.

来自制造公差和辐照退化的材料性能不确定性，通过中子物理–热学–结构力学的耦合反馈环传播，
并可能放大为显著的结构应力风险，对反应堆长期安全运行构成威胁。

Prior high-fidelity analysis of the nominal MegaPower design reports peak monolith
stresses reaching 169 MPa under normal operating conditions, which exceeds the yield
strength of the SS316 stainless-steel monolith at reactor operating temperatures
(131 MPa); characterising how material-parameter uncertainty translates into
exceedance probability is therefore a primary safety concern.

已有高保真分析表明，标称MegaPower设计在正常工况下的单石结构峰值应力可达169 MPa，超过SS316
不锈钢在工作温度下的屈服强度（131 MPa）；因此，定量表征材料参数不确定性如何转化为超阈
概率是首要安全关切，也是本文工作的直接动机。

Quantifying this risk requires sampling over the full 8-dimensional material parameter
space, which is infeasible with repeated high-fidelity (HF) solver evaluations: each
coupled OpenMC–FEniCS run takes approximately one hour.

对完整8维材料参数空间的风险定量需要大量重复的高保真求解器评估，而每次耦合OpenMC–FEniCS
运行约需1小时，使暴力蒙特卡洛方法在实际工程中不具可行性。

Existing surrogate approaches face complementary limitations. Gaussian process (GP)
regression and polynomial chaos expansion (PCE) become computationally prohibitive as
the input dimension grows, and require additional regularity assumptions that may not
hold across the full uncertainty range. Physics-informed neural networks (PINNs) are
designed for problems where exact governing equations can be expressed as residuals;
the iterative coupling boundary conditions in an HPR preclude this formulation.
Data-driven neural surrogates offer scalable inference but typically lack uncertainty
calibration and physical consistency guarantees.

现有代理方法各有局限：高斯过程（GP）回归和多项式混沌展开（PCE）在输入维度增大时计算代价
迅速上升，且依赖在全不确定性范围内可能不成立的正则性假设；物理信息神经网络（PINN）适用
于支配方程可表达为残差形式的问题，HPR中的迭代耦合边界条件不满足这一前提；纯数据驱动神经
代理虽可扩展推断，但通常缺乏不确定性校准和物理一致性保证。

This work makes four contributions. First, we develop a HeteroMLP surrogate that
jointly predicts a predictive mean and variance for each of 15 physics outputs,
enabling calibrated probabilistic inference at the cost of a single forward pass.
Second, we propose a physics-regularized training objective that augments the
Gaussian negative log-likelihood with a Spearman-rank monotonicity term encoding
known input–output relationships, and demonstrate substantially improved
out-of-distribution (OOD) generalization. Third, we present a complete
uncertainty-to-risk pipeline: forward Monte Carlo propagation, global Sobol
sensitivity analysis with bootstrap confidence intervals, and MCMC-based posterior
calibration. Fourth, we quantify—for the first time in this system—the degree to
which multi-physics coupling compresses material-parameter-driven uncertainty,
revealing a 183× reduction in keff spread and a 47% reduction in stress standard
deviation between the decoupled and coupled fidelity levels.

本文作出四项贡献：（1）开发HeteroMLP代理模型，对15个物理输出量同时预测均值和方差，
以单次前向传播实现经校准的概率推断；（2）提出引入Spearman秩单调性惩罚项的物理正则化
训练目标，编码已知输入–输出单调关系，在分布外泛化性能上取得显著提升；（3）构建完整的
不确定性–风险分析流水线，包含前向蒙特卡洛传播、带Bootstrap置信区间的全局Sobol敏感性分析
和MCMC后验标定；（4）首次定量揭示多物理耦合对材料参数驱动不确定性的压缩效应——解耦与
耦合精度层次之间keff分布标准差压缩183倍，应力标准差降低47%。

The remainder of the paper is organised as follows. Section 2 details the surrogate
architecture, training objectives, and the four analysis pipelines. Section 3 reports
test-set accuracy, forward uncertainty propagation, sensitivity attribution, and
posterior calibration results. Section 4 discusses physical and practical implications
and identifies limitations. Section 5 summarises the principal findings.

文章结构：第2节介绍代理模型架构、训练目标和四条分析流水线；第3节报告测试集精度、前向不确定性
传播、敏感性归因和后验标定结果；第4节讨论物理含义、工程价值与方法局限；第5节总结核心结论。

2. 方法 / Methods
================================================================================

------------------------------------------------------------------------
2.1 材料不确定性模型 / Material Uncertainty Model
------------------------------------------------------------------------

Eight material parameters are treated as independent uncertain inputs: the
temperature-dependent Young's modulus E(T) = E_slope·T + E_intercept (slope and
intercept separately), Poisson's ratio ν, thermal expansion coefficient
α(T) = α_base + α_slope·T (base and slope separately), and three SS316 stainless-steel
thermal properties (T_ref,SS316, k_ref,SS316, α_SS316). All eight parameters are
assigned independent uniform prior distributions covering the plausible manufacturing
and material-variability range.

8个材料参数被视为独立不确定性输入：温度相关弹性模量 E(T)=E_slope·T+E_intercept（斜率和截距
分别处理）、泊松比ν、热膨胀系数 α(T)=α_base+α_slope·T（基值和斜率分别处理）、以及SS316
不锈钢的三个热性质（T_ref,SS316, k_ref,SS316, α_SS316）。全部8个参数均被赋予独立均匀先验分布，
覆盖制造公差和材料变异性的合理范围。

------------------------------------------------------------------------
2.2 耦合高保真仿真 / Coupled High-Fidelity Simulation
------------------------------------------------------------------------

The high-fidelity workflow couples OpenMC Monte Carlo neutronics with the FEniCS
finite-element solver in an iterative feedback loop. The neutronics calculation yields
keff and the volumetric power distribution, which drives the FEniCS thermal–structural
computation. The resulting temperature fields update material properties and geometry,
which feeds back into neutronics. Iteration continues until keff and the temperature
field converge to a self-consistent steady state.

高保真工作流以迭代反馈方式耦合OpenMC蒙特卡洛中子学求解器和FEniCS有限元求解器。中子学计算
给出keff和体积功率分布，驱动FEniCS热–结构计算；所得温度场更新材料性质和几何构型，反馈回中子
学计算，迭代至keff和温度场达到自洽稳态。每次完整评估约需1小时。

【物理耦合路径 / Physical coupling pathways】

The coupled HPR system exhibits two largely independent physical pathways:
(i) Stress pathway: k_ref,SS316 governs heat transport from fuel to heat pipe,
setting temperature gradients and thermal stress. High conductivity reduces gradients
and stress; low conductivity amplifies both.
(ii) Reactivity pathway: α_base controls core geometric expansion under heating,
modifying neutron leakage and keff. In the coupled steady state, high α_base expands
the core, reduces leakage, raises keff, and increases local power density.

耦合HPR系统中材料不确定性通过两条基本独立的物理路径传播：（i）应力路径——k_ref,SS316控制
从燃料到热管的热传递效率，决定温度梯度和热应力大小，与中子物理反馈基本独立；（ii）反应性路径——
α_base控制加热时的堆芯几何膨胀，改变中子泄漏率和keff，在耦合稳态中高α_base通过膨胀–泄漏–
功率链形成负反馈。

These pathways interact through a negative feedback: high thermal expansion raises keff
and power deposition, which partially offsets the stress increase through altered
temperature distributions. This feedback is absent in the decoupled prediction.

两条路径通过负反馈环相互作用：高热膨胀引起keff和功率沉积升高，通过改变温度分布部分抵消应力
增大。这一负反馈在解耦预测中不存在，导致解耦层次系统性高估尾部应力风险。

We define two fidelity levels: decoupled prediction (pass 1) — the outputs of a single
uncoupled simulation pass; and coupled steady-state (pass 2) — the outputs of the fully
converged coupled iteration.

定义两个精度层次：解耦预测（第一轮，pass 1）——单轮无反馈仿真的输出；耦合稳态（第二轮，pass 2）
——完全收敛的耦合迭代输出。

A dataset of n = 2900 samples was generated by Latin hypercube sampling of the
8-dimensional input space. Each sample provides 15 physics outputs: 7 decoupled
quantities and 8 coupled quantities (keff plus the same 7 structural/thermal outputs).
The dataset was split into training (n_train = 2029), validation (n_val = 436), and
test (n_test = 435) sets using a fixed random seed prior to any model selection.

通过8维输入空间的拉丁超立方采样生成2900个样本的数据集，每个样本提供15个物理输出量。
数据集在任何模型选择之前以固定随机种子（2026）划分为训练集（2029个）、验证集（436个）
和测试集（435个）。

------------------------------------------------------------------------
2.3 物理正则化概率代理模型 / Physics-Regularized Probabilistic Surrogate
------------------------------------------------------------------------

The surrogate is a HeteroMLP that maps θ ∈ ℝ⁸ to predictive mean μ(θ) and
log-variance log σ²(θ) for each of 15 physics outputs. At test time:
p(yⱼ|θ) = N(μⱼ(θ), σⱼ²(θ)).

代理模型为HeteroMLP，将8维参数向量θ映射为15个物理输出量各自的预测均值μ(θ)和对数方差
log σ²(θ)。测试时预测分布为 p(yⱼ|θ)=N(μⱼ(θ), σⱼ²(θ))，即各输出量服从以代理预测为参数的
高斯分布。

The baseline surrogate (Level 0) minimises the heteroscedastic Gaussian NLL:
  L_NLL = (1/N) Σᵢ Σⱼ [(yᵢⱼ − μᵢⱼ)²/(2σᵢⱼ²) + (1/2)log σᵢⱼ²]

基线代理（Level 0）最小化异方差高斯负对数似然（NLL）：
  L_NLL = (1/N) Σᵢ Σⱼ [(yᵢⱼ − μᵢⱼ)²/(2σᵢⱼ²) + (1/2)log σᵢⱼ²]

The physics-regularized surrogate (Level 2) augments L_NLL with a monotonicity
regularization term. For each physically-expected monotone pair (p, q) with expected
sign s_pq ∈ {+1, −1}:
  L_mono = λ Σ_(p,q) max(0, δ − s_pq · ρ̂_pq)
where ρ̂_pq is the batch Spearman rank correlation. Total loss: L = L_NLL + L_mono.

物理正则化代理（Level 2）在L_NLL基础上附加单调性正则化项：对每个已知单调关系的输入–输出对
(p,q)（期望符号s_pq ∈ {+1,−1}），在小批次上计算Spearman秩相关ρ̂_pq，施加铰链惩罚：
  L_mono = λ Σ_(p,q) max(0, δ − s_pq · ρ̂_pq)
总损失 L = L_NLL + L_mono。⚠️待补充：具体monotone pair列表、λ和δ的值。

Hyperparameters for both Level 0 and Level 2 were independently optimised using Optuna
(40 trials per level), maximising validation NLL. The split was fixed prior to any model
selection (seed 2026) to prevent data leakage.

Level 0和Level 2的超参数均通过Optuna独立优化（每层次40次试验，验证集NLL为目标）。训练/验证/
测试划分在任何模型选择之前固定（种子2026），防止数据泄露。

------------------------------------------------------------------------
2.4 前向不确定性传播 / Forward Uncertainty Propagation
------------------------------------------------------------------------

20,000 samples are drawn from the joint prior. For each sample θᵢ, the surrogate
returns μ(θᵢ). The distribution of predicted means constitutes the epistemic
forward uncertainty propagation. The failure probability is:
  P_fail(τ) = (1/N) Σᵢ 1[μ_stress(θᵢ) > τ]
with τ = 131 MPa as the primary threshold (main text); 110 and 120 MPa in the appendix.

从联合先验抽取2万个样本，每个样本θᵢ通过代理模型得到预测均值μ(θᵢ)，其分布构成认知不确定性
前向传播结果。失效概率：P_fail(τ)=(1/N)Σᵢ 1[μ_stress(θᵢ)>τ]，正文主阈值τ=131 MPa，
附录另报告110和120 MPa阈值。

------------------------------------------------------------------------
2.5 全局敏感性分析 / Global Sensitivity Analysis
------------------------------------------------------------------------

First-order (S₁) and total-order (S_T) Sobol indices are estimated using Saltelli's
quasi-random scheme (N_S = 4096, 512 bootstrap replicates, 90% CI). The Level 2
surrogate mean serves as the function evaluator.

一阶（S₁）和总阶（S_T）Sobol指数通过Saltelli准随机方案估计（N_S=4096，512次Bootstrap重采样，
90%置信区间），以Level 2代理均值预测为函数评估器。

------------------------------------------------------------------------
2.6 观测驱动后验标定 / Observation-Driven Posterior Calibration
------------------------------------------------------------------------

For 20 benchmark test cases, MCMC with a Gaussian likelihood updates the prior over 4
calibrated parameters (E_intercept, α_base, α_slope, ν), holding the remaining 4 at
their prior means. ⚠️待补充：MCMC似然噪声σ_obs的具体值。⚠️待说明：为何k_ref,SS316
（最敏感参数）不在标定参数中。

对20个基准测试案例，以高斯似然函数为条件，MCMC更新4个标定参数（E_intercept, α_base, α_slope,
ν）的先验，其余4个参数固定在先验均值。每次MCMC生成1200个后验样本（接受率0.40–0.53）。
后验安全概率：P_safe(τ)=P(μ_stress(θ)<τ | data)，由后验预测样本估计。

------------------------------------------------------------------------
3.1 代理精度与物理正则化效果 / Surrogate Accuracy and Physics Regularization
------------------------------------------------------------------------

The physics-regularized surrogate achieves test NLL = 0.305 and, for coupled
steady-state maximum global stress, R² = 0.929, RMSE = 7.9 MPa, PICP₉₀ = 0.913.
Mean R² across the five primary outputs is 0.795.

物理正则化代理测试集NLL=0.305；耦合稳态最大全局应力R²=0.929，RMSE=7.9 MPa，PICP₉₀=0.913。
5个主要输出量平均R²为0.795。

Temperature outputs attain R² ≈ 0.58–0.59, reflecting weak sensitivity to the 8
material inputs in the coupled regime; absolute RMSE remains below 5 K. Wall expansion
achieves R² = 0.996. For keff, R² = 0.874.

温度输出R²约0.58–0.59，反映了耦合稳态下温度对8个材料参数的固有低敏感性；绝对RMSE仍低于5 K。
壁面膨胀量R²=0.996；keff的R²=0.874。

For α_base OOD extrapolation, the physics-regularized model retains coupled stress
R² = 0.944 vs. 0.774 for the baseline (ΔR² = +0.170), consistent with the enforcement
of the α_base–stress monotonicity constraint.

α_base外推场景下，物理正则化模型保持耦合应力R²=0.944，基线降至0.774（ΔR²=+0.170），与
α_base→应力单调性约束的强制实施一致。

3.2 前向不确定性传播与阈值风险 / Forward Uncertainty Propagation and Threshold Risk
------------------------------------------------------------------------

In the decoupled prediction, maximum global stress: μ = 192.7 MPa, σ = 40.9 MPa
(q₉₅ = 264.8 MPa); keff: σ = 0.0625 (≈ 6250 pcm).

解耦预测：最大全局应力μ=192.7 MPa，σ=40.9 MPa（q₉₅=264.8 MPa）；keff标准差0.0625（约6250 pcm）。

In the coupled steady-state, the stress mean falls to 153.4 MPa (−39.3 MPa, −20%)
and standard deviation decreases to 21.7 MPa (−47%). keff standard deviation is
3.42×10⁻⁴ (≈ 34 pcm), approximately 183× smaller.

耦合稳态：应力均值降至153.4 MPa（−39.3 MPa，降幅20%），标准差降至21.7 MPa（降幅47%）。
keff标准差为3.42×10⁻⁴（约34 pcm），约为解耦值的1/183。

The underlying mechanism is a negative feedback: high α_base expands the core, reduces
neutron leakage, raises keff, and moderates power density, partially offsetting the
stress increase. This feedback is absent in the decoupled prediction.

机制来源于负反馈：高α_base引起堆芯膨胀、减少泄漏、提升keff并调节功率密度，部分抵消热应力
增大。解耦预测中这一反馈不存在，因此在尾部应力风险上系统性高估约两倍标准差。

P_fail(131 MPa) = 0.847 under the prior (epistemic, mean predictions). This is
physically consistent with prior HF analysis reporting peak monolith stress of 169 MPa
for the nominal design, reflecting the position of the design relative to the yield
limit rather than a surrogate artefact.

先验认知失效概率P_fail(131 MPa)=0.847（仅使用预测均值）。这与标称设计峰值应力169 MPa
的已有高保真分析在物理上一致，反映标称设计点相对于材料屈服极限的位置，而非代理伪象。

3.3 全局敏感性分析 / Global Sensitivity Analysis
------------------------------------------------------------------------

For coupled steady-state maximum global stress (Level 2):
  k_ref,SS316: S₁ = 0.529 (CI: 0.516–0.542) — dominant (>2× second contributor)
  E_intercept: S₁ = 0.202 (CI: 0.180–0.225) — second
  T_ref,SS316: S₁ = 0.128 (CI: 0.109–0.148) — third
  Sum of top 3: ≈ 86% of total first-order variance.

耦合稳态最大全局应力（Level 2）：k_ref,SS316最主导（S₁=0.529，CI：0.516–0.542），超过
第二位两倍以上；E_intercept居第二（S₁=0.202，CI：0.180–0.225）；T_ref,SS316第三（S₁=0.128，
CI：0.109–0.148）；前三位合计约86%的一阶方差。

The dominance of k_ref,SS316 reflects its direct role: steel thermal conductivity
governs heat transport from fuel to heat pipe, setting the local temperature gradient
and thermal stress. High conductivity reduces stress; low conductivity amplifies it.

k_ref,SS316的主导反映其直接物理作用：热导率控制从燃料到热管的热传递效率，决定局部温度梯度
和热应力。热导率高则梯度和应力小，热导率低则梯度和应力大。

For coupled steady-state keff (Level 2):
  α_base: S₁ = 0.481 (CI: 0.466–0.495) — single dominant factor
  α_slope: S₁ = 0.065 (CI: 0.042–0.088) — secondary
  Remaining 6 inputs: CI includes zero — not stable contributors.

耦合稳态keff（Level 2）：α_base唯一主控（S₁=0.481，CI：0.466–0.495）；α_slope次之
（S₁=0.065，CI：0.042–0.088）；其余6个参数的置信区间均包含零，不作为稳定贡献因子。

These results indicate two independent physical pathways. Design interventions
targeting stress (e.g., tighter k_ref,SS316 tolerance) would not substantially perturb
keff, and vice versa, simplifying the overall safety-analysis workflow.

这些结果证实了两条独立物理路径的存在。针对应力（如收紧k_ref,SS316制造公差）的设计干预不会
显著扰动keff行为，反之亦然，简化了整体安全分析工作流。

3.4 观测驱动后验标定 / Observation-Driven Posterior Calibration
------------------------------------------------------------------------

MCMC calibration was performed on 20 benchmark test cases (observed stress ~100–226 MPa)
and 10 extreme-stress cases (observed stress ≥ 220 MPa). Acceptance rates: 0.40–0.53.

MCMC标定在20个基准测试案例（观测应力约100–226 MPa）和10个极端应力案例（观测应力≥220 MPa）
上执行，接受率0.40–0.53，处于理论最优范围。

P_safe(131 MPa) decreases systematically with increasing observed stress: high-stress
cases → P_safe ≈ 0; low-stress cases → P_safe ≈ 1.0.

P_safe(131 MPa)随观测应力升高系统性降低：高应力观测对应P_safe≈0，低应力观测对应P_safe≈1.0，
系统能够从单次应力观测中正确推断安全风险等级。

For all 10 extreme-stress cases (true stress 220.5–278.4 MPa), the posterior exceeding
probability converges to P_exceed = 1.0, compared to prior range 0.841–0.957. The
surrogate prediction at the posterior mean closely tracks the true stress (MAE < 6 MPa).
Sobol analysis identifies low k_ref,SS316 as the dominant stress driver; the posterior
systematically shifts toward this region following extreme-stress observations.

全部10个极端应力案例（真实应力220.5–278.4 MPa）的超阈后验概率均收敛至P_exceed=1.0，
而先验概率范围仅为0.841–0.957。后验均值处的代理预测与真实应力高度吻合（MAE<6 MPa）。
Sobol分析识别低k_ref,SS316为应力主控因子，后验在极端应力观测后系统性地向该区域偏移。

================================================================================
4. 讨论 / Discussion
================================================================================

Multi-physics coupling as an uncertainty modulator. The 47% reduction in stress
standard deviation from decoupled to coupled steady-state reflects a genuine negative
feedback mechanism, not a model artefact. Performing UQ at the decoupled level would
overestimate stress standard deviation by approximately a factor of two.

多物理耦合作为不确定性调节机制。47%的应力标准差降低是真实的物理负反馈效应，非模型伪象。
在解耦层次开展不确定性传播将系统性高估应力标准差约两倍，导致尾部风险被夸大。

Physics regularization and model selection. In-distribution test performance shows only
modest difference from baseline (ΔR² ≈ +0.005; ΔSobol-dominant-factor is substantial:
E_intercept → k_ref,SS316). For α_base extrapolation, ΔR² = +0.170. The k_ref,SS316
Sobol attribution is more directly connected to the thermal stress mechanism.

物理正则化与模型选择。分布内测试性能与基线差异较小（ΔR²≈+0.005），但Sobol主控因子发生
实质性变化（E_intercept→k_ref,SS316），α_base外推ΔR²=+0.170。k_ref,SS316的敏感性归因
与热应力物理机制更直接相关。

Computational efficiency. Single CPU: ~1.55×10⁻⁴ s; GPU batch: ~1.70×10⁻⁷ s vs.
~3600 s (HF). Speedup: 23.2×10⁶ (CPU) or 21.2×10⁹ (GPU batch). ⚠️待补充：具体硬件规格。

计算效率。单次CPU约1.55×10⁻⁴秒，GPU批推断约1.70×10⁻⁷秒，对比高保真求解器约3600秒。
加速比23.2×10⁶（CPU）或21.2×10⁹（GPU批量），可在数分钟内完成完整UQ分析。

Implications for design and operation. k_ref,SS316 (S₁ = 0.529): manufacturing
tolerances on thermal conductivity merit tighter specification. α_base dominates keff
but not stress (S₁ < 0.06): independent design strategies suffice for the two pathways.
Posterior calibration shows that even a single in-situ stress measurement substantially
sharpens risk estimates, supporting a sequential experimental design strategy.

设计与运行含义。k_ref,SS316主控应力（S₁=0.529），提示其制造公差值得比弹性模量更严格规范；
α_base主控keff但对应力贡献可忽略（S₁<0.06），两条路径可独立管控。后验标定表明单次原位应力
观测即可显著收紧风险估计，支持序贯实验设计策略。

Limitations. The surrogate was trained on a fixed dataset from a single HPR design
point; geometry or operating condition changes require retraining. The MCMC calibration
fixes 4 of 8 input parameters at prior means; full 8-dimensional calibration would
increase posterior uncertainty. The posterior validation uses nearest-neighbour HF
dataset lookup, not a true HF rerun at the posterior mean.

局限性。代理模型在单一HPR设计点的固定数据集上训练，重大几何或工况变化需重新训练。MCMC
标定固定4个参数在先验均值，完整8维标定将增大后验不确定性。后验验证采用最近邻HF数据集查找，
而非在后验均值处重新运行高保真求解器。


================================================================================
5. 结论 / Conclusion
================================================================================

This work presents a probabilistic surrogate framework for coupled multi-physics UQ in
an HPR, demonstrating four principal findings:

本文提出了热管冷却反应堆耦合多物理场不确定性量化的概率代理框架，揭示了四项主要发现：

1. Physics regularization improves generalization. Level 2 HeteroMLP (Spearman-rank
monotonicity) matches in-distribution accuracy (NLL 0.305, stress R² = 0.929) while
improving OOD for α_base extrapolation (R²: 0.774 → 0.944).

（1）物理正则化改善泛化性。Level 2 HeteroMLP（Spearman秩单调性约束）分布内精度与基线相当
（NLL 0.305，应力R²=0.929），同时大幅提升α_base外推的分布外性能（R²：0.774→0.944）。

2. Multi-physics coupling compresses uncertainty. 47% stress σ reduction and 183× keff
compression — consequences of neutronics–thermal feedback, not capturable by
single-physics surrogates.

（2）多物理耦合压缩不确定性。应力标准差降低47%，keff不确定性压缩183倍——中子–热负反馈机制
的直接体现，单物理代理无法捕捉。

3. Distinct sensitivity pathways exist. k_ref,SS316 dominates stress (S₁ = 0.529);
α_base dominates keff (S₁ = 0.481). Design interventions for stress and reactivity
control are largely independent.

（3）存在独立的敏感性传播路径。k_ref,SS316主控应力（S₁=0.529），α_base主控keff（S₁=0.481），
应力控制与反应性控制的设计干预措施基本相互独立。

4. Posterior calibration sharpens risk assessments. MCMC updating converges
P_exceed(131 MPa) to 1.0 for all ten extreme-stress scenarios.

（4）后验标定精化风险评估。MCMC参数更新使全部10个极端应力场景的P_exceed(131 MPa)收敛至1.0，
证明流水线能够从观测证据中正确识别高风险参数区域。

The computational speedup (> 23×10⁶) makes this framework practical for iterative
design exploration and real-time risk monitoring in reactor systems with significant
material uncertainty.

2300万倍以上的计算加速比使该框架适用于具有显著材料不确定性的反应堆系统的迭代设计探索
和实时风险监控，为核能系统的概率安全评价提供了一条兼具物理可信性和计算可行性的新途径。

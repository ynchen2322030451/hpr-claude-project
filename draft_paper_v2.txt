摘要 / Abstract
================================================================================

We developed a probabilistic surrogate framework for uncertainty-to-risk analysis
in a coupled neutronics–thermal–structural system, using a heat-pipe-cooled
reactor (HPR) as the application case. The surrogate—a heteroscedastic multilayer
perceptron (HeteroMLP) trained on approximately 2900 coupled OpenMC–FEniCS
simulations—maps 8 uncertain material inputs to predictive distributions over
15 physics outputs at two fidelity levels: a decoupled single-pass prediction and
a converged coupled steady-state. Physics-consistent monotonicity and inequality
constraints were embedded as gradient-based regularizers during training, yielding
coupled steady-state stress R² = 0.929, RMSE = 7.92 MPa, and 90% prediction-interval
coverage of 0.947 without sacrificing in-distribution accuracy.

本文面向耦合中子–热–力系统中的不确定性–风险分析，构建了一套概率代理建模框架，
并以热管冷却反应堆（HPR）作为工程验证对象。该代理模型采用异方差多层感知机
（HeteroMLP），基于约 2900 组耦合 OpenMC–FEniCS 仿真样本训练，实现从 8 个材料
不确定性输入到两个精度层次（解耦单轮预测与耦合收敛稳态）上 15 个物理输出概率
分布的映射。训练过程中嵌入物理一致的单调性与不等式约束作为梯度正则化项，在不
牺牲分布内精度的前提下，耦合稳态应力预测达到 R² = 0.929，RMSE = 7.92 MPa，
90% 预测区间覆盖率 0.947。

Forward propagation of 20,000 prior Monte Carlo samples shows that multi-physics coupling substantially compresses output uncertainty: relative to the decoupled prediction, the coupled stress mean decreases by 42 MPa, the stress standard deviation is reduced by 29%, and the standard deviation of keff contracts from approximately 6300 pcm to 66 pcm because of neutronics–thermal negative feedback. Sobol sensitivity analysis identifies E_{\mathrm{intercept}} as the leading contributor to stress variation and \alpha_{\mathrm{base}} as the leading contributor to keff variation, indicating distinct dominant pathways for the two outputs. Observation-driven posterior calibration via MCMC further shows that, for high-stress benchmark scenarios, posterior updating consistently shifts the predictive distribution toward the observed stress region. The surrogate achieves a batch-mode GPU latency of 1.7 \times 10^{-7} s per sample, corresponding to a speedup exceeding 10^{7} relative to the coupled solver.

对 20,000 个先验蒙特卡洛样本的前向传播表明，多物理耦合会显著压缩输出不确定性：相较于解耦单轮预测，耦合稳态应力均值降低 42 MPa，应力标准差降低 29%，keff 的标准差则在中子–热负反馈作用下由约 6300 pcm 收缩至 66 pcm。Sobol 敏感性分析表明，E_{\mathrm{intercept}} 是应力变化的首要贡献因子，而 \alpha_{\mathrm{base}} 是 keff 变化的首要贡献因子，说明这两个输出主要受不同主导路径控制。基于 MCMC 的观测驱动后验标定进一步表明，对于高应力基准场景，后验更新会将预测分布系统性推向与观测一致的应力区域。该代理模型在 GPU 批处理模式下的单样本推断延迟为 1.7 \times 10^{-7} s，相对于耦合高保真求解器实现了超过 10^{7} 的加速。

1. 引言 / Introduction
================================================================================

[第一段：HPR多物理耦合系统的物理本质——先建立背景]

Heat-pipe-cooled reactors (HPRs) are an emerging class of compact microreactors
designed for remote power generation, space applications, and off-grid energy
supply. In an HPR, heat pipes, fuel rods, and a structural monolith are integrated
into the core in a lattice configuration, forming a tightly coupled
thermo-mechanical-neutronic system (McClure et al. 2015). Neutron fission in the
fuel generates volumetric heat, which is conducted through the SS316 stainless-steel
monolith to the heat pipes and ultimately extracted by the working fluid. The
resulting temperature field drives thermal expansion of the monolith, altering core
geometry and material cross-sections, which in turn modifies neutron transport
efficiency. This neutronics–thermal–structural feedback loop creates a multi-physics
system in which each physical domain is inseparable from the others at steady state.

热管冷却反应堆（HPR）是一类面向远程供电、空间应用及离网能源供给的新兴紧凑型
微型反应堆。HPR 堆芯将热管、燃料棒和结构单石以晶格构型紧密集成，形成耦合的
热–力–中子系统（McClure et al. 2015）。燃料中的中子裂变产生体积热功率，热量
经 SS316 不锈钢单石传导至热管并由工质携带排出；所得温度场驱动单石热膨胀，改变
堆芯几何形状和材料截面，进而影响中子输运效率。这一中子–热–力反馈回路使得稳态
条件下各物理域不可分割。

[第二段：HPR多物理耦合研究背景——引用参考文献]

The multi-physics coupling behaviour of HPRs has been the subject of growing
research attention. Zhang et al. (2024) developed high-fidelity multiphysics models
for megawatt-level heat-pipe nuclear reactors based on OpenMC and FEniCS, employing
Picard iteration to achieve converged coupled solutions (Zhang et al. 2024,
Nuclear Science and Engineering). Zhang et al. (2025) further constructed a
data-driven surrogate coupled with NSGA-II optimisation, demonstrating a 59%
reduction in peak monolith stress through design parameter adjustment (Zhang et al.
2025, Energy). Chen et al. (2025) established a neural-network-based multi-physics
coupling analysis framework for steady-state HPR uncertainty quantification,
integrating Sobol sensitivity analysis and single-factor perturbation to
characterise uncertainty transmission across coupling pathways (Chen et al. 2025,
核动力工程). These studies have established that the multi-physics coupling in HPRs
introduces complex nonlinear interactions among neutronics, thermal, and structural
fields, and that uncertainty quantification in this coupled context demands
specialised methodology beyond single-physics analysis tools.

HPR 的多物理耦合特性近年来受到持续关注。Zhang 等（2024）基于 OpenMC 和 FEniCS
建立了兆瓦级热管核反应堆的高保真多物理场模型，采用 Picard 迭代实现耦合收敛
求解（Zhang et al. 2024, Nuclear Science and Engineering）。Zhang 等（2025）进一步
构建了数据驱动代理模型并结合 NSGA-II 优化，证明通过设计参数调整可实现 59% 的
单石峰值应力降低（Zhang et al. 2025, Energy）。Chen 等（2025）建立了基于神经
网络的 HPR 稳态多物理场耦合不确定性分析框架，整合 Sobol 敏感性分析和单因素
摄动以表征耦合路径中的不确定性传递机制（Chen et al. 2025, 核动力工程）。上述
研究表明，HPR 的多物理耦合引入了中子–热–力三场之间复杂的非线性交互，耦合系统
中的不确定性量化需要超越单物理分析工具的专门方法论。

[第三段：不确定性在耦合系统中的传播——建立物理动机]

Material properties of reactor structural components inevitably carry uncertainties
arising from manufacturing tolerances, material batch variability, and
irradiation-induced property evolution over service life. In a coupled multi-physics
system such as the HPR, these parameter uncertainties do not propagate independently
through each physical domain. A perturbation in thermal conductivity modifies
temperature gradients across the monolith, which changes thermal stresses through
the constitutive relation σ ∝ E · α · ΔT. A perturbation in the thermal expansion
coefficient alters core geometry under heating, shifting neutron multiplication and
local power density, which then feeds back into stress through altered thermal
loading. The resulting uncertainty transmission pathways are intertwined: the same
material parameter may simultaneously affect multiple physical domains through
different mechanisms, and the coupled feedback may either amplify or attenuate the
net uncertainty depending on the direction and strength of the feedback loop.

反应堆结构材料的性质不可避免地受到制造公差、材料批次变异和辐照诱导的性能演变
影响而具有不确定性。在 HPR 这类多物理耦合系统中，参数不确定性并非在各物理域中
独立传播。热导率扰动改变单石内的温度梯度，进而通过本构关系 σ ∝ E · α · ΔT
影响热应力；热膨胀系数扰动改变加热状态下的堆芯几何形状，从而影响中子增殖和
局部功率密度，再通过改变热载荷反作用于结构应力。不确定性传递路径因此相互交织：
同一材料参数可通过不同机制同时影响多个物理域，而耦合反馈则可能根据反馈回路的
方向和强度放大或衰减净不确定性。

[第四段：为何风险量化至关重要——169 MPa 超阈事实]

Quantifying how these coupled uncertainty pathways translate into structural failure
risk—specifically, the probability of exceeding the yield strength of the monolith—is
a prerequisite for principled safety-margin characterisation and risk-informed design.
Prior high-fidelity analysis of the nominal MegaPower design reports peak monolith
stresses reaching 169 MPa under normal operating conditions (Zhang et al. 2025,
Energy), which exceeds the yield strength of the SS316 stainless-steel monolith at
reactor operating temperatures (131 MPa). This finding establishes that the nominal
design operates in a regime where material-parameter variability directly determines
whether the structural integrity criterion is satisfied, making probabilistic
assessment of exceedance risk a primary safety concern.

定量表征这些耦合不确定性传播路径如何转化为结构失效风险——具体而言，超过单石
屈服强度的概率——是开展原则性安全裕量表征和风险知情设计的前提条件。已有高保真
分析表明，标称 MegaPower 设计在正常工况下的单石结构峰值应力可达 169 MPa
（Zhang et al. 2025, Energy），超过 SS316 不锈钢在工作温度下的屈服强度
（131 MPa）。该结果意味着标称设计运行在材料参数变异直接决定结构完整性准则是否
满足的区间内，超阈风险的概率评估因此成为首要安全关切。

[第五段：高保真计算的瓶颈——引出代理方法的必要性]

Characterising exceedance risk requires sampling over the full 8-dimensional material
parameter space to obtain the distribution of structural outputs under uncertainty.
Direct Monte Carlo with repeated high-fidelity solver evaluations is computationally
infeasible: each coupled OpenMC–FEniCS run requires approximately 2000 s (~33 min)
on a dedicated compute node, with the neutronics calculation accounting for
approximately 67% of the total time and the remaining time distributed between
thermo-elastic and heat-pipe wall temperature calculations (Zhang et al. 2025,
Energy, Table 1). A full forward uncertainty quantification (UQ) analysis requiring
20,000 evaluations would consume over 460 compute-days; Bayesian calibration
requiring thousands of likelihood evaluations faces a comparable or larger cost
barrier. Surrogate models that approximate the input–output mapping at negligible
per-evaluation cost are therefore essential for enabling both forward UQ and inverse
inference in this coupled system.

风险评估需要在完整 8 维材料参数空间上进行大量采样以获得不确定性下的结构输出
分布。直接蒙特卡洛暴力采样在计算上不具可行性：每次耦合 OpenMC–FEniCS 运行
约需 2000 秒（约 33 分钟），其中中子学计算约占总时间的 67%，其余时间分配于
热弹性和热管壁温计算（Zhang et al. 2025, Energy, Table 1）。包含 20,000 次
评估的完整前向 UQ 分析将消耗超过 460 个计算日；需要数千次似然评估的贝叶斯
标定面临同等或更大的计算障碍。因此，以可忽略的单次评估成本近似输入–输出映射
的代理模型对于在该耦合系统中实现前向 UQ 和逆向推断而言不可或缺。

[第六段：现有代理方法在本问题中的局限——逐一分析]

Existing surrogate approaches each face specific limitations in this context.
Gaussian process (GP) regression provides principled uncertainty estimates through
the posterior predictive distribution, but its cubic scaling with training-set size
and the curse of dimensionality in 8-dimensional input spaces limit its practical
applicability to this problem. Polynomial chaos expansion (PCE) offers spectral
convergence for smooth response surfaces, but the smoothness assumptions underlying
PCE may not hold across the full material-variability range when the response
involves coupled nonlinear feedback. Physics-informed neural networks (PINNs)
embed governing equations as residual terms in the training loss; while in principle
applicable to stationary coupled systems, the iterative boundary-condition coupling
in the HPR—where the neutronics and thermal modules exchange boundary conditions
through Picard iteration (Zhang et al. 2024)—would require reformulating the
multi-physics iteration loop as a differentiable residual, adding substantial
implementation complexity without clear benefit over data-driven surrogates trained
on converged solutions. Standard data-driven neural surrogates offer scalable
inference, but without explicit uncertainty calibration or physical consistency
constraints, they may violate known monotone input–output relationships under
distributional shift, leading to physically implausible predictions in
extrapolation regions.

现有代理方法在本问题中各有其局限性。高斯过程（GP）回归通过后验预测分布提供
原则性的不确定性估计，但其随训练集规模的立方增长和 8 维输入空间的维度灾难
限制了其在本问题上的实用性。多项式混沌展开（PCE）对光滑响应面提供谱收敛，
但当响应涉及耦合非线性反馈时，其光滑性假设在完整材料变异范围内不一定成立。
物理信息神经网络（PINN）将支配方程嵌入训练损失的残差项；尽管原则上可应用于
稳态耦合系统，但 HPR 中中子学和热学模块通过 Picard 迭代交换边界条件的耦合
方式（Zhang et al. 2024）需将多物理迭代循环重写为可微残差形式，实现复杂度高，
相比训练于收敛解的数据驱动代理无明显优势。标准数据驱动神经代理推断高效，但
在缺乏显式不确定性校准和物理一致性约束的情况下，可能在分布偏移时违反已知的
单调输入–输出关系，导致外推区域出现物理上不合理的预测。

[第七段：本文与前期工作的区别——定位]

The present work builds upon and extends our prior conference contribution
(Chen et al. 2025, RPHA), which employed a Bayesian neural network (BNN) for HPR
thermo-mechanical uncertainty quantification with three calibrated parameters and a
stress threshold of 45 MPa. The current study differs in several respects: (1) we
adopt a heteroscedastic MLP architecture that achieves comparable predictive
uncertainty at substantially lower computational cost than full BNN inference;
(2) we embed physics-consistent monotonicity and inequality constraints as
differentiable regularizers, whereas the prior work used unconstrained BNN training;
(3) we expand the calibration to four parameters and use the actual SS316 yield
strength (131 MPa) as the safety threshold; and (4) we perform a systematic
analysis of how multi-physics coupling compresses uncertainty relative to decoupled
predictions—a dimension not explored in the prior work.

本文在我们前期会议论文（Chen et al. 2025, RPHA）的基础上进行了扩展和深化。
前期工作采用贝叶斯神经网络（BNN）进行 HPR 热力不确定性量化，标定 3 个参数，
应力阈值为 45 MPa。本文在以下方面有所不同：（1）采用异方差 MLP 架构，在显著
低于 BNN 推断的计算成本下实现可比的预测不确定性估计；（2）将物理一致的单调性
和不等式约束嵌入为可微正则化项，前期工作使用无约束 BNN 训练；（3）将标定参数
扩展至 4 个，并采用 SS316 的实际屈服强度（131 MPa）作为安全阈值；（4）系统
分析了多物理耦合相对于解耦预测对不确定性的压缩效应——这是前期工作未涉及的
维度。

[第八段：本文贡献]

This work makes four contributions:
(1) We develop a HeteroMLP surrogate that jointly predicts mean and variance for
each of 15 physics outputs, enabling calibrated probabilistic inference without
the weight-distribution overhead of Bayesian neural networks—the uncertainty arises
from a heteroscedastic output head, not from maintaining a posterior over weights.
(2) We propose a physics-consistent regularised training objective that embeds known
input–output monotone relationships and output inequality constraints as gradient
penalties, and show that it improves out-of-distribution (OOD) robustness for the
stress response under α_base extrapolation (ΔR² = +0.170 relative to the
unconstrained baseline).
(3) We construct a complete uncertainty-to-risk pipeline comprising prior Monte Carlo
forward propagation, global Sobol sensitivity analysis with bootstrap confidence
intervals, and MCMC-based observation-driven posterior calibration.
(4) We quantify the degree to which multi-physics coupling compresses
material-parameter-driven uncertainty, revealing a roughly 95-fold reduction in keff
spread and a 29% reduction in stress standard deviation between the decoupled and
coupled steady-state predictions.

本文的主要贡献如下：
（1）开发异方差多层感知机（HeteroMLP）代理模型，对 15 个物理输出量同时预测

置信区间的全局 Sobol 敏感性分析和 MCMC 观测驱动后验标定；
（4）定量揭示多物理耦合对材料参数驱动不确定性的压缩效应——解耦与耦合稳态
之间 keff 不确定性压缩约 95 倍，应力标准差降低 29%。

2. 方法 / Methods
================================================================================

------------------------------------------------------------------------
2.1 材料不确定性模型 / Material Uncertainty Model
------------------------------------------------------------------------

The coupled HPR simulation involves material properties that enter the calculation
through constitutive models for elasticity, thermal expansion, and heat conduction.
Eight of these properties are treated as uncertain inputs, spanning the mechanical
and thermal characteristics of the fuel–monolith–heat-pipe system. They are grouped
by physical function into three categories.

HPR 耦合仿真涉及通过弹性、热膨胀和热传导本构模型进入计算的材料性质。其中
8 个性质被视为不确定性输入，覆盖燃料–单石–热管系统的力学和热学特征，按物理
功能分为三组。

Structural mechanics parameters govern the elastic response of the monolith under
thermal loading. The temperature-dependent Young's modulus is modelled as a linear
function E(T) = E_slope · T + E_intercept, where E_intercept determines the
baseline stiffness at the reference condition and E_slope captures its rate of
change with temperature. Poisson's ratio ν characterises the multi-axial stress
state. These three parameters (E_slope, E_intercept, ν) directly control the
magnitude and spatial distribution of thermal stress through the generalised
Hooke's law.

结构力学参数控制单石在热载荷下的弹性响应。温度相关弹性模量建模为线性函数
E(T) = E_slope · T + E_intercept，其中 E_intercept 确定参考条件下的基础
刚度，E_slope 捕捉其随温度的变化速率。泊松比 ν 表征多轴应力状态。这三个
参数（E_slope、E_intercept、ν）通过广义胡克定律直接控制热应力的大小和空间
分布。

Thermal deformation parameters determine how much the monolith geometry changes
under heating. The temperature-dependent thermal expansion coefficient is modelled as
α(T) = α_base + α_slope · T, where α_base is the expansion coefficient at the
reference condition and α_slope captures its temperature dependence. These two
parameters govern the thermal strain field, which serves as the loading source
for thermal stress; they also control core geometric expansion, which is the
primary driver of neutronic feedback through changes in neutron leakage.

热变形参数决定单石几何形状在加热下的变化量。温度相关热膨胀系数建模为
α(T) = α_base + α_slope · T，其中 α_base 为参考条件下的膨胀系数，α_slope
捕捉其温度依赖性。这两个参数控制热应变场——它是热应力的载荷源——同时也控制
堆芯几何膨胀，后者通过改变中子泄漏率成为中子学反馈的首要驱动因素。

SS316 thermal transport parameters characterise the heat conduction behaviour of the
stainless-steel monolith, which serves as the primary thermal pathway from fuel to
heat pipes. The thermal conductivity is modelled as
k_SS316(T) = k_ref + k_slope · (T − T_ref), where T_ref is the reference
temperature, k_ref is the conductivity at T_ref, and k_slope is the temperature
coefficient of conductivity (units: W/(m·K²)). Note that k_slope is distinct from
the structural thermal expansion parameters α_base and α_slope: the former governs
heat transport, while the latter govern geometric deformation. These three parameters
(T_ref, k_ref, k_slope) control the temperature gradient across the monolith, which
is the physical intermediary between fission power deposition and thermal stress.

SS316 热传导参数表征不锈钢单石的导热行为——单石是从燃料到热管的主要热通道。
热导率建模为 k_SS316(T) = k_ref + k_slope · (T − T_ref)，其中 T_ref 为
参考温度，k_ref 为 T_ref 处的热导率，k_slope 为热导率温度系数（单位：
W/(m·K²)）。注意 k_slope 与结构热膨胀参数 α_base、α_slope 在物理含义上完全
不同：前者控制热传输，后者控制几何变形。这三个参数（T_ref、k_ref、k_slope）
控制单石内的温度梯度——它是裂变功率沉积与热应力之间的物理中间量。

全部 8 个参数均被赋予独立均匀先验分布，半宽等于标称设计值的 10%：

2.2 耦合高保真仿真 / Coupled High-Fidelity Simulation
------------------------------------------------------------------------

The high-fidelity workflow couples OpenMC Monte Carlo neutronics with the FEniCS
finite-element solver in an iterative feedback loop, following the Picard
fixed-point iteration scheme described in Zhang et al. (2024). Starting from initial
material parameters, the neutronics module computes the effective neutron
multiplication factor keff and the volumetric fission power distribution. These
outputs drive the FEniCS thermal–structural computation, which solves for the
temperature field, thermal expansion, and stress distribution. The resulting
temperature and displacement fields update the material properties (temperature-
dependent elastic modulus and thermal conductivity) and the core geometry
(thermal expansion), which are fed back into the neutronics calculation. Iteration
continues until keff and the temperature field converge to a self-consistent steady
state, with convergence defined as a maximum temperature difference below 1 K
between successive iterations combined with an under-relaxation factor of 0.1 to
stabilise and accelerate the process (Zhang et al. 2025, Energy).

高保真工作流以迭代反馈方式耦合 OpenMC 蒙特卡洛中子学求解器和 FEniCS 有限元
求解器，遵循 Zhang et al.（2024）描述的 Picard 不动点迭代方案。从初始材料
参数出发，中子学模块计算有效中子增殖因子 keff 和体积裂变功率分布；这些输出
驱动 FEniCS 热–结构计算，求解温度场、热膨胀和应力分布；所得温度和位移场更新
材料性质（温度相关弹性模量和热导率）和堆芯几何（热膨胀），反馈回中子学计算。
迭代持续至 keff 和温度场达到自洽稳态，收敛判据为连续迭代间最大温差低于 1 K，
并结合 0.1 的亚松弛因子以稳定和加速收敛过程（Zhang et al. 2025, Energy）。

Each complete coupled evaluation requires approximately 2000 s (~33 min) on a
dedicated compute node, with the computational cost dominated by the neutronics
module (approximately 67% of total time), followed by the thermo-elastic module
and heat-pipe wall-temperature calculation (Zhang et al. 2025, Energy, Table 1).

每次完整耦合评估约需 2000 秒（约 33 分钟），计算成本由中子学模块主导
（约占总时间 67%），其次是热弹性模块和热管壁温计算（Zhang et al. 2025,
Energy, Table 1）。

[物理耦合路径 / Physical coupling pathways]

The coupled HPR system exhibits two distinct physical pathways through which
material-parameter uncertainty propagates to safety-critical outputs:

耦合 HPR 系统中材料不确定性通过两条独特的物理路径传播至安全关键输出：

(i) Stress pathway. Young's modulus intercept E_intercept is the primary stiffness
parameter: through the constitutive relation σ = E · ε_thermal, where thermal strain
ε_thermal = α · ΔT is determined by the temperature field, a higher E_intercept
directly amplifies thermal stress under the same temperature gradient. Thermal
conductivity k_ref,SS316 governs the temperature gradient itself: higher conductivity
reduces the fuel-to-heat-pipe temperature difference, producing smaller differential
thermal expansion and thus lower stress; lower conductivity amplifies the gradient,
increasing stress. This pathway is primarily governed by elastic and thermal-transport
properties. At first order, it has limited direct coupling to the neutronics feedback,
because the stress is predominantly determined by the local temperature gradient and
stiffness rather than by the global power level.

（i）应力路径。弹性模量截距 E_intercept 是主要刚度参数：通过本构关系
σ = E · ε_thermal（其中热应变 ε_thermal = α · ΔT 由温度场决定），更高的
E_intercept 在相同温度梯度下直接放大热应力。热导率 k_ref,SS316 控制温度梯度
本身：热导率越高，燃料至热管的温差越小，差异热膨胀和热应力越低；热导率越低，
温差和应力越大。该路径主要由弹性和热传导性质控制。在一阶上，它与中子物理反馈
的直接耦合有限，因为应力主要由局部温度梯度和刚度决定，而非全局功率水平。

(ii) Reactivity pathway. Thermal expansion coefficient α_base controls the
geometric expansion of the core under heating. In the coupled steady state, a
higher α_base causes greater radial and axial expansion of the monolith, which
increases the effective neutron leakage probability by enlarging the core volume
relative to its surface area. This increased leakage reduces keff and, consequently,
fission power. The resulting decrease in power density lowers the temperature field,
which partially suppresses the thermal expansion itself—creating a self-stabilising
negative feedback chain: α_base↑ → core expansion↑ → neutron leakage↑ → keff↓ →
fission power↓ → temperature↓ → thermal stress partially↓. This
expansion–leakage–power chain constitutes the negative reactivity feedback that is
the fundamental self-regulation mechanism in compact reactor designs.

（ii）反应性路径。热膨胀系数 α_base 控制加热时堆芯的几何膨胀。在耦合稳态中，
较高的 α_base 引起单石较大的径向和轴向膨胀，通过增大堆芯体积与表面积的比值
提高有效中子泄漏概率。泄漏增大导致 keff 降低，进而降低裂变功率。功率密度的
降低使温度场下降，温度下降又部分抑制了热膨胀本身——形成自稳定的负反馈链：
α_base↑ → 堆芯膨胀↑ → 中子泄漏↑ → keff↓ → 裂变功率↓ → 温度↓ → 热应力
部分↓。这一"膨胀–泄漏–功率"链条构成了紧凑型反应堆设计中基本的负反应性反馈
自调节机制。

These pathways interact: high α_base simultaneously tends to increase thermal stress
through direct expansion (the strain ε_thermal = α · ΔT increases) and to decrease
it through the reactivity feedback (reduced power lowers ΔT). The net effect is a
partial cancellation: the coupled steady-state stress standard deviation is
approximately 29% lower than the decoupled prediction, which omits the reactivity
feedback entirely. This negative-feedback-driven compression of output uncertainty
is the primary physical mechanism studied in Section 3.2.

两条路径相互作用：较高的 α_base 同时倾向于通过直接热膨胀效应增大热应力
（热应变 ε_thermal = α · ΔT 增大）和通过反应性负反馈减小热应力（功率降低使
ΔT 减小）。净效果是部分对消：耦合稳态应力标准差比完全忽略反应性反馈的解耦
预测低约 29%。这种由负反馈驱动的输出不确定性压缩效应是第 3.2 节研究的核心
物理机制。

α_base 应导致 keff 降低（更大膨胀→更多中子泄漏→keff↓），这是标准的负温度

We define two fidelity levels for the surrogate training and analysis: the
decoupled single-pass prediction, which represents the outputs of a single
simulation pass before the iterative coupling converges; and the coupled
steady-state, which represents the outputs after full convergence. The former serves
as a reference baseline to quantify the effect of multi-physics coupling on
uncertainty propagation—it is the prediction one would obtain if the coupling
feedback were entirely absent.

定义两个精度层次用于代理训练和分析：解耦单轮预测——迭代耦合收敛之前的单次
仿真输出；耦合稳态——完全收敛后的输出。前者作为参考基准，量化多物理耦合对
不确定性传播的影响——即在完全忽略耦合反馈的情况下所得的预测。

Note that the decoupled keff (from the first iteration) reflects the initial
neutronics calculation before thermal feedback has been incorporated; because the
power distribution has not yet converged with the temperature field, this keff value
does not represent a physical steady-state criticality measure. It is included
solely to quantify the scale of uncertainty compression across fidelity levels.

注意：解耦 keff（来自第一轮迭代）反映热反馈纳入前的初始中子学计算结果；由于
功率分布与温度场尚未耦合一致，该 keff 值不代表物理上的稳态临界条件，仅用于
量化跨精度层次的不确定性压缩尺度。

A dataset of n = 2900 samples was generated by Latin hypercube sampling of the
8-dimensional input space, covering the full prior uncertainty range. Each sample
provides 15 physics outputs: 7 decoupled quantities (average fuel temperature,
peak fuel temperature, peak monolith temperature, maximum global stress, monolith
temperature, core height after expansion, wall expansion) and 8 coupled quantities
(keff plus the same 7 structural/thermal outputs). The dataset was split into
training (n_train = 2029, ~70%), validation (n_val = 436, ~15%), and test
(n_test = 435, ~15%) sets using a fixed random seed prior to any model selection,
ensuring that no information from the test set influenced model training or
hyperparameter tuning.

通过 8 维输入空间的拉丁超立方采样生成 n = 2900 个样本的数据集，覆盖完整
先验不确定性范围。每个样本提供 15 个物理输出量：7 个解耦量（平均燃料温度、
峰值燃料温度、峰值单石温度、最大全局应力、单石温度、膨胀后堆芯高度、壁面
膨胀量）和 8 个耦合量（keff 加上相同的 7 个结构/热学输出）。数据集在任何
模型选择之前以固定随机种子划分为训练集（2029 个，约 70%）、验证集（436 个，
约 15%）和测试集（435 个，约 15%），确保测试集信息不影响模型训练或超参数调优。

2.3 物理正则化概率代理模型 / Physics-Regularized Probabilistic Surrogate
------------------------------------------------------------------------

[架构]

The surrogate used in this study is a heteroscedastic multilayer perceptron (HeteroMLP), a deterministic feedforward network that predicts both the mean and variance of each output. Unlike Bayesian neural networks, which represent uncertainty through distributions over weights, the HeteroMLP produces predictive uncertainty through an input-dependent output head, allowing efficient probabilistic inference with a single forward pass.

Two training variants are considered. The first is a data-only baseline trained with the heteroscedastic Gaussian negative log-likelihood. The second augments this objective with monotonicity and inequality regularization to improve physical consistency and robustness. In the current manuscript, all reported primary quantitative results are based on the regularized surrogate variant used in the archived experiment package; the exact final configuration name and whether it should be treated as data-screened or fully physics-prior regularization remain 待核实 and must be unified before submission.

本文采用的代理模型为异方差多层感知机（HeteroMLP），其本质上是一个确定性前馈网络，同时预测每个输出量的均值和方差。与通过权重分布表达不确定性的贝叶斯神经网络不同，HeteroMLP 通过输入依赖的输出头给出预测不确定性，因此可在单次前向传播中实现高效的概率推断。

本文考虑两类训练变体。第一类为纯数据驱动基线模型，仅以异方差高斯负对数似然作为训练目标；第二类在此基础上进一步引入单调性约束和不等式约束，以提升物理一致性和分布外鲁棒性。当前稿件中报告的主要定量结果均来自归档实验包中的正则化代理模型；其最终应统一表述为“数据筛选的物理一致正则化”还是“完全基于物理先验的正则化”，目前仍待核实，投稿前必须统一。

This architectural choice is motivated by computational efficiency: the HeteroMLP
requires only one forward pass per evaluation (cost comparable to a standard MLP),
whereas BNN inference with Linear Reparameterisation layers (as used in Chen et al.
2025, RPHA) requires multiple stochastic forward passes to approximate the
predictive distribution. The heteroscedastic output head naturally captures
input-dependent aleatoric uncertainty—the observation that prediction uncertainty
may vary across the input space, for instance being larger for input configurations
that produce near-threshold stress values where the response surface is steeper.

该架构选择以计算效率为动机：HeteroMLP 每次评估仅需一次前向传播（成本与标准
MLP 相当），而采用线性重参数化层的 BNN 推断（如 Chen et al. 2025, RPHA 中
使用的）需要多次随机前向传播来近似预测分布。异方差输出头自然捕捉输入依赖的
偶然不确定性——即预测不确定性可能在输入空间中变化的特征，例如在产生近阈值
应力的输入配置处（响应面更陡）不确定性更大。

[训练目标]

The data-only baseline surrogate is trained by minimising the heteroscedastic
Gaussian negative log-likelihood (NLL):
  L_NLL = (1/N) Σ_i Σ_j [(y_ij − μ_ij)² / (2σ²_ij) + (1/2) log σ²_ij]
This training objective jointly optimises predictive accuracy (the residual term
penalises mean prediction errors) and uncertainty calibration (the variance term
prevents the model from assigning uniformly large or small uncertainty). Without
structural constraints, the baseline model has no guarantee of preserving known
monotone input–output relationships, particularly in data-sparse regions of the
input space.

纯数据驱动基线代理最小化异方差高斯负对数似然（NLL）：
  L_NLL = (1/N) Σ_i Σ_j [(y_ij − μ_ij)² / (2σ²_ij) + (1/2) log σ²_ij]
该训练目标同时优化预测精度（残差项惩罚均值预测误差）和不确定性校准（方差项
防止模型赋予均一过大或过小的不确定性）。无结构约束下，基线模型不保证在输入
空间的数据稀疏区域保持已知的单调输入–输出关系。

The physics-regularized surrogate augments L_NLL with two constraint terms derived
from physical reasoning about the system.

物理正则化代理在 L_NLL 基础上附加两个基于系统物理推理的约束项。

Monotonicity constraint. For each identified monotone input–output pair (input
dimension i, output dimension j) with expected gradient sign s_ij ∈ {+1, −1}, the
penalty penalises violations of the gradient sign via:
  L_mono = w_mono · (1/|P|) Σ_{(i,j,s)∈P} mean_batch[ReLU(−s_ij · ∂μ_j/∂x_i)]
where P is the set of constrained pairs and w_mono is an Optuna-optimised weight.
The monotone pairs are derived from physical reasoning about the HPR system:
  - E_intercept → stress (+1): higher stiffness amplifies thermal stress (σ ∝ E·ε)
  - α_base → stress (+1): higher expansion → more thermal strain → more stress
  - k_ref,SS316 → stress (−1): higher conductivity → lower ΔT → less stress
  - k_ref,SS316 → fuel temperature (−1): higher conductivity → better heat removal
  - k_ref,SS316 → monolith temperature (−1): same heat-removal mechanism
  - α_base → keff (−1): higher expansion → more neutron leakage → lower keff
    (medium confidence, marked for validation)
  - α_slope → keff (−1): same mechanism as α_base (medium confidence)

单调性约束。对每个识别出的单调输入–输出对（输入维度 i，输出维度 j，期望梯度
符号 s_ij ∈ {+1, −1}），通过梯度符号惩罚违反项：
  L_mono = w_mono · (1/|P|) Σ_{(i,j,s)∈P} mean_batch[ReLU(−s_ij · ∂μ_j/∂x_i)]
其中 P 为约束对集合，w_mono 为 Optuna 优化的权重参数。单调对来自对 HPR 系统的
物理推理：
  - E_intercept → 应力 (+1)：更高刚度放大热应力（σ ∝ E·ε）
  - α_base → 应力 (+1)：更大膨胀→更多热应变→更大应力
  - k_ref,SS316 → 应力 (−1)：更高导热→更小ΔT→更小应力
  - k_ref,SS316 → 燃料温度 (−1)：更高导热→更好的散热
  - k_ref,SS316 → 单石温度 (−1)：同样的散热机制
  - α_base → keff (−1)：更大膨胀→更多中子泄漏→更低keff（中等置信度）
  - α_slope → keff (−1)：与α_base机制相同（中等置信度）

Inequality constraint. Physical consistency requires that certain output
relationships hold for all inputs—for example, the peak fuel temperature must be
at least as high as the average fuel temperature, and the peak monolith temperature
must exceed the average monolith temperature. The inequality regulariser penalises
violations of these ordering relationships:
  L_ineq = w_ineq · (1/|Q|) Σ_{(j,k)∈Q} mean_batch[ReLU(μ_k − μ_j)]
where Q is the set of inequality-constrained output pairs (j must be ≥ k) and
w_ineq is the corresponding regularisation weight. This constraint prevents the
surrogate from producing physically impossible output configurations such as an
average temperature exceeding the peak temperature.

不等式约束。物理一致性要求某些输出关系对所有输入成立——例如，峰值燃料温度
必须不低于平均燃料温度，峰值单石温度必须超过平均单石温度。不等式正则化项
惩罚这些顺序关系的违反：
  L_ineq = w_ineq · (1/|Q|) Σ_{(j,k)∈Q} mean_batch[ReLU(μ_k − μ_j)]
其中 Q 为不等式约束的输出对集合（j 必须 ≥ k），w_ineq 为对应的正则化权重。
该约束防止代理产生物理上不可能的输出配置，如平均温度超过峰值温度。

The total training loss is:
  L = L_NLL + L_mono + L_ineq.

总训练损失为：L = L_NLL + L_mono + L_ineq。

2.4 前向不确定性传播 / Forward Uncertainty Propagation
------------------------------------------------------------------------

The goal of forward uncertainty propagation is to characterise how the joint prior
uncertainty in the 8 material parameters translates into uncertainty in the physics
outputs of interest, particularly the coupled steady-state stress and keff.

前向不确定性传播的目标是表征 8 个材料参数的联合先验不确定性如何转化为关注的
物理输出——特别是耦合稳态应力和 keff——的不确定性。

20,000 samples are drawn from the joint prior distribution by Latin hypercube
sampling to ensure uniform coverage of the 8-dimensional input space. For each
sample θ_i, the surrogate returns the predictive mean μ(θ_i) for all 15 outputs.
The empirical distribution of these means constitutes the prior-predictive
uncertainty propagation. We use the surrogate mean rather than sampling from the
full predictive distribution (which would additionally incorporate the
heteroscedastic variance) to isolate the epistemic component of uncertainty—the
variability arising from material-parameter uncertainty alone, without the
additional spread from surrogate prediction noise. This choice provides a
conservative lower bound on exceedance probability: incorporating the predictive
variance would further broaden the output distribution and increase the
exceedance probability estimate.

从联合先验分布通过拉丁超立方采样抽取 20,000 个样本，以确保 8 维输入空间的
均匀覆盖。对每个样本 θ_i，代理模型返回所有 15 个输出的预测均值 μ(θ_i)。这些
均值的经验分布构成先验预测不确定性传播结果。使用代理均值而非从完整预测分布中
采样（后者还会纳入异方差方差），以隔离认知不确定性分量——仅由材料参数不确定性
引起的变异，不含代理预测噪声的额外展宽。此选择提供超阈概率的保守下界：纳入
预测方差将进一步展宽输出分布，增大超阈概率估计。

The comparison between the decoupled and coupled output distributions directly
reveals the effect of multi-physics coupling on uncertainty propagation: any
narrowing of the coupled distribution relative to the decoupled distribution is
attributable to the coupling feedback mechanisms described in Section 2.2.

解耦与耦合输出分布的对比直接揭示多物理耦合对不确定性传播的影响：耦合分布
相对于解耦分布的任何收窄均可归因于第 2.2 节所述的耦合反馈机制。

2.5 全局敏感性分析 / Global Sensitivity Analysis
------------------------------------------------------------------------

Global variance-based sensitivity analysis decomposes the total variance of a model
output into contributions attributable to each input and their interactions. This
decomposition is based on the ANOVA-like functional representation of the model
(Sobol 1993), which expresses the total output variance as a sum of terms
corresponding to individual inputs, pairs of inputs, and higher-order combinations.

全局方差基敏感性分析将模型输出的总方差分解为各输入及其交互作用的贡献。该分解
基于模型的 ANOVA 类函数表示（Sobol 1993），将总输出方差表示为对应于单个输入、
输入对和高阶组合的项之和。

The first-order Sobol index S₁ for input i measures the fraction of total output
variance that would be eliminated if the true value of x_i were known:
  S₁_i = Var[E(Y | X_i)] / Var[Y].
It quantifies the direct, independent contribution of each input to the output
uncertainty. The total-order index S_T measures the total effect of x_i including
all interactions with other inputs:
  S_T_i = 1 − Var[E(Y | X_{~i})] / Var[Y],
where X_{~i} denotes all inputs except x_i. The difference S_T − S₁ indicates the
magnitude of interaction effects involving x_i.

输入 i 的一阶 Sobol 指数 S₁ 衡量若已知 x_i 的真值后可消除的总输出方差比例：
  S₁_i = Var[E(Y | X_i)] / Var[Y]。
它量化每个输入对输出不确定性的直接、独立贡献。总阶指数 S_T 衡量 x_i 包括与
其他输入全部交互作用的综合效应：
  S_T_i = 1 − Var[E(Y | X_{~i})] / Var[Y]，
其中 X_{~i} 表示除 x_i 外的所有输入。差值 S_T − S₁ 指示涉及 x_i 的交互作用
效应大小。

Indices are estimated using the Saltelli quasi-random sampling scheme with the
Jansen estimator, which provides a numerically stable estimate of S_T that avoids
the underestimation bias present in older formulations. Specifically, two
independent quasi-random matrices A and B (each of size N_S × 8, with N_S = 4096)
are constructed, and the cross-matrices A_B^(i) are formed by replacing the i-th
column of A with the corresponding column of B. This yields N_S · (8 + 2) = 40,960
model evaluations per output per replication. Because the surrogate mean μ(θ)
serves as the function evaluator, the full Sobol analysis cost is reduced from
thousands of HF evaluations to a fraction of a second.

指数采用 Saltelli 准随机采样方案结合 Jansen 估计量计算——Jansen 估计量提供
数值稳定的 S_T 估计，避免了旧公式中常见的低估偏差。具体而言，构造两个独立
准随机矩阵 A 和 B（各 N_S × 8，N_S = 4096），通过将 A 的第 i 列替换为 B 的
对应列形成交叉矩阵 A_B^(i)，每个输出每次重复共需 N_S · (8 + 2) = 40,960 次
模型评估。由于代理均值 μ(θ) 充当函数评估器，全 Sobol 分析的计算成本从数千次
高保真评估压缩至不到一秒。

To quantify the statistical uncertainty of the estimated indices, the entire Sobol
analysis is independently repeated 50 times with different random seeds. The mean
and standard error of S₁ across replications provide a normal-approximation 90%
confidence interval: S̄₁ ± 1.645 · SE. A factor is considered a stable contributor
only if the lower bound of this confidence interval exceeds zero; factors whose CI
includes zero are not treated as reliably identified sensitivity drivers.

为量化估计指数的统计不确定性，整个 Sobol 分析以不同随机种子独立重复 50 次。
S₁ 跨重复的均值和标准误提供正态近似 90% 置信区间：S̄₁ ± 1.645 · SE。仅当该
置信区间下界大于零时，该因子被视为稳定贡献者；CI 包含零的因子不被视为可靠
识别的敏感性驱动因素。

2.6 观测驱动后验标定 / Observation-Driven Posterior Calibration
------------------------------------------------------------------------

The posterior calibration framework addresses the inverse problem: given
observations of physics outputs (such as in-situ stress measurements), what can
be inferred about the material parameters that produced them? The framework
combines the surrogate model with Bayesian inference to update the prior
distribution of selected material parameters conditioned on observed output data.

后验标定框架处理逆问题：给定物理输出的观测（如原位应力测量），能够推断产生
这些观测的材料参数的哪些信息？该框架将代理模型与贝叶斯推断结合，在观测输出
数据的条件下更新选定材料参数的先验分布。

[标定参数选择]

Four parameters are selected for calibration: E_intercept, α_base, α_slope, and ν.
This selection is motivated by the Sobol sensitivity analysis (Section 2.5) computed
under the data-mono-ineq canonical model: E_intercept is the dominant stress driver
(S₁ ≈ 0.586), α_base and α_slope are the dominant keff drivers (S₁ ≈ 0.771 and 0.198),
and ν is the third-ranked stress driver (S₁ ≈ 0.064, CI clearly away from zero)
through its role in the multi-axial stress state. Together, these four parameters
encompass the leading sensitivity factors for both primary outputs of interest.
The remaining four parameters (E_slope, T_ref,SS316, k_ref,SS316, k_slope,SS316)
are held at their true test-case values, because their first-order Sobol indices
have confidence intervals that include or approach zero and they are not identified
as stable sensitivity drivers—fixing them reduces the calibration dimension without
significant loss of posterior expressiveness. In particular, k_ref,SS316 is no longer
a prominent stress driver under data-mono-ineq (unlike the earlier phy-mono variant),
and its calibration would additionally require independent in-situ thermal
conductivity measurements to provide an informative likelihood, which are not
available in this benchmark.

标定 4 个参数：E_intercept、α_base、α_slope 和 ν。此选择以数据单调-不等式约束
（data-mono-ineq）canonical 模型下的 Sobol 敏感性分析（第 2.5 节）为依据：
E_intercept 为应力主控因子（S₁ ≈ 0.586），α_base 与 α_slope 为 keff 主控因子
（S₁ ≈ 0.771 与 0.198），ν 则通过多轴应力状态成为应力第三灵敏因子
（S₁ ≈ 0.064，置信区间明显远离零）。这四个参数共同涵盖了两个主要输出的首要
敏感因子。其余 4 个参数（E_slope、T_ref,SS316、k_ref,SS316、k_slope,SS316）
固定在对应案例的真实输入值：它们的一阶 Sobol 指数置信区间包含或接近零，不被
识别为稳定敏感因子——固定它们可在不显著损失后验表达能力的前提下降低标定维度。
其中 k_ref,SS316 在 data-mono-ineq 下已不再是主导应力因子（与早期 phy-mono
版本不同），且将其纳入标定还需独立的原位热导率测量以提供信息性似然，本基准
中不具备此类观测。

[基准案例来源]

Benchmark cases are drawn from the test split (experiments_0404/_shared/fixed_split/),
ensuring independence from the surrogate training data. 18 benchmark cases span three
stress categories (6 low, 6 near-threshold, 6 high), covering a stress range of
approximately 102–198 MPa. A separate set of 10 extreme-stress reference cases
with true stress values of 133–194 MPa (all exceeding the 131 MPa design threshold)
is used to evaluate posterior updating under safety-critical conditions.

基准案例来自测试集划分（experiments_0404/_shared/fixed_split/），确保与代理训练
数据的独立性。18 个基准案例涵盖三个应力类别（6 低、6 近阈值、6 高），应力范围
约 102–198 MPa。另有 10 个极端应力参考案例（真实应力 133–194 MPa，均超过
131 MPa 设计阈值）用于评估安全关键条件下的后验更新能力。

[似然函数与噪声模型]

Observations comprise the five primary coupled outputs (stress, fuel temperature,
monolith temperature, wall expansion, keff) corrupted by independent Gaussian noise
with fractional standard deviation σ_obs = 2% of the true output value. This
noise model simulates the combined effect of instrument measurement error and
surrogate interpolation error: each observed quantity y_obs is drawn from
N(y_true, (0.02 · y_true)²). For example, if the true stress is 150 MPa, the
observation noise standard deviation is 3 MPa; if keff = 1.10, the noise
standard deviation is 0.022.

观测包括 5 个主要耦合输出（应力、燃料温度、单石温度、壁面膨胀量、keff），
每个量独立受到分数标准差 σ_obs = 2% 的高斯噪声污染。该噪声模型模拟仪器测量
误差和代理插值误差的综合效应：每个观测量 y_obs 从 N(y_true, (0.02·y_true)²)
中采样。例如，若真实应力为 150 MPa，则观测噪声标准差约 3 MPa；若 keff = 1.10，
则噪声标准差为 0.022。

[MCMC协议]

Each MCMC chain uses the Metropolis-Hastings (MH) algorithm with a Gaussian
proposal distribution. Each chain runs for 8000 total iterations with 2000
burn-in steps discarded and a thinning factor of 5, yielding 1200 effective
posterior samples per case. The proposal scale is set to 0.40, tuned to achieve
acceptance rates in the range consistent with adequate posterior exploration for a
4-dimensional target distribution. The observed acceptance rates across all
benchmark cases range from 0.47 to 0.61. For a 4-dimensional MH sampler, the
theoretically optimal acceptance rate is approximately 0.44 for a Gaussian target
(Roberts et al. 1997); the observed range indicates that the proposal scale covers
the posterior geometry without excessive rejection, confirming adequate chain
exploration.

每条 MCMC 链使用高斯提议分布的 Metropolis-Hastings（MH）算法，运行 8000 步
迭代，丢弃前 2000 步烧入期，每 5 步保留一个样本，每个案例共获得 1200 个有效
后验样本。提议尺度设为 0.40，调优以在 4 维目标分布中达到充分后验探索的接受率
范围。所有基准案例的观测接受率为 0.47–0.61。对 4 维 MH 采样器，理论最优接受率
约 0.44（高斯目标，Roberts et al. 1997）；观测范围表明提议尺度能够覆盖后验
几何形状而不产生过多拒绝，确认链的探索充分。

Convergence is assessed by trace-plot inspection: after the burn-in period, all
four calibrated parameters exhibit stationary random fluctuations within consistent
ranges without directional drift.

收敛性通过 trace plot 检验评估：烧入期结束后，4 个标定参数均呈现在一致范围内
的平稳随机波动，无方向性漂移。

3.2 前向不确定性传播 / Forward Uncertainty Propagation
------------------------------------------------------------------------

Multi-physics coupling substantially reshaped both the stress and keff uncertainty
distributions (Table 4, Fig. 3).

多物理耦合显著重塑了应力和 keff 的不确定性分布（表 4，图 3）。

In the decoupled single-pass prediction, the maximum global stress distribution
had a mean of 205.0 MPa and a standard deviation of approximately 42.9 MPa.
The decoupled keff standard deviation was approximately 0.063 (approximately
6300 pcm). As noted in Section 2.2, this decoupled keff reflects the initial
neutronics calculation before thermal feedback and does not represent a steady-state
criticality measure; it is reported solely to quantify the scale of uncertainty
compression.

解耦单轮预测下，最大全局应力分布的均值为 205.0 MPa，标准差约为 42.9 MPa。
解耦 keff 标准差约为 0.063（约 6300 pcm）。如第 2.2 节所述，该解耦 keff
反映热反馈纳入前的初始中子学计算，不代表稳态临界条件，仅用于量化不确定性
压缩尺度。

In the coupled steady-state, both distributions narrowed markedly. The stress mean
fell to 162.6 MPa—a reduction of 42.4 MPa (20.7% relative to the decoupled
value)—and the standard deviation contracted from 42.9 to 30.5 MPa, a 29%
reduction. The compression of keff uncertainty was more pronounced: the coupled
steady-state standard deviation was 6.6 × 10⁻⁴ (approximately 66 pcm),
representing a roughly 95-fold reduction from the decoupled value.

耦合稳态下，两类分布均显著收窄。应力均值降至 162.6 MPa——相对于解耦值降低
42.4 MPa（20.7%）——标准差从 42.9 收缩至 30.5 MPa，降幅 29%。keff 不确定性的
压缩更为显著：耦合稳态标准差为 6.6 × 10⁻⁴（约 66 pcm），较解耦值压缩
约 95 倍。

The physical mechanism driving this compression is the neutronics–thermal negative
feedback loop described in Section 2.2. In the decoupled prediction, a sample
with high α_base produces high thermal strain and high stress without any
moderating feedback from the neutronics. In the coupled steady-state, the same
high α_base sample triggers core expansion, which increases neutron leakage,
reduces keff, lowers fission power density, and thereby partially offsets the
temperature increase. This self-stabilising feedback attenuates the spread of the
output distribution. The 29% stress-std compression indicates that a substantial
fraction of the apparent material-parameter-driven stress variability predicted by a
decoupled analysis is suppressed by the coupling feedback. The roughly 95-fold keff
compression reflects the particularly strong negative feedback on keff: the
coupling drives all sampled configurations toward a narrow band of keff values
near the self-consistent steady state.

驱动这一压缩的物理机制是第 2.2 节描述的中子–热负反馈回路。在解耦预测中，
高 α_base 的样本产生高热应变和高应力，而无中子学的调节反馈。在耦合稳态中，
同一高 α_base 样本触发堆芯膨胀，增大中子泄漏，降低 keff 和裂变功率密度，
从而部分抵消温度升高。这一自稳定反馈衰减了输出分布的展宽。29% 的应力标准差
压缩表明，解耦分析预测的由材料参数驱动的表观应力变异中相当一部分被耦合反馈
所抑制。约 95 倍的 keff 压缩反映了 keff 上特别强的负反馈：耦合将所有采样
配置驱动至自洽稳态附近的一个窄带 keff 值。

A direct consequence is that performing UQ at the decoupled level would
systematically overestimate the tail stress risk, and would entirely miss the
roughly 95-fold keff uncertainty compression. This finding is
consistent with Chen et al. (2025, 核动力工程), who observed that multi-physics
coupling can produce uncertainty compression effects that single-physics analysis
cannot capture, and extends it by providing a quantitative characterisation of the
compression magnitude for both stress and keff.

直接推论是，在解耦层次开展 UQ 将系统性地高估尾部应力风险，并完全忽略
约 95 倍的 keff 不确定性压缩。该发现与 Chen et al.（2025, 核动力工程）的观测
一致——多物理耦合可产生单物理分析无法捕捉的不确定性压缩效应——并将其扩展为
对应力和 keff 压缩幅度的定量表征。

Sobol sensitivity analysis identified distinct dominant pathways for the two primary

For coupled steady-state maximum global stress (data-mono-ineq surrogate),
E_intercept was the dominant first-order factor with S₁ = 0.586 (90% CI:

耦合稳态最大全局应力（data-mono-ineq 代理）：E_intercept 是最主导的一阶因子

The dominance of E_intercept reflects its role as the primary stiffness parameter
in the constitutive relation σ = E · ε_thermal. Higher E_intercept directly
amplifies thermal stress under the same temperature gradient through the mechanical
pathway, independently of the neutronics coupling. This independence from the
coupling pathway explains both the high magnitude of S₁ and its narrow confidence
interval: E_intercept affects stress through a direct, first-order mechanical
channel that does not require interaction with other parameters.

E_intercept 的主导性反映其作为本构关系 σ = E · ε_thermal 中主要刚度参数的
角色。更高的 E_intercept 通过力学路径在相同温度梯度下直接放大热应力，独立于
中子学耦合。这种与耦合路径的独立性解释了 S₁ 的高数值和窄置信区间：
E_intercept 通过直接的一阶力学通道影响应力，不需要与其他参数的交互作用。

The smaller but non-zero S₁ of ν (0.064) reflects a geometric coupling in the
elastic response: Poisson's ratio modulates the multiaxial stress state under
constrained thermal expansion, partially masked by the stronger stiffness effect.
Together, E_intercept (S₁ ≈ 0.586, ~59% of variance), α_base (S₁ ≈ 0.169,
~17%), and ν (S₁ ≈ 0.064, ~6%) collectively account for approximately 82% of
the coupled stress variance. The remaining ~18% reflects higher-order interactions
and contributions from the other five inputs, none of which rises above the noise
floor at 90% CI.

ν 的较小但非零的 S₁（0.064）反映了弹性响应中的几何耦合：泊松比在约束热膨胀
下调制多轴应力状态，但这一效应被更强的刚度效应所部分掩盖。综合来看，
E_intercept（S₁ ≈ 0.586，~59% 方差）、α_base（S₁ ≈ 0.169，~17%）和 ν
（S₁ ≈ 0.064，~6%）三者共覆盖约 82% 的耦合应力方差。其余约 18% 反映高阶
交互作用和其他 5 个输入的贡献。

For coupled steady-state keff, α_base was the single dominant factor with
S₁ = 0.771 (CI: 0.770–0.772), followed by α_slope as a secondary contributor
(S₁ = 0.198, CI: 0.195–0.200). The remaining six parameters had small S₁ values
with confidence intervals including zero. The dominance of α_base reflects that
thermal expansion governs core geometry—and thereby neutron leakage probability—
which is the principal physical mechanism determining keff variation in the coupled
steady state.

耦合稳态 keff：α_base 是唯一主控因子（S₁ = 0.771，CI：0.770–0.772），α_slope
为次级贡献者（S₁ = 0.198，CI：0.195–0.200）。其余 6 个参数的 S₁ 值较小且
置信区间包含零。α_base 的主导性反映热膨胀控制堆芯几何形状——进而控制中子泄漏
概率——这是决定耦合稳态 keff 变异的主要物理机制。

The comparison between S₁ and S_T provides additional insight into interaction
strength. For E_intercept on stress, S_T − S₁ was small (< 0.03), confirming
that E_intercept affects stress primarily through a direct, independent channel
with limited higher-order interaction. For α_base on keff, S_T − S₁ was also
small, consistent with α_base acting as a dominant independent driver of
reactivity. The small interaction terms support the interpretation that the two
primary outputs are governed by largely non-overlapping physical pathways at the
dominant-factor level.

S₁ 与 S_T 的对比提供了关于交互作用强度的额外信息。E_intercept 对应力的
S_T − S₁ 很小（< 0.03），确认 E_intercept 主要通过直接独立通道影响应力，
高阶交互作用有限。α_base 对 keff 的 S_T − S₁ 同样很小，与 α_base 作为反应性
独立主控因子的解读一致。较小的交互项支持两个主要输出在主控因子层面由基本不
重叠的物理路径支配这一解读。

Concretely, these results suggest the following engineering implications:
(i) tightening E_intercept manufacturing tolerance has the highest stress-reduction
leverage, targeting approximately 60% of stress variance, without perturbing keff;
(ii) improving SS316 thermal conductivity consistency addresses the secondary
thermal-pathway contribution (~8%);
(iii) controlling α_base uncertainty is the primary lever for keff stability,
affecting only ~15% of stress variance.
This pathway separation suggests that stress-margin optimisation and reactivity
control can be pursued through largely independent measurement and manufacturing
strategies, reducing cross-interference in the safety-analysis workflow.

具体而言，这些结果提示以下工程含义：（i）收紧 E_intercept 制造公差具有最高的
应力降低杠杆率，覆盖约 60% 的应力方差，且不扰动 keff；（ii）提升 SS316 热
导率一致性针对次级热传导路径贡献（~8%）；（iii）α_base 不确定性管控是 keff
稳定性的首要杠杆，对应力方差影响仅约 15%。该路径分离性提示应力裕量优化和
反应性控制可通过基本独立的测量和制造策略推进，减少安全分析中的交叉干扰。

3.4 观测驱动后验标定 / Observation-Driven Posterior Calibration
------------------------------------------------------------------------

MCMC calibration was performed on 18 benchmark cases (stress range ~102–198 MPa,
drawn from the test split) and 10 extreme-stress reference cases (true stress
133–194 MPa, all exceeding the 131 MPa design threshold). Acceptance rates across
all cases ranged from 0.47 to 0.61; trace-plot inspection confirmed stationarity
after burn-in for all four calibrated parameters (Fig. A1).

MCMC 标定在 18 个基准案例（应力范围约 102–198 MPa，来自测试集）和 10 个极端
应力参考案例（真实应力 133–194 MPa，均超过 131 MPa 设计阈值）上执行。所有
案例的接受率为 0.47–0.61；trace plot 检验确认 4 个标定参数在烧入后均达到
平稳（图 A1）。

The marginal posterior distributions exhibited non-Gaussian features consistent with
the multiplicative stress structure σ ∝ E · α · ΔT. For high-stress cases,
E_intercept shifted toward higher values (higher stiffness produces higher stress,
consistent with the Sobol attribution), and α_base concentrated at values that, in
combination with the shifted E_intercept, are consistent with the observed stress
level through the multiplicative relationship. The joint posterior of E_intercept
and α_base showed a pronounced compensation ridge: parameter combinations where
both are simultaneously at the extremes that produce the observed stress cluster
together, reflecting the structural non-identifiability inherent in the
multiplicative σ ∝ E · α · ΔT form.

边际后验分布显示与乘积应力结构 σ ∝ E · α · ΔT 一致的非高斯特征。高应力案例
中，E_intercept 向高值偏移（更高刚度产生更高应力，与 Sobol 归因一致），α_base
集中在与偏移后的 E_intercept 组合后通过乘积关系与观测应力水平一致的值。
E_intercept 与 α_base 的联合后验呈现显著的"补偿脊"：两参数同时处于产生观测
应力的极端值处的参数组合聚集在一起，反映了乘积形式 σ ∝ E · α · ΔT 固有的
结构不可识别性。

For the 10 extreme-stress reference scenarios (true stress 133–194 MPa), posterior
updating consistently shifted the predictive distribution toward higher-stress
regions. The posterior exceedance probability P(σ > 131 MPa | obs) ranged from
0.63 to 1.0, with 4 of 10 cases reaching P = 1.0. All 10 cases were correctly
identified as exceeding the design threshold. The surrogate prediction at the
posterior mean closely tracked the true stress across these cases (MAE < 6 MPa),
indicating that the posterior localised in the correct region of the parameter
space.

10 个极端应力参考场景（真实应力 133–194 MPa）中，后验更新一致性地将预测分布
推向更高应力区域。后验超阈概率 P(σ > 131 MPa | obs) 为 0.63–1.0，其中 4 个
案例达到 P = 1.0。全部 10 个案例均被正确识别为超过设计阈值。后验均值处的代理
预测与真实应力高度吻合（MAE < 6 MPa），表明后验定位于参数空间的正确区域。

For non-extreme benchmark cases spanning the full stress range (102–198 MPa), the
posterior exceedance probability decreased systematically with lower observed stress,
indicating that the calibration correctly stratified risk across the operating
range: high-stress observations produced P(σ > 131 MPa | obs) approaching 1.0,
while low-stress observations produced values approaching 0.

对于覆盖完整应力范围（102–198 MPa）的非极端基准案例，后验超阈概率随观测应力
降低系统性下降，表明标定在全运行范围内正确划分了风险等级：高应力观测产生
P(σ > 131 MPa | obs) 接近 1.0，低应力观测产生接近 0 的值。

4. 讨论 / Discussion
================================================================================

[4.1 多物理耦合作为不确定性调节机制]

Multi-physics coupling as an uncertainty modulator. The 29% reduction in stress
standard deviation and roughly 95-fold keff compression from decoupled to coupled
steady-state are direct consequences of the negative reactivity feedback in the HPR
system. This feedback—whereby thermal expansion reduces keff and power density,
partially offsetting the temperature increase—acts as a physical uncertainty
attenuator that narrows the output distributions. The magnitude of this compression
is not a surrogate artefact: it reflects the physical self-regulation inherent in the
coupled system. Chen et al. (2025, 核动力工程) observed qualitatively similar
uncertainty compression effects in their HPR multi-physics analysis, noting that
keff showed strong negative feedback under material perturbation; the present work
extends this by providing a quantitative decomposition of the compression for both
stress (29%) and keff (~95×) using the surrogate framework.

多物理耦合作为不确定性调节机制。从解耦到耦合稳态，29% 的应力标准差降低和
约 95 倍的 keff 压缩是 HPR 系统负反应性反馈的直接后果。这一反馈——热膨胀降低
keff 和功率密度，部分抵消温度升高——作为物理不确定性衰减器收窄了输出分布。
压缩的量级不是代理模型的伪象，而是反映耦合系统固有的物理自调节。Chen et al.
（2025, 核动力工程）在其 HPR 多物理分析中观测到定性上类似的不确定性压缩效应，
指出 keff 在材料摄动下呈现强负反馈；本文利用代理框架对应力（29%）和 keff
（~95×）的压缩进行了定量分解，扩展了上述发现。

A practical implication is that performing uncertainty propagation at the decoupled
level would overestimate the tail stress risk by approximately a factor of two. For
safety-margin characterisation, this overestimate is conservative—it would not lead
to unsafe decisions—but it would inflate the apparent safety penalty associated with
material-property uncertainty, potentially leading to unnecessarily restrictive
manufacturing tolerances or operational constraints. The surrogate framework enables
the quantitative comparison of decoupled and coupled UQ at negligible additional
cost, supporting more realistic safety-margin estimation.

实际含义是在解耦层次开展不确定性传播将系统性高估尾部应力风险约两倍。对于安全
裕量表征，该高估是保守的——不会导致不安全决策——但会夸大与材料性能不确定性
相关的表观安全惩罚，可能导致不必要的严格制造公差或运行约束。代理框架以可忽略
的额外成本实现解耦与耦合 UQ 的定量比较，支持更真实的安全裕量估计。

[4.2 物理正则化为何重要]

Physics regularization and model robustness. The selection of the
physics-regularized surrogate over the unconstrained baseline is supported by three
lines of evidence. First, in-distribution accuracy was comparable between the two
models (stress R² differed by +0.001, Appendix), confirming that the regularization
did not degrade nominal performance. Second, the OOD test under α_base
extrapolation—the most physically consequential distributional shift in this system,
as α_base is the dominant keff driver—showed a ΔR² of +0.170 for stress, directly
attributable to the monotonicity constraint preventing non-physical gradient
reversals in data-sparse regions. Third, both surrogates identified E_intercept as
the dominant stress factor with consistent S₁ values (baseline: 0.613, regularized:
0.586), providing cross-model validation of the Sobol decomposition. The modest
decrease in S₁ under regularization is consistent with the redistribution of
attribution toward physically appropriate secondary factors (α_base, ν).

物理正则化与模型鲁棒性。选择物理正则化代理而非无约束基线有三条证据支持。
第一，分布内精度在两个模型间相当（应力 R² 差异 +0.001，见附录），确认正则化
未降低标称性能。第二，α_base 外推的 OOD 测试——本系统中最具物理后果的分布偏移，
因为 α_base 是 keff 的主控因子——显示应力 ΔR² = +0.170，直接归因于单调性约束
防止了数据稀疏区域的非物理梯度反转。第三，两种代理均将 E_intercept 识别为
应力主控因子，S₁ 值一致（基线：0.613，正则化：0.586），提供了 Sobol 分解的
跨模型验证。正则化下 S₁ 的小幅降低与归因向物理合理的次级因子重新分配一致。

[4.3 工程设计与测量含义]

Implications for design, measurement, and risk assessment. The Sobol pathway
separation between stress (dominated by E_intercept) and keff (dominated by α_base)
has concrete engineering implications. For stress risk reduction, the highest
leverage lies in tightening the manufacturing tolerance of E_intercept (targeting
~60% of stress variance), followed by improving SS316 thermal conductivity
consistency (~8%). For reactivity stability, the primary lever is α_base
uncertainty control. Because these two sets of parameters have limited
cross-influence on each other's target output, stress-margin optimisation and
reactivity control can be pursued through largely independent material
characterisation and quality-control programmes.

设计、测量与风险评估含义。Sobol 路径分离——应力由 E_intercept 主导，keff 由
α_base 主导——具有具体的工程含义。对于应力风险降低，最高杠杆率在于收紧
E_intercept 的制造公差（覆盖约 60% 的应力方差），其次是提升 SS316 热导率一致性
（约 8%）。对于反应性稳定性，首要杠杆是 α_base 不确定性管控。由于这两组参数
对彼此目标输出的交叉影响有限，应力裕量优化和反应性控制可通过基本独立的材料
表征和质量控制项目推进。

The posterior calibration results suggest that a sequential experimental design
strategy is viable: prior-based surrogate screening can identify high-risk parameter
regions, and subsequent limited in-situ measurements can sharpen the risk estimate.
For the extreme-stress cases examined here, conditioning on a single observation
vector was sufficient to shift the posterior exceedance probability from the prior
range to values consistent with the true stress level. This approach is compatible
with the phased commissioning and qualification testing typical of advanced reactor
programmes.

后验标定结果提示序贯实验设计策略可行：基于先验的代理筛查可识别高风险参数区域，
后续有限的原位测量可精化风险估计。对于本文考察的极端应力案例，以单次观测向量
为条件即可将后验超阈概率从先验范围偏移至与真实应力水平一致的值。该方法与先进
反应堆工程中典型的分阶段调试和鉴定测试兼容。

[4.4 计算效率]

Computational efficiency. The surrogate achieved a single-sample CPU latency of
approximately 1.55 × 10⁻⁴ s and a per-sample GPU batch latency of approximately
1.70 × 10⁻⁷ s, compared to approximately 2000 s for the coupled HF solver. The
resulting speedup is approximately 1.3 × 10⁷ (single-sample comparison) or
approximately 1.2 × 10¹⁰ (GPU batched per-sample), enabling full UQ analyses—
including 20,000-sample Monte Carlo propagation, 50-replication Sobol analysis,
and 18-case MCMC calibration—within minutes of wall-clock time. This speedup
makes iterative design-space exploration and real-time risk monitoring
computationally feasible for HPR systems with significant material uncertainty.

计算效率。代理模型单次 CPU 推断约 1.55 × 10⁻⁴ 秒，GPU 批推断约 1.70 × 10⁻⁷
秒，对比耦合高保真求解器约 2000 秒。加速比约 1.3 × 10⁷（单样本对比）或约
1.2 × 10¹⁰（GPU 批量每样本），使完整 UQ 分析——包括 2 万样本蒙特卡洛传播、
50 次重复 Sobol 分析和 18 例 MCMC 标定——可在数分钟内完成。该加速比使具有显著
材料不确定性的 HPR 系统的迭代设计空间探索和实时风险监控在计算上可行。

[4.5 局限性与适用范围]

Limitations and scope of applicability. Several limitations scope the conclusions
of this study.

局限性与适用范围。以下几项局限界定了本研究结论的适用范围。

First, the surrogate was trained on a fixed dataset of 2900 samples from a single
HPR design point (the MegaPower reference design). Changes in reactor geometry,
fuel enrichment, or operating conditions would require retraining the surrogate on
a new dataset. The training cost (~2900 HF evaluations × ~33 min ≈ ~67 compute-days)
is a one-time investment; the subsequent UQ analyses incur negligible cost.

第一，代理模型在单一 HPR 设计点（MegaPower 参考设计）的 2900 个样本固定数据集
上训练。反应堆几何、燃料富集度或运行工况的改变需要在新数据集上重新训练代理。
训练成本（~2900 次 HF 评估 × ~33 分钟 ≈ ~67 个计算日）为一次性投资；后续 UQ
分析的成本可忽略。

Second, the MCMC calibration fixed 4 of 8 parameters at their true test-case values.
As discussed in Section 2.6, this represents a partial-observation scenario; the
reported posterior uncertainty reduction is a lower bound on what could be achieved
with full 8-dimensional calibration. In particular, releasing k_ref,SS316—the
third-ranked stress sensitivity driver—would require independent thermal conductivity
measurements but would allow the posterior to further contract in the stress-relevant
conductivity direction.

第二，MCMC 标定将 8 个参数中的 4 个固定在真实测试案例值。如第 2.6 节讨论，这
代表部分观测场景；报告的后验不确定性压缩量是完整 8 维标定可达到的下界。特别地，
放开 k_ref,SS316（第三灵敏因子）需要独立热导率测量，但将允许后验进一步收缩。

Third, under OOD conditions, keff prediction degraded substantially for both
surrogate variants (R² ≈ 0.29 in Appendix OOD tests), indicating that keff
extrapolation beyond the training range is unreliable with the current architecture.
This limitation is specific to keff: stress extrapolation under α_base shift
retained R² = 0.944 with physics regularization, demonstrating that the
regularization is effective where applicable monotone constraints exist.

第三，在 OOD 条件下，两种代理变体的 keff 预测均显著退化（附录 OOD 测试
R² ≈ 0.29），表明当前架构的 keff 外推在训练范围之外不可靠。此局限性专属于
keff：α_base 偏移下的应力外推在物理正则化下保持 R² = 0.944，证明正则化在
存在适用单调约束的地方是有效的。

Fourth, the decoupled keff from the first iteration does not represent a physical
steady-state criticality measure and should not be used for criticality safety
assessment; it is included solely to quantify the scale of coupling-driven
uncertainty compression.

第四，来自第一轮迭代的解耦 keff 不代表物理稳态临界条件，不应用于临界安全评估；
纳入它仅为量化耦合驱动的不确定性压缩尺度。


================================================================================
5. 结论 / Conclusion
================================================================================

This work presented a probabilistic surrogate framework for coupled multi-physics
uncertainty-to-risk analysis in a heat-pipe-cooled reactor. The four principal
findings are:

本文提出了热管冷却反应堆耦合多物理场不确定性–风险分析的概率代理框架，四项
主要发现如下：

(1) Physics-consistent regularization improves out-of-distribution robustness
without sacrificing in-distribution accuracy. The monotonicity- and
inequality-constrained HeteroMLP maintained stress R² = 0.929 and PICP₉₀ = 0.947
in-distribution, while achieving ΔR² = +0.170 under α_base extrapolation relative
to the unconstrained baseline.

（1）物理一致的正则化在不牺牲分布内精度的前提下提升分布外鲁棒性。单调性和
不等式约束的 HeteroMLP 在分布内保持应力 R² = 0.929 和 PICP₉₀ = 0.947，
同时在 α_base 外推下相对于无约束基线实现 ΔR² = +0.170。

(2) Multi-physics coupling compresses material-parameter-driven uncertainty. The
coupled steady-state stress standard deviation was 29% smaller than the decoupled
prediction, and keff uncertainty was compressed roughly 95-fold—direct consequences of the
neutronics–thermal negative feedback that is absent in decoupled analysis.

（2）多物理耦合压缩材料参数驱动的不确定性。耦合稳态应力标准差比解耦预测小
29%，keff 不确定性压缩约 95 倍——中子–热负反馈的直接体现，解耦分析中不存在
该效应。

(3) Stress and keff are governed by distinct sensitivity pathways. E_intercept is
the dominant stress driver (S₁ = 0.586) and α_base the dominant keff driver
(S₁ = 0.771), with limited cross-interaction at the dominant-factor level. This
pathway separation supports modular design strategies where stress-margin and
reactivity-control interventions can be optimised largely independently.

（3）应力和 keff 受不同的敏感性路径支配。E_intercept 是应力主控因子
（S₁ = 0.586），α_base 是 keff 主控因子（S₁ = 0.771），在主控因子层面交叉
作用有限。该路径分离性支持模块化设计策略——应力裕量和反应性控制的干预措施
可基本独立地优化。

(4) Observation-driven posterior calibration correctly identifies high-risk
parameter regions. For 10 high-stress benchmark scenarios (true stress 133–194 MPa),
posterior updating shifted the exceedance probability to 0.63–1.0, with all cases
correctly classified relative to the 131 MPa design threshold.

（4）观测驱动的后验标定正确识别高风险参数区域。对于 10 个高应力基准场景
（真实应力 133–194 MPa），后验更新将超阈概率偏移至 0.63–1.0，所有案例相对于
131 MPa 设计阈值均被正确分类。

The computational speedup exceeding 10⁷ relative to the coupled solver makes this
framework suitable for iterative design exploration, sensitivity-guided tolerance
allocation, and risk monitoring in reactor systems with significant material
uncertainty.

超过 10⁷ 倍的计算加速比（相对于耦合求解器）使该框架适用于具有显著材料不确定性
的反应堆系统的迭代设计探索、敏感性导向的公差分配和风险监控。

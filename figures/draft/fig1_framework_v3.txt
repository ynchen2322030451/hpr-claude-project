严谨说明 / RIGOROUS EXPLANATION
─────────────────────────────────

Top row (modeling chain), left to right:
  A  8 uncertain empirical / material inputs: E_slope, E_intercept, ν,
     α_base, α_slope, SS316 T_ref, k_ref, k_slope
  B  High-fidelity multiphysics coupling — OpenMC (neutronics, k_eff / power)
     coupled with FEniCS (thermal–mechanical response)
  C  Two-iteration coupling outputs: iter-1 uncoupled prediction (muted) and
     iter-2 converged coupled state (emphasized; primary outputs k_eff, max
     fuel / monolith T, max global stress, wall-2)
  D  Surrogate dataset (n ≈ 2900 input–output pairs from two-iteration HF runs)
  E  Physics-regularized probabilistic surrogate — predictive mean +
     heteroscedastic uncertainty; acts as the unified computational engine
     for every downstream analysis

Bottom row (downstream UQ branches), drawn as three separate lines so the
scientific questions are not conflated:
  F1  Forward uncertainty propagation  →  F1-2  Stress-risk quantification
      (exceedance probability under stress thresholds; main text: 131 MPa;
      appendix sweep: 110 / 120 / 131 MPa)
  F2  Global sensitivity analysis — Sobol S₁, S_T with CI; attribution of
      output uncertainty to the 8 inputs
  F3  Observation-driven posterior inference  →  F3-2  Posterior-informed
      feasible region (feasible-region contraction under multiphysics
      constraints; 18 benchmark cases from the frozen test split)

Visual emphasis choices:
  • iteration-2 box uses a stronger fill (#F9C98A) and thicker border (lw=1.8)
    so that "converged coupled state" is the dominant object of the coupling
    module; iteration-1 is muted (pale tan, lw=1.0)
  • Surrogate block E also uses lw=1.8, signalling that it is the single
    engine feeding all three downstream branches
  • Risk (F1-2) and Feasible region (F3-2) are drawn as consequences of the
    preceding analysis block, not as parallel peers of Sobol — matching the
    logical dependency in the manuscript
  • Three curved arrows from E fan out to F1 / F2 / F3 with italic labels
    (forward propagation, sensitivity decomposition, Bayesian updating),
    making explicit that the surrogate is the shared entry point

Figure 2 v1 — Surrogate Accuracy (three-panel)
===============================================

通俗说明 / PLAIN LANGUAGE EXPLANATION
─────────────────────────────────────

This figure checks how accurate our neural network is compared to the real
simulation results it was never trained on (the "test set").

本图用于验证神经网络代理模型对从未参与训练的测试样本（留出测试集）的预测精度，
从三个不同角度展示模型的准确性。

Panel A (parity plot): Each dot represents one test case. The x-axis is the true
stress from the real simulation; the y-axis is what our model predicted. If the
model were perfect, all dots would lie on the dashed diagonal. The dots cluster
tightly around this line (R² = 0.929), meaning the model is quite accurate.

面板A（预测–真实散点图）：每个数据点代表一个测试样本，横轴为高保真仿真的真实应力值，
纵轴为代理模型的预测值。若模型完全准确，所有点应落在对角虚线上。图中点云紧密聚集于
对角线附近（R²=0.929），表明代理模型具有较高的预测精度。

Panel B (R² bars): Compares accuracy across five important outputs. The blue bars
(regularized model) are consistently at or above the grey bars (basic model). Wall
expansion is nearly perfect (R² ≈ 1.0). Temperature outputs are harder to predict
(R² ≈ 0.58) but absolute errors are still small (< 5 K).

面板B（R²柱状对比图）：对比5个主要输出量的预测精度。蓝色柱（物理正则化模型）在各输出上
持续达到或超过灰色柱（基线模型）。壁面膨胀量的拟合优度接近完美（R²≈1.0）；温度输出
预测难度较高（R²≈0.58），但绝对误差仍在5 K以内。

Panel C (OOD check): Tests what happens when we push the model outside its training
range. For the thermal expansion coefficient (α_base), the standard model drops from
R² = 0.944 to 0.774 when extrapolating, while the physics-regularized model stays
high at 0.944 — a 17% improvement. This validates that the physics constraints help
the model generalize better.

面板C（分布外泛化检验）：测试模型在训练范围之外的表现。对于热膨胀系数（α_base）的外推
场景，基线模型的应力R²从0.944降至0.774，而物理正则化模型保持在0.944——提升幅度达ΔR²=+0.170。
这一结果验证了物理约束有效增强了模型在输入分布外区域的泛化能力。


严谨说明 / RIGOROUS EXPLANATION
─────────────────────────────────

Figure 2 (v1) reports test-set prediction accuracy for the Level 2 physics-regularized
HeteroMLP surrogate on the held-out test set (n = 435, fixed split).

图2（v1）报告Level 2物理正则化HeteroMLP代理模型在留出测试集（n=435，固定划分）上的预测精度。

Panel A — Parity diagram for coupled steady-state maximum global stress:
Predicted mean μ(θ) vs. true y, Level 2. R² = 0.929, RMSE = 7.9 MPa,
PICP₉₀ = 0.913. The in-set annotation reports all three metrics.
Comparison: Level 0 achieves identical R² = 0.929 on this output; the primary
advantage of Level 2 is in calibration (NLL: 0.305 vs. 0.359) and OOD robustness.

面板A——耦合稳态最大全局应力的预测–真实散点图：Level 2模型的预测均值 μ(θ) 与真实值 y 对比。
R²=0.929，RMSE=7.9 MPa，90%预测区间覆盖率PICP₉₀=0.913，三项指标均标注于图内。
对比：Level 0在该输出上同样达到R²=0.929；Level 2的主要优势在于概率校准性（NLL：0.305 vs. 0.359）
和分布外泛化鲁棒性，而非点预测精度。

Panel B — R² grouped bar chart for all five primary outputs:
Grey bars: Level 0 (baseline); blue bars: Level 2 (selected model).
Outputs sorted by R² magnitude: wall expansion (0.996) > stress (0.929) >
keff (0.874) > temperature outputs (0.58–0.59). The two temperature outputs
show lower R² reflecting weak sensitivity to the 8 material inputs in the
coupled regime, though absolute RMSE remains < 5 K.

面板B——五个主要输出量的R²分组柱状图：灰色柱为Level 0（基线），蓝色柱为Level 2（选定模型）。
输出量按R²大小排列：壁面膨胀（0.996）> 应力（0.929）> keff（0.874）> 温度输出（0.58–0.59）。
两个温度输出的R²较低，反映了耦合稳态下8个材料参数对温度输出的敏感度有限；
但绝对均方根误差仍低于5 K，工程精度上是可接受的。

Panel C — Out-of-distribution robustness (stress R²):
Two OOD features shown: E_intercept and α_base extrapolation.
For α_base, Level 2 achieves R² = 0.944 vs. Level 0 R² = 0.774 (ΔR² = +0.170),
consistent with the α_base–stress Spearman monotonicity constraint embedded in
the Level 2 training objective. Source: paper_ood_multi_feature_summary.csv.

面板C——分布外鲁棒性评估（应力R²）：展示 E_intercept 和 α_base 外推场景下的结果对比。
对于 α_base 外推，Level 2达到R²=0.944，而Level 0仅为0.774（ΔR²=+0.170），这一改善与
Level 2训练目标中嵌入的 α_base→应力 Spearman单调性约束一致。数据来源：
paper_ood_multi_feature_summary.csv。

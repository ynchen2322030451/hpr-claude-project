Figure A2 — Prior vs. Posterior Marginal Distributions
=======================================================

通俗说明 / PLAIN LANGUAGE EXPLANATION
─────────────────────────────────────

Each cell shows a histogram of what the prior (grey, what we knew before) vs.
posterior (blue, what we know after observing stress) looks like for one parameter
in one case.

每个格子展示同一参数在同一案例中的先验分布（灰色，观测前的认知）与后验分布（蓝色，
观测应力后更新的认知）。直方图越窄，说明观测提供的约束越强。

Narrow blue histograms = the posterior has "learned" a lot from the observation,
constraining the parameter range significantly.
Wide blue histograms similar to grey = the observation didn't give much information
about that particular parameter.

蓝色直方图越窄，说明后验从观测中获得的信息量越大，参数约束越强。若蓝色与灰色宽度相近，
则说明该观测对该参数的约束作用有限。

Generally, α_base shows the most contraction for high-stress cases (the observation
strongly constrains this dominant keff/reactivity driver), while ν (Poisson's ratio)
shows less contraction in most cases.

总体规律：α_base在高应力案例中表现出最强的后验收缩（观测对这个keff主控因子施加了强约束），
而泊松比ν在多数案例中收缩较弱。


严谨说明 / RIGOROUS EXPLANATION
─────────────────────────────────

Figure A2 presents prior–posterior marginal comparisons in a 4×4 grid
(4 parameters × 4 cases).

图A2以4×4网格（4个参数×4个案例）呈现先验–后验边缘分布对比。

Grid layout:
- Rows: E_intercept, α_base, α_slope, ν
- Columns: Case 1 (131.8 MPa), Case 4 (114.4 MPa), Case 0 (225.7 MPa), Case 5 (216.7 MPa)

网格布局：行对应参数（E_intercept, α_base, α_slope, ν），列对应案例（案例1：131.8 MPa，
案例4：114.4 MPa，案例0：225.7 MPa，案例5：216.7 MPa）。

Grey histograms: prior samples from benchmark_case{i:03d}_prior_samples_reduced_maintext.csv
Blue histograms: posterior samples from benchmark_case{i:03d}_posterior_samples_reduced_maintext.csv
(1200 MCMC samples, 4-parameter calibration).

灰色直方图：来自 benchmark_case{i:03d}_prior_samples_reduced_maintext.csv 的先验样本；
蓝色直方图：来自 benchmark_case{i:03d}_posterior_samples_reduced_maintext.csv 的后验样本
（1200个MCMC样本，4参数标定）。

The degree of posterior contraction reflects the sensitivity of the surrogate
output to each parameter (consistent with Sobol analysis):
- α_base: strong contraction for high-stress cases, consistent with its reactivity
  pathway connection via the coupled steady-state
- E_intercept: moderate contraction, reflecting its role as secondary stress driver
- α_slope, ν: weaker contraction in most cases

后验收缩程度反映了代理输出对各参数的敏感性（与Sobol分析结果一致）：α_base在高应力案例中
收缩最强，与其通过耦合稳态反应性路径的主控作用一致；E_intercept呈中等程度收缩，对应其
作为应力次要驱动因子的角色；α_slope和ν在多数案例中收缩较弱。

Note: The posteriors are conditioned on a single stress observation per case.
A full 8-parameter calibration with multiple observations would yield tighter posteriors.

注：各案例的后验仅以单次应力观测为条件。若采用多次观测进行全8参数标定，后验将进一步收紧。

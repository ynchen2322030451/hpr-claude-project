Figure 3 v1 — Forward UQ: Coupling Effect on Uncertainty (three-panel)
=======================================================================

通俗说明 / PLAIN LANGUAGE EXPLANATION
─────────────────────────────────────

This is one of the most physically important figures in the paper. It shows how
"coupling" the neutronics and thermal-structural physics together dramatically
tightens the uncertainty distributions.

本图是论文中物理意义最深刻的图之一，直观展示了将中子物理与热–结构力学进行"耦合"之后，
输出量的不确定性分布如何发生显著收窄。

Panel A (stress distributions): Two histograms on the same axis.
- Orange (decoupled, pass 1): When we run neutronics and structural analysis
  separately without feedback, the stress distribution is wide: average 193 MPa,
  standard deviation 41 MPa.
- Blue (coupled steady-state): When we let the neutronics and structural physics
  "talk to each other" through an iterative feedback loop, the stress distribution
  narrows significantly: average 153 MPa, standard deviation 22 MPa.
This is a 47% reduction in uncertainty spread. The red dashed line is the 131 MPa
safety threshold — 84.7% of coupled scenarios still exceed it.

面板A（应力分布对比）：同一坐标轴上的两组直方图。橙色（解耦，第一轮）代表中子物理与结构分析
各自独立运行时的应力分布，均值193 MPa，标准差41 MPa，分布较宽。蓝色（耦合稳态）代表两套
物理通过迭代反馈相互作用后达到稳态时的应力分布，均值153 MPa，标准差22 MPa，不确定性显著
收窄——标准差降低47%。红色虚线为131 MPa安全阈值，耦合稳态下仍有84.7%的场景超过该阈值。

WHY does coupling reduce uncertainty? Physical explanation:
Materials with high thermal expansion coefficients (α_base) in the decoupled case
produce large stresses. But in the coupled case, those same materials also expand
the reactor core, which reduces neutron leakage, raises keff, and in turn increases
power density — partially counteracting the stress. This negative feedback shrinks
the stress distribution.

为何耦合会压缩不确定性？物理机制解释：在解耦情形下，热膨胀系数较大的材料会产生较高应力；
但在耦合稳态中，相同材料引起的堆芯几何膨胀会减少中子泄漏、提升keff、进而提高功率密度，
从而部分抵消了热应力的增大——这种负反馈效应使应力分布向低值方向收缩。

Panel B (keff decoupled, broad view): Without coupling, keff varies enormously —
standard deviation of 0.0625 (= 6250 pcm in reactivity units). The histogram shows
a wide normal distribution.

面板B（解耦keff分布，宽尺度）：在解耦情形下，keff的变化范围极大，标准差高达0.0625
（换算为反应性单位即6250 pcm）。直方图呈现宽幅正态分布形态。

Panel C (keff coupled, zoomed ×183): The x-axis now shows offset from the coupled
mean in units of 10⁻⁴ (i.e., the total range is about 0.002, not 0.25).
This is 183 times narrower! The negative neutronics–thermal feedback essentially
"locks" keff to a very tight range regardless of material uncertainty. This is
physically very significant: the coupling mechanism acts as a self-stabilizing
regulator for neutron multiplication.

面板C（耦合keff分布，放大183倍）：横轴改为相对耦合均值的偏差，单位为10⁻⁴（即总范围约0.002，
而非解耦时的0.25）——比解耦情形窄183倍。中子–热负反馈机制实际上将keff"锁定"在极窄范围内，
与材料参数的变化基本解耦。这一现象具有重要物理意义：多物理耦合机制本身充当了中子增殖系数
的自稳定调节器。


严谨说明 / RIGOROUS EXPLANATION
─────────────────────────────────

Figure 3 (v1) quantifies the uncertainty compression effect of multi-physics coupling
using 20,000 prior Monte Carlo samples through the Level 2 surrogate (epistemic,
mean-only predictions).

图3（v1）基于20000个先验蒙特卡洛样本通过Level 2代理模型的认知不确定性（仅使用预测均值）
传播结果，定量表征多物理耦合的不确定性压缩效应。

Physical coupling pathways (see also Section 2.2):
Two largely independent pathways transmit material uncertainty to outputs:
1. Stress pathway: k_ref,SS316 → heat transport efficiency → temperature gradient
   → thermal stress (dominant driver: S₁ = 0.529).
2. Reactivity pathway: α_base → core geometric expansion → neutron leakage → keff
   (dominant driver: S₁ = 0.481).
In the coupled steady-state, high α_base raises keff and power, which partially
offsets the stress increase — a negative feedback absent in the decoupled calculation.

物理耦合路径（另见第2.2节）：材料不确定性通过两条基本独立的路径传播至输出量：
（1）应力路径：k_ref,SS316 → 热传递效率 → 温度梯度 → 热应力（主控因子：S₁=0.529）；
（2）反应性路径：α_base → 堆芯几何膨胀 → 中子泄漏 → keff（主控因子：S₁=0.481）。
在耦合稳态中，高 α_base 引起keff和功率升高，从而部分抵消应力增大——这一负反馈在解耦计算中
不存在。

Panel A — Max global stress distributions:
- Decoupled (pass 1): μ = 192.7 MPa, σ = 40.9 MPa, q₉₅ = 264.8 MPa
- Coupled steady-state: μ = 153.4 MPa, σ = 21.7 MPa, q₉₅ = 189.3 MPa
  (from forward_uq_joint_stress_keff_mu_level2.csv)
- Δμ = −39.3 MPa (20% reduction); Δσ/σ = −47%
- P_fail(131 MPa) = 0.847 (coupled, epistemic)
- The 131 MPa threshold (red dashed) is the SS316 yield strength at operating temperature.

面板A——最大全局应力分布：解耦（第一轮）：μ=192.7 MPa，σ=40.9 MPa，q₉₅=264.8 MPa；
耦合稳态：μ=153.4 MPa，σ=21.7 MPa，q₉₅=189.3 MPa（来源：forward_uq_joint_stress_keff_mu_level2.csv）。
均值降幅Δμ=−39.3 MPa（20%）；标准差降幅Δσ/σ=−47%。耦合认知失效概率P_fail(131 MPa)=0.847。
红色虚线131 MPa为SS316钢在工作温度下的屈服强度阈值。

Panel B — keff decoupled distribution (broad scale):
σ = 0.0625 ≈ 6250 pcm — a physically large reactivity uncertainty.

面板B——解耦keff分布（宽尺度）：标准差σ=0.0625≈6250 pcm，属于反应性意义上的大幅不确定性。

Panel C — keff coupled distribution (zoomed ×183):
σ_coupled ≈ 3.42 × 10⁻⁴ ≈ 34 pcm.
Compression ratio: 0.0625 / 3.42×10⁻⁴ ≈ 183×.
This near-complete suppression of keff uncertainty is a consequence of the
neutronics–thermal negative feedback.
Source: forward_uq_joint_stress_keff_mu_level2.csv.

面板C——耦合keff分布（放大183倍显示）：σ_coupled ≈ 3.42×10⁻⁴ ≈ 34 pcm。
压缩比：0.0625 / 3.42×10⁻⁴ ≈ 183×。keff不确定性的近乎完全压制是中子–热负反馈机制的
直接体现：任何使keff升高的扰动同时引发功率增加和温度升高，进而抑制初始扰动。
数据来源：forward_uq_joint_stress_keff_mu_level2.csv。

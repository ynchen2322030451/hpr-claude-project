Figure A1 — MCMC Posterior Traces (4 representative cases)
============================================================

通俗说明 / PLAIN LANGUAGE EXPLANATION
─────────────────────────────────────

Each subplot shows how the MCMC sampler explored the space for one parameter in
one test case. The x-axis is the iteration number (MCMC "step"), the y-axis is
the parameter value at that step.

每个子图展示MCMC采样器在某一测试案例中对某一参数的探索轨迹。横轴为迭代步数（MCMC"步"），
纵轴为该步对应的参数取值。

A well-mixing chain looks like a "fuzzy caterpillar" — randomly bouncing around a
stable level. If the chain drifts or gets stuck, the MCMC hasn't converged.

收敛良好的链呈"毛毛虫"状——在稳定水平附近随机游动。若链发生漂移或卡住，则说明MCMC尚未收敛。

All 16 subplots (4 parameters × 4 cases) show good mixing patterns, confirming
that the 1200-step MCMC runs are adequate for these 4-parameter calibrations.
Acceptance rates were 0.40–0.53 across cases, which is in the optimal range.

全部16个子图（4个参数×4个案例）均呈现良好的混合特征，确认1200步MCMC采样对4参数标定任务
已充分收敛。各案例的接受率在0.40–0.53之间，处于经典理论建议的最优范围（约0.44）。


严谨说明 / RIGOROUS EXPLANATION
─────────────────────────────────

Figure A1 presents MCMC trace plots for four representative benchmark cases:
- Case 1: near-threshold stress (obs ≈ 131.8 MPa)
- Case 4: low stress (obs ≈ 114.4 MPa)
- Case 0: high stress (obs ≈ 225.7 MPa)
- Case 5: extreme stress (obs ≈ 216.7 MPa)

图A1呈现四个代表性基准案例的MCMC迹图：案例1（近阈值应力，观测约131.8 MPa）、案例4（低应力，
观测约114.4 MPa）、案例0（高应力，观测约225.7 MPa）、案例5（极端应力，观测约216.7 MPa）。

Rows: 4 calibrated parameters (E_intercept, α_base, α_slope, ν).
Columns: 4 cases (labelled with observed stress and case type).

行对应4个标定参数（E_intercept, α_base, α_slope, ν），列对应4个案例（标注了观测应力和案例类型）。

Each trace: 1200 MCMC samples generated by a Gaussian random-walk Metropolis
algorithm with a Gaussian likelihood. Chains exhibit stationary behavior with
adequate variance, confirming no evidence of non-convergence or pathological
geometry in the 4-dimensional posterior.

每条迹线：由高斯随机游走Metropolis算法生成的1200个MCMC样本，采用高斯似然函数。
各链呈现平稳行为，方差充分，无收敛失败或4维后验几何结构异常的迹象。

Acceptance rates 0.40–0.53 are consistent with the theoretical optimal ~0.44
for high-dimensional Gaussian posteriors (Roberts et al., 1997).
Source: benchmark_case{i:03d}_posterior_samples_reduced_maintext.csv for i ∈ {0,1,4,5}.

接受率0.40–0.53与高斯后验在多维情形下的理论最优值约0.44（Roberts et al., 1997）相符。
数据来源：benchmark_case{i:03d}_posterior_samples_reduced_maintext.csv，i ∈ {0,1,4,5}。

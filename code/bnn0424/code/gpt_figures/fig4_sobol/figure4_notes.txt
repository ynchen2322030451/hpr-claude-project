Figure 4 — Sobol variance decomposition reveals distinct sensitivity pathways
图4 — Sobol方差分解揭示不同的灵敏度路径

[EN] Caption / annotation notes:
(a) First-order (S1) and total-effect (ST) Sobol indices for coupled maximum stress.
    E intercept dominates (S1 ~ 0.58), indicating elastic-constitutive pathway.
(b) First-order and total-effect Sobol indices for k_eff.
    alpha_base dominates (S1 ~ 0.79), indicating thermal-expansion pathway.
Points: first-order S1 with 90% confidence intervals.
Shaded bands: total-effect ST.
Gray points: S1 whose 90% CI spans zero (not statistically significant).

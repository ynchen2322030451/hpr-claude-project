[EN] Caption / annotation notes:
(a) Prior (uniform support, gray) and posterior (blue) marginal densities for four representative
    calibration parameters. Dashed line marks the true generating value.
(b) Representative joint posterior for E_intercept vs alpha_base, showing a compensation ridge
    (negative correlation rho approx -0.92) — increases in stiffness offset by thermal expansion changes.
    Red cross: true value; black dot: nominal design point.
(c) Prior (gray) and posterior (blue) predictive stress intervals compared with observed stress
    (red triangles) across 18 benchmark cases grouped by stress level.
    Posterior intervals contract significantly while maintaining coverage.

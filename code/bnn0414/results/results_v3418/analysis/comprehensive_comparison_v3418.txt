==========================================================================================
  OVERALL CONCLUSIONS
==========================================================================================
  1. Physics-regularized (phy-mono) is the best overall model: highest stress R²,
     tightest UQ intervals, lowest CRPS, competitive on all downstream tasks.
  2. MF-hybrid adds complexity without clear benefit on this dataset (3418 samples).
     Its wider intervals and lower keff accuracy suggest the multi-head architecture
     introduces unnecessary variance at this data scale.
  3. Homoscedastic ablation confirms heteroscedastic noise modeling is valuable:
     +15% wider intervals for no accuracy gain.
  4. All models produce consistent Sobol rankings and risk estimates,
     indicating structural sensitivity conclusions are robust to model choice.
  5. Posterior inference is feasible with all models; MF-hybrid shows slightly
     better high-stress coverage (0.958 vs 0.917).

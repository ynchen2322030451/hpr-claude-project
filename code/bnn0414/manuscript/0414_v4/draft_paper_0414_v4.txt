摘要 / Abstract
================================================================================

We train a Bayesian neural network (BNN) surrogate on coupled
OpenMC–FEniCS data of a heat-pipe-cooled reactor and use its posterior
predictive distribution as the single probabilistic object on which
forward uncertainty propagation, Sobol sensitivity attribution and
MCMC posterior calibration all operate, without retraining or
recalibration between them. Two-way coupling damps stress variability
by ≈ 30% relative to a decoupled prediction; stress and reactivity are
governed by largely separate parameters (E_intercept and α_base); and
across 18 benchmark calibrations the posterior contracts toward
observation-compatible regions with 90%-CI coverage 0.861. Each
posterior predictive draw costs ≈ 1.43 × 10⁵× less than a coupled
high-fidelity solve (2266 s, AMD EPYC 9654), enabling the full
uncertainty workflow to run end-to-end on a single workstation.

本文在耦合 OpenMC–FEniCS 热管冷却反应堆数据上训练贝叶斯神经网络
（BNN）代理，以其后验预测分布作为前向不确定性传播、Sobol 敏感性
归因与 MCMC 后验标定三类分析共用的概率对象，三者之间无需各自重训
或重新校准。双向耦合使应力变异相对解耦预测降低约 30%；应力与反应性
分别由 E_intercept 与 α_base 主导；在 18 例基准标定中，后验分布收敛
至与观测相容的区域，90%-CI 覆盖率为 0.861。每次后验预测抽样的代价较
耦合高保真求解（2266 s，AMD EPYC 9654）降低约 1.43 × 10⁵ 倍，
完整的不确定性工作流可在单台工作站上端到端运行。


================================================================================
1. 引言 / Introduction
================================================================================

Microreactors — compact, transportable fission systems rated at
1–20 MW_e — are being developed for decentralized power generation in
remote communities, defense installations and space missions [1,11].
NASA and DOE are advancing fission surface-power systems for sustained
lunar and planetary operations [12], while rapidly growing electricity
demand from AI-intensive data centres reinforces the case for
distributed, dispatchable zero-carbon generation [13]. Unlike
conventional large-scale plants, these designs concentrate fission
power, heat removal and structural loading within a compact monolithic
core where neutronics, heat transfer and structural mechanics are
tightly coupled through two-way feedback [1,2]. A single uncertain
material parameter can simultaneously alter the neutronic balance, the
thermal field and the stress state; the closed feedback loop may amplify
or damp the resulting variability in ways that single-physics analyses
cannot capture. The uncertainty problem in these systems is therefore
inherently coupled.

微型反应堆——额定 1–20 MW_e 的紧凑、可运输裂变系统——正面向偏远
社区、国防设施及太空任务中的分布式供能而开发 [1,11]。NASA 与 DOE 正
推进月面及行星任务的裂变地表供电系统 [12]，与此同时 AI 密集型数据中心
电力需求的快速增长进一步强化了分布式、可调度零碳发电的需求 [13]。与
常规大型电厂不同，此类设计将裂变功率、热量排出与结构载荷集中于紧凑的
一体式堆芯，中子学、传热与结构力学通过双向反馈紧密耦合 [1,2]。单个
不确定材料参数可同时改变中子平衡、温度场与应力状态；闭合反馈回路可能
以单物理分析无法捕捉的方式放大或衰减由此产生的变异。因此，此类系统
的不确定性问题本质上是耦合的。

High-fidelity coupled solvers now exist for these systems: MOOSE-based
toolchains handle neutronics–thermo-mechanics–heat-pipe coupling for
KRUSTY-class and larger designs [2,3], and thermal–structural analyses
have established stress concentration as a persistent safety concern in
monolithic-core configurations [4,5]. Surrogate-assisted probabilistic
modules in the MOOSE ecosystem [6,7] and digital-twin pipelines [8]
address forward propagation, sensitivity or calibration individually,
but each uses a separately constructed model with no shared
probabilistic basis between analyses.

高保真耦合求解器已适用于这类系统：基于 MOOSE 的工具链可处理 KRUSTY
类及更大设计上的中子–热力–热管耦合 [2,3]；热–力分析已确认应力集中是
基体式堆芯构型中持续性的安全关注 [4,5]。MOOSE 生态 [6,7] 与数字孪生
管线 [8] 中的代理辅助概率模块分别处理前向传播、敏感性或标定，但每类
分析各自构建独立模型，三者之间缺乏共享的概率基础。

Running the full probabilistic workflow — forward uncertainty
propagation, global sensitivity analysis and Bayesian calibration —
directly on the coupled solver is impractical at ~10³ s per solve.
Gaussian-process and polynomial-chaos surrogates [17] face
dimensionality constraints in nonlinear coupled settings;
physics-informed neural networks [18,19] require a closed residual form
that iterative solver exchange does not supply; deep ensembles [15]
provide distributional predictions but lack a principled posterior over
parameters needed for observation-conditioned calibration [16];
deterministic neural networks produce only point predictions. None of
these provides the coherent predictive distribution that all three
analyses require.

在耦合求解器上直接运行完整的概率工作流——前向不确定性传播、全局
敏感性分析与贝叶斯标定——不可行，每次求解约需 10³ s。高斯过程与多项
式混沌代理 [17] 在非线性耦合设置下受维度限制；物理信息神经网络 [18,19]
需要闭合残差形式，而迭代求解器交换无法提供；深度集成 [15] 可提供分布
预测但缺乏观测条件标定所需的参数后验 [16]；确定性神经网络仅产生点
估计。上述方案均无法提供三类分析共同所需的连贯预测分布。

A Bayesian neural network (BNN) fills this gap. By placing a
distribution over the network weights, a BNN yields a posterior
predictive distribution that can be sampled at negligible cost once
trained. This single distribution serves as the shared probabilistic
layer on which forward propagation, variance-based sensitivity
attribution and observation-conditioned posterior calibration all
operate, with no retraining or recalibration between them.

贝叶斯神经网络（BNN）填补了这一空缺。通过对权重引入分布，BNN 在
训练完成后即可以极低代价反复采样其后验预测分布。该分布充当前向传播、
基于方差的敏感性归因与观测条件后验标定共同使用的概率层，三者之间无需
重训或重新校准。

Our previous studies addressed adjacent but distinct questions. The
high-fidelity coupling and design-optimisation work [9] focused on
deterministic surrogate-assisted multi-objective optimisation of the
reactor's thermal-stress performance; the analysis pipeline there was
point-predictive, with no uncertainty propagation or posterior
inference. The subsequent steady-state uncertainty study [14] used
independently trained surrogate modules, conventional Monte Carlo
propagation and Sobol analysis to quantify parameter sensitivities
under a single-physics-at-a-time workflow, but did not establish a
shared probabilistic layer across analyses or perform observation-
conditioned calibration. The present work is positioned at the
methodological level that neither predecessor reached: a single BNN
posterior predictive distribution serves as the unified probabilistic
object on which forward propagation, sensitivity attribution and
posterior calibration all operate, within a fully coupled multi-physics
framework.

我们前期工作的研究问题与本文相邻但并不相同。面向设计优化的高保真
耦合分析 [9] 聚焦于确定性代理辅助多目标优化，以改善反应堆热应力性能；
其分析链条本质上是点预测驱动的，不涉及不确定性传播或后验推断。后续
稳态不确定性分析 [14] 采用各自独立训练的代理模块、常规蒙特卡洛传播与
Sobol 分析量化参数敏感性，但工作流仍为逐物理场分别处理，未建立各分析
共用的概率层，也未实现观测条件后验标定。本文的定位正是前述两项工作
均未触及的方法层面：以单一 BNN 后验预测分布作为前向传播、敏感性归因与
后验标定三者共用的统一概率对象，且整个分析在完全耦合的多物理框架内
完成。

This paper presents a BNN-based posterior-predictive framework for
end-to-end probabilistic analysis of coupled multi-physics systems. The
framework unifies forward uncertainty propagation, Sobol sensitivity
decomposition and MCMC posterior calibration on a single predictive
layer trained on coupled OpenMC–FEniCS data of a heat-pipe-cooled
reactor. It reveals that two-way coupling damps stress variability by
≈ 30% (standard-deviation reduction relative to the decoupled
prediction), that stress and reactivity are governed by largely separate
parameter pathways, and that the posterior calibration contracts the
material-parameter distribution toward observation-compatible regions —
all at five orders of magnitude less cost per evaluation than a coupled
high-fidelity solve.

本文提出了一套基于 BNN 后验预测分布的耦合多物理系统端到端概率分析
框架。该框架将前向不确定性传播、Sobol 敏感性分解与 MCMC 后验标定统一
到基于热管冷却反应堆耦合 OpenMC–FEniCS 数据训练的单一预测层上。框架
揭示：双向耦合使应力标准差相对解耦预测降低约 30%；应力与反应性分别受制于基本独立的
参数通道；后验标定将材料参数分布收敛至与观测相容的区域——以上分析
的单次评估代价较耦合高保真求解低约五个数量级。

2. 结果 / Results

------------------------------------------------------------------------
2.1 代理精度 / Surrogate Accuracy
------------------------------------------------------------------------

The framework rests on a single BNN that predicts all 15 coupled and
decoupled outputs simultaneously; if this surrogate is inaccurate, the
downstream propagation, sensitivity and calibration results are
unreliable. The full accuracy table is in Appendix D. The main-text
analyses focus on two outputs that bound separate safety margins: peak
global stress in the SS316 monolith (structural integrity) and the
multiplication factor k_eff (reactivity stability).

On 501 held-out test samples, the surrogate achieves R² = 0.944 for
stress (RMSE 7.39 MPa, MAE 5.44 MPa) and R² = 0.849 for k_eff
(Table 2, Fig. 2A). The 90% predictive intervals are conservatively
calibrated; incorporating physics-prior monotonicity constraints
tightens them without reducing coverage (ablation in Appendix B).

The accuracy is sufficient for the distribution-level analyses that
follow: the downstream conclusions depend on the shape and spread of
the propagated distribution, not on per-sample interval sharpness.
Because material-parameter perturbations induce only minimal k_eff
variation (σ ≈ 0.0007, reflecting the narrow reactivity response to
±10% material changes), the coefficient of determination for k_eff is
dominated by noise and can appear misleadingly low. The
calibration-sensitive metric CRPS, which rewards well-shaped predictive
distributions rather than explained variance, shows the BNN performing
competitively across all outputs (Supplementary Table S2). Compared
with MC-Dropout [15] and Deep Ensemble alternatives trained on the same
split, the BNN achieves the lowest CRPS on both stress (4.35 vs 4.50–
4.52) and k_eff (0.000161 vs 0.000175–0.000184); the full comparison
is in Supplementary Table S3.

整个框架建立在一个同时预测全部 15 个耦合与解耦输出的 BNN 之上——若
代理不准确，下游传播、敏感性与标定结果便不可靠。完整精度表见附录 D。
正文聚焦分属不同安全裕量的两个输出：SS316 基体峰值全局应力（结构
完整性）与中子增殖因子 k_eff（反应性稳定性）。

在 501 个留出测试样本上，代理对应力预测 R² = 0.944（RMSE 7.39 MPa，
MAE 5.44 MPa），对 k_eff 预测 R² = 0.849（表 2、图 2A）。90% 预测
区间为保守标定；引入物理先验单调性约束后区间收窄而覆盖率不降（消融
见附录 B）。该精度足以支撑后续分布层面分析：下游结论取决于传播分布的
形态与展宽，而非逐样本的区间锐度。由于材料参数扰动仅引起极微小的
k_eff 变化（σ ≈ 0.0007，反映反应性对 ±10% 材料变化的窄响应），k_eff
的决定系数受噪声主导，可能显得偏低。注重分布形态的校准敏感性指标 CRPS
在所有输出上均显示 BNN 具备竞争力（补充表 S2）。与在相同划分上训练的
MC-Dropout [15] 和 Deep Ensemble 相比，BNN 在应力（CRPS 4.35 vs 4.50–
4.52）和 k_eff（CRPS 0.000161 vs 0.000175–0.000184）上均取得最低
CRPS；完整对比见补充表 S3。

------------------------------------------------------------------------
2.2 前向不确定性传播 / Forward Uncertainty Propagation
------------------------------------------------------------------------

Propagating 20,000 samples from the 8-dimensional prior through the BNN
posterior predictive distribution yields a coupled stress distribution
with mean 161.7 MPa and standard deviation 31.9 MPa, versus a decoupled
mean of 203.5 MPa and standard deviation of 45.6 MPa. Coupling reduces
the stress standard deviation by ≈ 30% and lowers the mean by ≈ 42 MPa
(Table 4, Fig. 3).

将 20,000 个先验样本通过 BNN 后验预测分布传播，所得耦合应力分布
均值 161.7 MPa、标准差 31.9 MPa，解耦预测均值 203.5 MPa、标准
差 45.6 MPa。耦合使应力标准差降低约 30%，均值下移约 42 MPa
（表 4、图 3）。

The coupled k_eff has mean 1.1036 and standard deviation ≈ 77 pcm.
The decoupled k_eff is effectively constant because, at the reference
geometry, the sampled material parameters do not enter the neutronic
cross-section tables; all coupled k_eff variability originates from
thermal-expansion modification of core geometry.

耦合 k_eff 均值 1.1036，标准差约 77 pcm。解耦 k_eff 几乎为常数，
因为参考几何下采样材料参数不进入中子截面表；耦合 k_eff 的全部展宽
来自热膨胀对堆芯几何的改变。

------------------------------------------------------------------------
2.3 全局敏感性分析 / Global Sensitivity Analysis
------------------------------------------------------------------------

Sobol analysis (50 independent replications; full index table in
Appendix C) reveals that stress and k_eff are governed by two largely
separate information channels — the elastic-constitutive pathway and
the thermal-expansion pathway — whose dominant parameters barely
overlap (Table 5, Fig. 4). This separation is the central finding of
the sensitivity decomposition: it determines which observations
constrain which parameters and, consequently, how measurement
campaigns should be designed (Section 3.3).

基于方差的 Sobol 分析（50 次独立重复；完整指数表见附录 C）揭示
应力与 k_eff 分别受制于两条主控参数几乎不重叠的信息通道——弹性
本构通道与热膨胀通道（表 5，图 4）。这一分离是敏感性分解的核心
发现：它决定了哪些观测约束哪些参数，进而决定测量方案应如何设计
（§3.3）。

Stress is rooted in the elastic-constitutive pathway. E_intercept alone
accounts for ≈ 58% of the first-order variance (S₁ ≈ 0.58), and the
near-zero gap between its total-order and first-order indices
(S_T − S₁ ≈ 0.02) indicates that it acts with minimal interaction.
Secondary contributors — α_base, ν and k_ref — add a collective
≈ 29%, while the remaining four parameters are negligible. The
dominance of E_intercept is a direct consequence of the generalised
Hooke's law σ = C(E, ν) · ε: stiffness enters the stress field
linearly.

应力根植于弹性本构通道。E_intercept 单独解释约 58% 的一阶方差
（S₁ ≈ 0.58），其总阶与一阶指数之差接近零（S_T − S₁ ≈ 0.02），
表明交互效应极弱。次级贡献者——α_base、ν 与 k_ref——合计约 29%，
其余四个参数可忽略。E_intercept 的主导地位直接来自广义胡克定律
σ = C(E, ν) · ε：刚度线性进入应力场。

k_eff is rooted in the thermal-expansion pathway. α_base accounts for
≈ 79% of the first-order variance (S₁ ≈ 0.79): it sets the magnitude
of geometric expansion, which alters neutron leakage and shifts the
neutronic balance. α_slope contributes a further ≈ 18%, while no
elasticity or conductivity parameter has a first-order effect on
reactivity above the noise floor.

k_eff 根植于热膨胀通道。α_base 解释约 79% 的一阶方差（S₁ ≈ 0.79）：
它决定几何膨胀幅度，进而改变中子泄漏并偏移中子平衡。α_slope 额外
贡献约 18%，弹性与导热参数对反应性均无超出噪底的一阶效应。

The two pathways share almost no dominant parameters. This channel
separation has a direct consequence for downstream calibration
(Section 2.4): stress observations primarily constrain elastic
properties (E_intercept), while k_eff observations primarily constrain
thermal-expansion properties (α_base). Neither output alone can
substitute for the other, and a calibration protocol that combines both
provides stronger parameter identification than either alone — a
quantitative constraint for any monitoring or experimental-design
strategy that aims to reduce material-property uncertainty.

两条通道几乎不共享主控参数。这一通道分离对下游标定（§2.4）有直接
影响：应力观测主要约束弹性性质（E_intercept），k_eff 观测主要约束
热膨胀性质（α_base）。两类输出不可互相替代，联合标定所提供的参数
识别能力强于单一输出——这对任何旨在降低材料不确定性的监测或实验
设计策略都构成定量约束。

------------------------------------------------------------------------
2.4 后验标定 / Posterior Calibration
------------------------------------------------------------------------

Bayesian calibration [16] uses the same BNN to update the prior
parameter distribution given noisy observations. The four calibrated parameters
— E_intercept, α_base, α_slope and k_ref — are the four that the
Sobol analysis identified as covering the dominant variance channels for
stress and k_eff. Metropolis-Hastings MCMC is run on 18 benchmark cases
drawn from the test split (details in Section 4.6); acceptance rates
range from 0.58 to 0.63, mean 0.61. All four-chain runs satisfy
R̂ < 1.02 (max R̂ = 1.010), confirming convergence with no directional
drift (trace plots and per-parameter diagnostics in Appendix E).

贝叶斯标定使用同一 BNN，在含噪观测条件下更新先验参数分布。四个标定
参数——E_intercept、α_base、α_slope 与 k_ref——正是 Sobol 分析辨识
的应力与 k_eff 方差主通道。Metropolis-Hastings MCMC 在测试集中抽取的
18 个基准案例上运行（细节见 §4.6）；接受率范围 0.58–0.63，均值 0.61。
全部四链运行满足 R̂ < 1.02（最大 R̂ = 1.010），确认收敛且无方向性漂移
（迹图与逐参数诊断见附录 E）。

Across the 18 cases, the posterior 90%-CI coverage is 0.861 — the
observations successfully constrain the parameters that the Sobol
analysis flagged as most influential. In higher-stress cases, the
E_intercept marginal shifts toward larger values, consistent with
its dominant role in the elastic-constitutive pathway. The joint
posterior of E_intercept and α_base develops a compensation ridge:
an increase in stiffness can be partially offset by a decrease in
thermal expansion, leaving the stress product σ ∝ E · α · ΔT
approximately invariant. This partial non-identifiability arises from
the multiplicative structure and would not be visible in a
single-output calibration — it is precisely the cross-coupling that
the Sobol decomposition showed to be weak but non-zero. In short, the
directions along which the posterior contracts most strongly are
precisely the Sobol-identified dominant directions, and the residual
correlation structure reflects the inter-pathway coupling that the
Sobol decomposition quantified.

18 个案例的后验 90%-CI 覆盖率为 0.861——观测成功约束了 Sobol 分析
标出的最有影响力的参数。在高应力案例中，E_intercept 的边际向更大值
偏移，与其在弹性本构通道中的主导角色一致。E_intercept 与 α_base 的
联合后验出现补偿脊：刚度增加可被热膨胀降低部分抵消，使应力乘积
σ ∝ E · α · ΔT 近似不变。这种部分不可识别性源于乘积结构，在单输出
标定中不可见——它恰好对应 Sobol 分解所显示的弱但非零的交叉耦合。
简言之，后验收缩最强烈的方向恰为 Sobol 辨识的主导方向，残余相关
结构则反映了 Sobol 分解所量化的通道间耦合。

At the posterior-mean parameters of each case, the BNN-predicted stress
tracks the true value with MAE = 10.4 MPa over a true-stress range
of 102–198 MPa.

在每个案例的后验均值参数处，BNN 预测应力与真值吻合，MAE = 10.4 MPa，
真实应力范围 102–198 MPa。

3. 讨论 / Discussion
================================================================================

------------------------------------------------------------------------
3.1 耦合反馈与不确定性压缩 / Coupling Feedback and Uncertainty Compression
------------------------------------------------------------------------

The stress standard deviation drops from ≈ 45.6 MPa in the decoupled
response to ≈ 31.9 MPa after coupling convergence, a ≈ 30% reduction.
The mechanism is the negative neutronic–thermal feedback characteristic
of compact monolithic-core systems: thermal expansion modifies the core
geometry, which shifts the neutronic balance and partially offsets the
direct stress increase. What the BNN-based forward propagation
establishes is the net effect of this feedback on the output
distribution. A decoupled analysis does not see the feedback loop and
therefore overestimates the parameter-driven stress variability.

应力标准差从解耦响应的 ≈ 45.6 MPa 降至耦合收敛后的 ≈ 31.9 MPa，
降幅约 30%。机制为紧凑型基体堆芯典型的负向中子–热反馈：热膨胀改变
堆芯几何，偏移中子平衡并部分抵消直接的应力增加。BNN 前向传播所建立
的是反馈对输出分布的净效应。解耦分析看不到该反馈回路，因此会高估
参数驱动的应力变异。

The overestimate is conservative for safety-margin evaluation and cannot
produce an unsafe conclusion, but it inflates the apparent penalty of
material-property uncertainty. In practice, this can translate into
manufacturing tolerances stricter than the coupled response warrants.

该高估对安全裕量评价是保守的，不会得出不安全结论，但会夸大材料不确定
性所带来的表观惩罚。实际上，这可能转化为比耦合响应实际所需更严的
制造公差。

------------------------------------------------------------------------
3.2 BNN 校准与模型选择 / BNN Calibration and Model Selection
------------------------------------------------------------------------

Both the unconstrained baseline and the physics-prior surrogate achieve
zero monotonicity violations on the test set. Incorporating
physics-prior monotonicity constraints modestly tightens predictive
intervals without reducing coverage (stress CRPS 4.42 → 4.35;
full ablation and small-sample data-efficiency analysis in
Appendix B, Tables B1b and B3b). The same posterior predictive
distribution propagates consistently through forward UQ, Sobol
attribution and MCMC calibration — a point-estimate surrogate would
require separate uncertainty wrappers for each analysis with no
guarantee of mutual consistency. Heteroscedastic noise modelling is
retained over a homoscedastic alternative (Appendix B2).

基线模型与物理先验代理在测试集上均未出现单调性违反。引入物理先验
单调性约束可在不降低覆盖率的前提下适度收紧预测区间（应力 CRPS
4.42 → 4.35；完整消融与小样本数据效率分析见附录 B，表 B1b 与
B3b）。同一后验预测分布可在前向 UQ、Sobol 归因与 MCMC 标定之间
一致传播——点估计代理则需为每类分析分别构建不确定性，且无法保证
相互一致。异方差噪声建模优于同方差替代方案（附录 B2）。

------------------------------------------------------------------------
3.3 工程含义 / Engineering Implications
------------------------------------------------------------------------

The channel separation identified by the Sobol decomposition
(Section 2.3) — elastic-constitutive for stress, thermal-expansion
for k_eff — translates into three practical consequences for the
engineering workflow around this class of reactor.

Sobol 分解（§2.3）所辨识的通道分离——应力对应弹性本构通道、k_eff
对应热膨胀通道——转化为此类反应堆工程流程的三方面实际影响。

Information-channel-aware material characterisation. Because the two
output channels are governed by different material parameters, the
characterisation campaign can be designed channel by channel. For
stress-margin assessment, tightening the uncertainty on E_intercept
— e.g. through coupon tensile tests at operating temperature — is the
single highest-leverage action; for reactivity stability, dilatometry
to refine α_base is the priority. Because these two measurements
target different experimental setups, they can proceed in parallel
without competing for the same facility.

基于信息通道的材料表征。两个输出通道受制于不同的材料参数，因此
表征方案可逐通道设计。对应力裕量评估，收窄 E_intercept 不确定性
（例如通过运行温度下的试棒拉伸试验）是杠杆最高的单一行动；对反应
性稳定性，通过膨胀仪测量精化 α_base 是优先项。两项测量对应不同实验
装置，可并行推进而无需争夺同一设施。

Iterative updating. When measured or monitored data become available
— whether from prototype testing, post-irradiation examination or
in-service monitoring — the posterior calibration folds that information
into the same surrogate without retraining. The updated parameter
distribution propagates immediately to all 15 outputs, producing
progressively tighter safety-margin estimates as characterisation
accumulates. If a posterior update reveals that a safety margin is
tighter than initially expected, the Sobol ranking identifies which
parameter to prioritise in the next round of characterisation.

迭代更新。当实测或监测数据可用时——无论来自原型试验、辐照后检验还是
在役监测——后验标定将该信息反馈到同一代理中而无需重训。更新后的参数
分布立即传播到全部 15 个输出，随着表征积累逐步收紧安全裕量估计。若
后验更新显示某项安全裕量比预期更紧，Sobol 排序即可指出下一轮表征
应优先精化的参数。

Multi-output experimental design. The near-zero parameter overlap
between the stress and k_eff pathways means that a monitoring strategy
based on a single composite metric (e.g. a weighted safety index) would
lose information. Stress observations constrain elastic properties;
k_eff observations constrain thermal-expansion properties. A calibration
protocol that combines both provides stronger parameter identification
than either alone, and the Sobol decomposition quantifies how much each
observation type contributes.

多输出实验设计。应力与 k_eff 通道之间主控参数几乎不重叠，这意味着
基于单一综合指标（如加权安全指数）的监测策略会丢失信息。应力观测约束
弹性性质，k_eff 观测约束热膨胀性质。联合两者的标定协议所提供的参数
识别能力强于单一观测，而 Sobol 分解可量化每类观测的贡献。

These indications are model-driven and require verification on the
high-fidelity solver before adoption as design directives (see
Limitations, Section 3.5).

上述指引为模型驱动，采纳为设计指令前须在高保真求解器上核验
（见 §3.5 局限性）。

------------------------------------------------------------------------
3.4 计算效率 / Computational Efficiency
------------------------------------------------------------------------

The BNN produces one posterior-predictive draw, averaged over 50 Monte
Carlo weight samples, in 1.58×10⁻² s on GPU. Batched inference at batch
size 1024 reaches a per-sample cost of 1.7×10⁻⁵ s, equivalent to a
throughput of ≈ 6.0×10⁴ samples/s. The coupled HF solver requires
2266 s, or about 38 min, per case. This figure is the mean of three
independent runs with standard deviation 1.34 s, measured on an AMD EPYC
9654 96-core node with 566 GB RAM and 2× RTX 5090. The resulting
speedups are ≈ 1.43×10⁵ for one posterior-predictive draw, and
≈ 1.37×10⁸ for batched per-sample inference. A 20,000-sample forward-UQ
pass requires ≈ 0.3 s of GPU time excluding I/O, against 4.5×10⁷ s of
sequential HF cost. This is the operating cost on which the full
probabilistic workflow is built.

BNN 代理每完成一次后验预测抽样（对 50 个 MC 权重样本求均值）GPU 时间
为 1.58×10⁻² s。批大小 1024 时单样本推断时间为 1.7×10⁻⁵ s，吞吐量
约 6.0×10⁴ 样本/s。耦合高保真求解器单案例耗时 2266 s，约 38 分钟。
该数字为 3 次独立运行的均值，标准差 1.34 s；测试硬件为 AMD EPYC 9654
96 核节点，566 GB RAM，2× RTX 5090。由此得到的加速比为：单次后验预测
抽样相对 HF 约 1.43×10⁵，批处理单样本推断相对 HF 约 1.37×10⁸。
20,000 样本前向 UQ 推断（不含 I/O）约需 0.3 s GPU 时间，对应顺序 HF
成本约 4.5×10⁷ s。该推断代价构成完整概率工作流的基础。

This speedup enables the full probabilistic workflow — 20,000-sample
Monte Carlo propagation, 50-replication Sobol analysis, and 18-case
MCMC calibration — to run within minutes of wall-clock time, making
iterative coupled-model exploration computationally tractable for HPR
systems with significant material uncertainty.

该加速使完整概率工作流——20,000 样本蒙特卡洛传播、50 次重复 Sobol
分析与 18 例 MCMC 标定——可在数分钟内完成，使具有显著材料不确定性的
HPR 系统的迭代耦合模型探索在计算上可行。

------------------------------------------------------------------------
3.5 局限性 / Limitations
------------------------------------------------------------------------

The surrogate is trained on data from a single HPR design point. All
quantitative results describe this specific configuration only; a
different geometry, fuel enrichment or operating envelope would require
regenerating the training set and retraining the BNN. The methodology
itself is not design-specific: the same workflow of coupled-data
generation, BNN training and three-stage probabilistic analysis is
directly applicable to other reactor types and other coupled
multi-physics systems.

代理仅在单一 HPR 设计点的数据上训练，所有定量结果仅描述该构型。
不同几何、燃料富集度或运行工况需要重新生成训练集并重训 BNN。但方法
本身不依赖于特定设计：耦合数据生成、BNN 训练与三阶段概率分析的
同一工作流可直接应用于其他堆型和其他耦合多物理系统。

The MCMC calibration fixes four parameters (E_slope, T_ref, k_slope,
ν) at their true values and calibrates the remaining four. Extending to
a full eight-parameter calibration would require additional independent
observations to constrain the expanded parameter space. The reported
posterior contraction is therefore conditional on this four-parameter
scope and should not be directly extrapolated to the unconstrained
eight-dimensional case.

MCMC 标定将四个参数（E_slope、T_ref、k_slope、ν）固定在真实值上，
对其余四个参数进行标定。扩展到完整八参数标定需要额外的独立观测来
约束扩展的参数空间。所报告的后验收缩量以四参数标定为前提，不应直接
外推到无约束的八维情形。

The k_eff surrogate degrades on out-of-distribution (OOD) feature-tail
subsets: on the α_base tail, k_eff R² drops to ≈ 0.31 versus 0.849
in-distribution, while stress retains R² ≥ 0.93 across all tested
tails. The Sobol and posterior conclusions for k_eff therefore hold only
within the training range of the dominant reactivity parameter
(Appendix A).

k_eff 代理在分布外（OOD）特征尾部子集上退化：α_base 尾部 k_eff R²
降至约 0.31，分布内为 0.849；应力在所有尾部子集上均保持 R² ≥ 0.93。
因此 k_eff 的 Sobol 与后验结论仅在主控反应性参数的训练范围内成立
（附录 A）。

All posterior summaries in Section 2.4 are computed on the surrogate
side. An independent high-fidelity re-evaluation at the posterior-mean
parameter vectors (Appendix N; 18 cases × 3 posterior labels = 54 HF
runs) confirms consistency: stress MAE at the posterior mean is
5.65 MPa (4.5% relative) and the [5%, 95%] HF envelope covers all 18
true stress values. The present study uses synthetic observations
constructed from the high-fidelity solver because no real operational
data are available for this design. If experimental or monitoring data
become available in the future, the same posterior calibration workflow
can ingest real observations directly.

§2.4 的所有后验汇总量均在代理侧计算。在后验均值参数处对耦合求解器
的独立高保真重评估（附录 N；18 案例 × 3 后验标签 = 54 次 HF 运行）
确认了一致性：后验均值处应力 MAE 为 5.65 MPa（相对误差 4.5%），
[5%, 95%] HF 包络覆盖全部 18 个真实应力值。本研究使用由高保真
求解器构造的合成观测，因为该设计尚无真实运行数据。若未来获取实验或
监测数据，同一后验标定工作流可直接接入真实观测。

4. 方法 / Methods

------------------------------------------------------------------------
4.1 材料不确定性模型 / Material Uncertainty Model
------------------------------------------------------------------------

The coupled HPR simulation involves eight material properties of the
SS316 monolith, the structural component that simultaneously bears
thermal stress and transmits heat between fuel and heat pipes. All eight
enter the calculation through the constitutive models for elasticity,
thermal expansion and thermal conductivity of this single material.
Fuel thermal conductivity, heat-pipe working-fluid properties and other
non-monolith parameters are held at their nominal values in the present
study because they do not participate in the stress constitutive law and
their influence on k_eff is mediated primarily through the monolith
temperature field, which the eight selected parameters already control.
The eight parameters are grouped by physical function into three
categories.

HPR 耦合仿真涉及 SS316 基体的八个材料性质。基体是同时承受热应力并在
燃料与热管之间传导热量的结构构件。全部八个参数通过同一材料的弹性、
热膨胀与热传导本构模型进入计算。燃料热导率、热管工质性质等非基体参数
在本研究中固定在标称值，因为它们不参与应力本构关系，且对 k_eff 的影响
主要经由基体温度场传递，而基体温度场已被上述八个参数所控制。八个参数
按物理功能分为三组。

Structural mechanics parameters. The temperature-dependent Young's modulus
of the SS316 monolith is modelled as a linear function of temperature,
E(T) = E_slope · T + E_intercept. E_intercept sets the baseline
stiffness, with nominal value 2 × 10¹¹ Pa. E_slope captures its
temperature softening, with nominal value −7 × 10⁷ Pa/K. Poisson's
ratio ν, nominal 0.31, characterises the multi-axial stress state. These
three parameters control stress through the generalised Hooke's law,
σ = C(E, ν) · ε, where C is the fourth-order stiffness tensor.

结构力学参数。SS316 基体的温度相关弹性模量建模为温度的线性函数
E(T) = E_slope · T + E_intercept。E_intercept 决定基础刚度，标称值
2 × 10¹¹ Pa；E_slope 反映温度软化，标称值 −7 × 10⁷ Pa/K。泊松比 ν
表征多轴应力状态，标称值 0.31。三个参数通过广义胡克定律
σ = C(E, ν) · ε 控制应力，C 为四阶刚度张量。

Thermal deformation parameters. The coefficient of thermal expansion is
modelled as α(T) = α_base + α_slope · T. α_base is the expansion
coefficient at the reference condition, with nominal value
1 × 10⁻⁵ K⁻¹. α_slope captures its temperature dependence, with nominal
value 5 × 10⁻⁹ K⁻². These two parameters serve a dual role. They govern
thermal strain ε_th = α · ΔT, which is the loading source for thermal
stress. They also determine the magnitude of core geometric expansion,
which is the primary pathway through which material properties affect
the neutronic balance.

热变形参数。热膨胀系数建模为 α(T) = α_base + α_slope · T。α_base 为
参考条件下的膨胀系数，标称值 1 × 10⁻⁵ K⁻¹；α_slope 为温度依赖性，
标称值 5 × 10⁻⁹ K⁻²。这两个参数具有双重角色。其一，控制热应变
ε_th = α · ΔT，即热应力的载荷源；其二，决定堆芯几何膨胀的幅度，亦是
材料性质影响中子学平衡的主要路径。

SS316 thermal transport parameters. The thermal conductivity of the
monolith is modelled as k(T) = k_ref + k_slope · (T − T_ref). This form
introduces three uncertain inputs. The reference conductivity k_ref has
nominal value 23.2 W/(m·K). The reference temperature T_ref has nominal
value 923.15 K. The temperature-dependence coefficient k_slope has
nominal value 1/75 W/(m·K²). Together, these three parameters control
the temperature gradient across the monolith, the physical intermediary
between fission power deposition and thermal stress. For consistency
with the code and data files released with this work, the source names
the three quantities SS316_k_ref, SS316_T_ref and SS316_alpha
respectively. The physics symbols k_ref, T_ref and k_slope are used
throughout the manuscript.

SS316 热传导参数。基体热导率建模为 k(T) = k_ref + k_slope · (T − T_ref)。
该形式引入三个不确定输入。参考热导率 k_ref 标称值 23.2 W/(m·K)；参考
温度 T_ref 标称值 923.15 K；温度依赖系数 k_slope 标称值 1/75 W/(m·K²)。
三者共同决定基体内部温度梯度，即裂变功率沉积与热应力之间的物理中间量。
为与本文公开代码与数据保持一致，源代码中三者分别记作 SS316_k_ref、
SS316_T_ref 与 SS316_alpha。正文统一使用物理符号 k_ref、T_ref 与
k_slope。

All eight parameters are assigned independent uniform priors with
half-width 10% of the nominal design value, θ_k ~ U[0.9 θ_nom,k,
1.1 θ_nom,k]; the priors are summarised in Table 1. The ±10% range is a
conventional uncertainty margin for manufactured stainless-steel
properties. The same prior distribution is used throughout the workflow:
as the sampling distribution in forward UQ (Section 4.4), as the input
domain for Sobol analysis (Section 4.5), and as the prior in posterior
calibration (Section 4.6).

全部 8 个参数赋予独立均匀先验，半宽为标称值的 10%，
θ_k ~ U[0.9 θ_nom,k, 1.1 θ_nom,k]；先验汇总于表 1。±10% 区间是制造级
不锈钢性质的常规不确定性裕量。该先验分布在整套工作流中统一使用：在
§4.4 前向 UQ 中作为采样分布，在 §4.5 Sobol 分析中作为输入域，在
§4.6 后验标定中作为先验。

------------------------------------------------------------------------
4.2 耦合高保真仿真 / Coupled High-Fidelity Simulation
------------------------------------------------------------------------

The high-fidelity (HF) workflow couples OpenMC Monte Carlo neutronics
with the FEniCS finite-element solver through a Picard fixed-point
iteration scheme [9]. The iteration starts from the
eight material parameters and the reference core geometry. OpenMC then
computes the neutron multiplication factor k_eff and the spatially
resolved fission power distribution. These quantities are passed to the
FEniCS thermo-elastic module, which solves for the temperature field,
the thermal expansion displacement and the stress field. The updated
geometry and temperature-dependent material cross-sections are then fed
back to OpenMC for the next iteration. Convergence is declared when the
maximum temperature difference between consecutive iterations falls
below 1 K. An under-relaxation factor of 0.1 is applied to stabilise
the iteration [9].

高保真（HF）工作流通过 Picard 不动点迭代方案耦合 OpenMC 蒙特卡洛
中子学与 FEniCS 有限元求解器 [9]。迭代从八个材料参数
与参考堆芯几何出发。OpenMC 计算中子增殖因子 k_eff 与空间分辨的裂变
功率分布。上述结果传递给 FEniCS 热弹性模块，求解温度场、热膨胀位移
与应力场。更新后的几何与温度相关材料截面反馈回 OpenMC，进入下一轮
迭代。当连续迭代间最大温差低于 1 K 时判定收敛。迭代采用 0.1 的亚松弛
因子以保证稳定 [9]。

Two fidelity levels are retained from this iteration process. The
decoupled single-pass prediction consists of the outputs after the first
iteration, before coupling convergence. At this point FEniCS has
received the initial-geometry power distribution from OpenMC and has
produced thermal-structural outputs without feedback. The coupled
steady-state prediction consists of the outputs after full convergence.
Comparing these two levels isolates the effect of multi-physics coupling
on the output distributions. The BNN is trained to predict both levels
simultaneously.

迭代过程保留两个精度层次。解耦单轮预测为耦合收敛前的首轮输出，此时
FEniCS 接收 OpenMC 的初始几何功率分布，产生无反馈的热力输出。耦合稳态
预测为完全收敛后的输出。比较两者可分离多物理耦合对输出分布的影响。BNN
同时预测两个层次。

Each HF evaluation produces 15 scalar outputs. The 7 decoupled outputs
are average fuel temperature, peak fuel temperature, peak monolith
temperature, peak global stress, monolith mean temperature, core height
after expansion and wall expansion. The 8 coupled outputs add k_eff to
the same 7 thermal-structural quantities after convergence. The
posterior likelihood of Section 4.6 uses 5 primary outputs: coupled peak
global stress, coupled peak fuel temperature, coupled peak monolith
temperature, coupled wall expansion and k_eff.

每次 HF 评估产生 15 个标量输出。7 个解耦输出包括平均燃料温度、峰值
燃料温度、峰值基体温度、峰值全局应力、基体均温、膨胀后堆芯高度与
壁面膨胀量。8 个耦合输出在收敛后于上述 7 个热力量基础上加入 k_eff。
§4.6 后验似然使用 5 个主输出：耦合峰值全局应力、耦合峰值燃料温度、
耦合峰值基体温度、耦合壁面膨胀量与 k_eff。

A dataset of n = 3418 samples was generated by Latin hypercube sampling
over the 8-dimensional prior. The samples were split into training,
validation and test sets with a fixed random seed (seed = 2026): 2339
training (~70%), 501 validation (~15%) and 501 test (~15%). This split
is reused across all experiments for reproducibility. The BNN maps
directly from the 8 input parameters to the 15 scalar outputs without
resolving intermediate coupling fields.

通过拉丁超立方采样在 8 维先验空间上生成 n = 3418 个样本。样本以固定
随机种子（seed = 2026）划分为训练集 2339 个（约 70%）、验证集 501 个
（约 15%）与测试集 501 个（约 15%）。该划分在所有实验中复用以保证可
重复性。BNN 直接从 8 个输入参数映射到 15 个标量输出，不解析中间耦合场。

------------------------------------------------------------------------
4.3 贝叶斯神经网络代理 / Bayesian Neural Network Surrogate
------------------------------------------------------------------------

The surrogate is a Bayesian neural network (BNN) that places a prior
distribution over the network weights and produces a posterior
predictive distribution through mean-field variational inference
(MFVI, Bayes-by-Backprop [10]). The network uses a fully connected
backbone (depth 3–8 layers, width 64–256, SiLU activation) with two
output heads: a mean head μ ∈ ℝ¹⁵ and a log-variance head
log σ²_obs ∈ ℝ¹⁵ that models heteroscedastic observation noise.

代理模型为贝叶斯神经网络（BNN），对网络权重引入先验分布，通过
mean-field 变分推断（MFVI，即 Bayes-by-Backprop [10]）产生后验预测
分布。网络采用全连接主干（深度 3–8 层，宽度 64–256，SiLU 激活），
并列两个输出头：均值头 μ ∈ ℝ¹⁵ 与对数方差头 log σ²_obs ∈ ℝ¹⁵，
后者建模异方差观测噪声。

The model assumes a Gaussian likelihood per output channel,
y_j | θ, w ~ N(μ_{j,w}(θ), σ²_{obs,j,w}(θ)), with an isotropic
Gaussian weight prior p(w) = N(0, σ²_p I). Training minimises the
negative evidence lower bound (ELBO), balancing a heteroscedastic
data-fit term against a KL regulariser toward the prior. The posterior
predictive distribution at a new input θ is approximated by Monte Carlo
averaging over M weight samples from the trained variational posterior
q(w):

模型对每个输出通道假设高斯似然
y_j | θ, w ~ N(μ_{j,w}(θ), σ²_{obs,j,w}(θ))，权重先验为
p(w) = N(0, σ²_p I)。训练最小化负证据下界（ELBO），在异方差数据拟合
项与朝向先验的 KL 正则项之间取平衡。新输入 θ 处的后验预测分布通过
对训练后变分后验 q(w) 的 M 个权重样本做蒙特卡洛平均来近似：

  p(y | θ, D) ≈ (1/M) Σ_{m=1..M} p(y | θ, w^(m)),   w^(m) ~ q(w).

At inference, M = 50 weight samples are drawn; Sobol analysis uses
M = 30 and the MCMC likelihood uses M = 20. The posterior predictive
mean and variance decompose into observation-noise and weight-uncertainty
components (Appendix H). Hyperparameters were optimised via Optuna
(details in Supplementary Table S1). Physics-prior monotonicity
constraints are incorporated as an auxiliary loss term during training;
the ablation study in Appendix B compares this against an unconstrained
ELBO-only baseline and an inequality-constraint variant.

推断时抽取 M = 50 个权重样本；Sobol 分析使用 M = 30，MCMC 似然使用
M = 20。后验预测均值与方差可分解为观测噪声分量与权重不确定性分量
（附录 H）。超参数经 Optuna 优化（细节见补充表 S1）。训练中引入物理
先验单调性约束作为辅助损失项；附录 B 的消融研究将其与无约束 ELBO-only
基线及不等式约束变体进行比较。

------------------------------------------------------------------------
4.4 前向不确定性传播方法 / Forward UQ Methodology
------------------------------------------------------------------------

Forward uncertainty propagation draws N = 20,000 samples from the
8-dimensional prior (Section 4.1) and evaluates the BNN posterior
predictive distribution at each sample, drawing one realisation per
input from p(y | θ, D). The resulting empirical distribution of
{ŷ(θ_i)} reflects both parametric uncertainty in the inputs and the
BNN's own predictive spread. Because the aleatoric component
(σ_obs ≈ 9.7 MPa for stress) is modest relative to the
input-driven spread (σ ≈ 31.9 MPa coupled), the distribution shape
is dominated by input uncertainty. The predictive-mean variant
(Appendix M) confirms that conclusions are insensitive to this choice.

前向不确定性传播从 8 维先验（§4.1）中抽取 N = 20,000 个样本，对每
个样本从 BNN 后验预测分布 p(y | θ, D) 中抽取一个实现。{ŷ(θ_i)} 的
经验分布同时反映输入参数不确定性和 BNN 自身的预测展宽。由于偶然分量
（应力 σ_obs ≈ 9.7 MPa）相对输入驱动的展宽（耦合 σ ≈ 31.9 MPa）较小，
分布形态主要由输入不确定性决定。基于预测均值的变体（附录 M）确认结论
对此选择不敏感。

------------------------------------------------------------------------
4.5 Sobol 敏感性分析方法 / Sobol Sensitivity Methodology
------------------------------------------------------------------------

Global sensitivity is assessed through variance-based Sobol indices.
First-order indices S_i measure the fraction of output variance
attributable to input i alone; total-order indices S_{T,i} include all
interactions involving input i. Indices are estimated with the Saltelli
cross-matrix scheme (N_S = 4096 per matrix, 40,960 surrogate
evaluations per replication) using the BNN posterior predictive mean as
the deterministic evaluator. The analysis is independently repeated 50
times to quantify sampling noise; a factor is treated as a stable
contributor only if the lower bound of its 90% CI exceeds zero.
Methodological details are in Supplementary Methods.

全局敏感性通过基于方差的 Sobol 指数评估。一阶指数 S_i 度量仅由输入 i
单独引起的输出方差比例；总阶指数 S_{T,i} 汇集所有涉及输入 i 的效应。
采用 Saltelli 交叉矩阵方案（每矩阵 N_S = 4096，每次重复 40,960 次
代理评估），以 BNN 后验预测均值为确定性评估器估计指数。分析独立重复
50 次以量化采样噪声；仅当 90% CI 下界严格大于零时对应因子视为稳定
贡献者。方法细节见补充方法。

------------------------------------------------------------------------
4.6 后验标定方法 / Posterior Calibration Methodology
------------------------------------------------------------------------

Posterior calibration uses Bayesian inference to update the prior
parameter distribution given noisy coupled observations. The posterior
is

  p(θ | y_obs) ∝ p(y_obs | θ) · π(θ),

where the likelihood is evaluated through the BNN posterior predictive
mean.

The Gaussian log-likelihood combines two independent variance
components: an externally specified measurement-noise term σ²_obs (2%
multiplicative noise on each observed output) and the BNN total
predictive uncertainty σ²_surrogate(θ), which itself aggregates
epistemic (weight-posterior) and heteroscedastic aleatoric
contributions via quadrature sum:

  σ²_total(θ) = σ²_obs + σ²_surrogate(θ).

This formulation ensures that the posterior contracts only where both
the surrogate is confident and the observations are informative;
regions of high surrogate uncertainty are automatically down-weighted.

Four parameters are calibrated — E_intercept, α_base, α_slope
and k_ref — selected on the basis of their Sobol ranking; the remaining
four are fixed at their true values.

后验标定使用贝叶斯推断在含噪耦合观测条件下更新先验参数分布。后验为

  p(θ | y_obs) ∝ p(y_obs | θ) · π(θ)，

似然通过 BNN 后验预测均值评估。

高斯对数似然结合两个独立方差分量：外部指定的测量噪声项 σ²_obs（对每个
观测输出施加 2% 乘性噪声）与 BNN 总预测不确定性 σ²_surrogate(θ)，后者
通过均方根叠加聚合认识论（权重后验）与异方差偶然贡献：

  σ²_total(θ) = σ²_obs + σ²_surrogate(θ)。

该表达式确保后验仅在代理高置信且观测携带信息的区域收缩；代理不确定性
高的区域被自动降权。

标定四个参数——E_intercept、α_base、
α_slope 与 k_ref——基于 Sobol 排序选取；其余四个参数固定在真实值上。

The 18 benchmark cases are drawn from the test split to cover a
representative range of stress levels. For each case, observations are
the true HF outputs of 5 primary coupled quantities perturbed by 2%
multiplicative Gaussian noise. The posterior is sampled by Metropolis-
Hastings MCMC with 4 independent chains (8000 iterations, burn-in 2000,
thinning 5). Convergence is assessed by the Gelman-Rubin R̂ statistic;
implementation details, trace plots and per-case diagnostics are in
Appendix E–F and Supplementary Methods. Posterior summaries are computed
entirely on the surrogate side; an independent HF re-evaluation at
posterior-mean parameters is in Appendix N.

18 个基准案例从测试集中抽取，覆盖代表性应力水平范围。每个案例的观测
为 5 个主耦合输出的真实 HF 值加 2% 乘性高斯噪声。后验通过
Metropolis-Hastings MCMC 采样，4 条独立链（8000 步，烧入 2000，抽稀
5）。收敛以 Gelman-Rubin R̂ 统计量评估；实现细节、迹图与逐案例诊断
见附录 E–F 及补充方法。后验汇总量完全在代理侧计算；在后验均值参数
处的独立 HF 重评估见附录 N。

================================================================================
5. 结论 / Conclusion
================================================================================

This work demonstrates that a single BNN posterior predictive
distribution can serve as the shared probabilistic layer on which
forward propagation, sensitivity attribution and posterior calibration
all operate — without retraining or recalibration between analyses.
Applied to a coupled OpenMC–FEniCS heat-pipe reactor model, the
framework yields three linked findings. First, forward Monte Carlo
propagation shows that two-way coupling damps stress variability by
≈ 30% relative to a decoupled prediction — a reduction invisible to
single-physics analyses. Second, Sobol decomposition reveals that
stress and k_eff are governed by largely separate parameter groups
(E_intercept and α_base, respectively), so the two observables carry
non-redundant information for inverse analysis. Third, MCMC calibration
contracts the material-parameter distribution toward observation-
compatible regions with 90%-CI coverage 0.861 across 18 held-out
benchmarks, and the posterior-mean BNN stress tracks the true
high-fidelity stress at MAE 10.4 MPa over the 102–198 MPa range.

The surrogate is roughly five orders of magnitude cheaper per evaluation
than a coupled high-fidelity solve. This cost reduction makes the full
uncertainty workflow — propagation, sensitivity and calibration —
executable end-to-end on a single workstation. More broadly, the
posterior-predictive framework converts analyses that were previously
gated by repeated high-fidelity solves into routine computations on the
same probabilistic object. The workflow is not specific to this reactor
design: any coupled multi-physics system for which a BNN surrogate can
be trained admits the same three-stage analysis pipeline.

本文表明，单一 BNN 后验预测分布可作为前向传播、敏感性归因与后验标定
三类分析共同依赖的概率层，且三者之间无需各自重训或重新校准。将该框架
应用于热管冷却反应堆的耦合 OpenMC–FEniCS 模型，得出三项相互关联的
发现。其一，前向蒙特卡洛传播表明双向耦合使应力变异相对解耦预测降低
约 30%——单物理分析无法观测到这一降低。其二，Sobol 分解揭示应力与
k_eff 分别由 E_intercept 与 α_base 主导，两类观测量在反演中提供互补
信息。其三，MCMC 标定在 18 个基准案例上将材料参数分布收敛至与观测
相容的区域，90%-CI 覆盖率为 0.861，后验均值 BNN 应力与真实高保真应力
的 MAE 为 10.4 MPa，覆盖 102–198 MPa 应力范围。

代理单次评估的代价较耦合高保真求解低约五个数量级。这一代价下降使
完整的不确定性工作流——传播、敏感性与标定——可在单台工作站上端到端
运行。更一般地，后验预测框架将原本受制于反复高保真求解的分析转变为在
同一概率对象上的常规计算。该工作流不局限于本堆型：任何能够训练 BNN
代理的耦合多物理系统均可采用同一三阶段分析流程。

at the lowest data fractions and for constrained outputs.

小样本实验使用单一随机种子进行子采样。多种子重复可降低采样噪声，但
不改变定性结论：物理先验优势集中于最低数据比例以及受约束的输出上。


================================================================================
Appendix B2. Heteroscedastic vs Homoscedastic Noise Modelling /
附录 B2. 异方差 vs 同方差噪声建模
================================================================================

To assess whether input-dependent (heteroscedastic) aleatoric noise
modelling improves uncertainty quantification, we train an otherwise
identical BNN with a single shared log-variance parameter
(homoscedastic ablation) and compare it against the
reference heteroscedastic surrogate on the same fixed split
(n_train = 2339, n_test = 501).

为评估输入依赖的（异方差）偶然噪声建模对不确定性量化的改进效果，
我们训练了一个除噪声参数化外完全相同的 BNN，使用单一共享对数方差
参数（同方差消融），并在相同固定划分
（n_train = 2339, n_test = 501）上与参考异方差代理比较。

--- Table B2. Heteroscedastic vs homoscedastic ablation (test set) ---

| Metric            | Hetero (ref) | Homo       | Δ (%)     |
|-------------------|-------------|------------|-----------|
| Stress R²         | 0.9418      | 0.9402     | −0.2%     |
| keff R²           | 0.8445      | 0.8327     | −1.4%     |
| Stress MPIW (MPa) | 40.2        | 45.2       | +12.4%    |
| Stress CRPS       | 4.424       | 4.683      | +5.9%     |
| PICP              | 99.0%       | 99.2%      | +0.2 pp   |

表 B2. 异方差 vs 同方差消融（测试集）

| 指标               | 异方差（参考） | 同方差      | Δ (%)     |
|-------------------|-------------|------------|-----------|
| 应力 R²            | 0.9418      | 0.9402     | −0.2%     |
| keff R²           | 0.8445      | 0.8327     | −1.4%     |

Point accuracy (R²) is virtually unchanged, confirming that the
heteroscedastic head does not harm the mean prediction. However, the
homoscedastic model produces 12.4% wider predictive intervals for
stress and 5.9% higher CRPS, because a single variance must cover both
low-uncertainty and high-uncertainty input regions. In the
heteroscedastic model, aleatoric standard deviation varies from
≈ 3.1 MPa in low-stress regions to ≈ 8.7 MPa at high stress,
reflecting genuine input-dependent noise structure in the coupled
simulation. The homoscedastic model, forced to use a single noise
scale, inflates intervals uniformly and degrades probabilistic
calibration.

点精度（R²）几乎不变，确认异方差输出头不损害均值预测。然而，同方差
模型的应力预测区间宽出 12.4%，CRPS 高出 5.9%，因为单一方差须同时
覆盖低不确定性与高不确定性输入区域。异方差模型中，偶然标准差从
低应力区的 ≈ 3.1 MPa 变化到高应力区的 ≈ 8.7 MPa，反映了耦合仿真中
真实的输入依赖噪声结构。同方差模型被迫使用单一噪声尺度，均匀膨胀
区间，降低概率校准质量。

Conclusion: heteroscedastic noise modelling is retained in the main-text
surrogate because it delivers tighter, better-calibrated intervals at no
accuracy cost.

结论：异方差噪声建模保留在正文代理中，因为它在无精度损失的前提下
提供更紧凑、校准更优的不确定性区间。

Appendix B3. Multi-Fidelity Hybrid Architecture Ablation /
附录 B3. 多保真混合架构消融
================================================================================

We evaluate whether a multi-fidelity (MF) hybrid BNN architecture —
which learns both a residual correction between uncoupled and coupled
outputs and a direct mapping for coupled-only outputs — improves over
the single-fidelity reference surrogate. The MF-hybrid model
(bnn-mf-hybrid) uses a two-head architecture: the first head predicts
uncoupled-pass outputs, and the second predicts coupled residuals plus
coupled-only outputs directly. Both heads share a common input encoder.

我们评估多保真（MF）混合 BNN 架构——同时学习非耦合与耦合输出之间的
残差校正以及耦合专有输出的直接映射——是否优于单保真参考代理。
MF-hybrid 模型（bnn-mf-hybrid）采用双头架构：第一头预测非耦合遍

在 3418 个训练样本下，MF-hybrid 架构在点精度上无改善（应力 R² 相同；

measurable benefit on this dataset. MF strategies may become relevant

uncoupled data are available — a regime not explored in this study.

且有大量非耦合数据可用时变得相关——这一情形不在本研究探索范围内。

\centering

\caption{\textbf{Fig.~S4~|~Out-of-distribution (OOD) coverage and
epistemic uncertainty.}
90\% predictive interval coverage for in-distribution versus four
feature-tail subsets. Stress retains coverage above 0.75 across all
tails. \keff{} coverage degrades substantially on the \abase{} tail
($R^2$ drops to approximately 0.29), indicating that the Sobol and
posterior conclusions for \keff{} are valid only within the training
range of this dominant parameter. Elevated epistemic ratios on the
\abase{} tail confirm that the BNN correctly signals increased model
uncertainty outside the training domain.\\
\small 图~S4~|~分布外覆盖率与认知不确定性。分布内与四个特征尾部子集
的覆盖率对比。\keff{} 在 \abase{} 尾部显著下降。}

\subsection{Note A: BNN training details / BNN 训练细节}

\subsubsection*{A.1.~Evidence lower bound (ELBO) derivation / 证据下界推导}

The BNN posterior over weights $\mathbf{w}$ is approximated by a
mean-field variational distribution
$q(\mathbf{w}) = \prod_i \mathcal{N}(w_i \mid \mu_i, \sigma_i^2)$.
The variational parameters are optimised by minimising the negative
evidence lower bound:
\begin{equation}
\mathcal{L}(\boldsymbol{\mu}, \boldsymbol{\sigma})
= \mathrm{KL}\bigl[q(\mathbf{w}) \,\|\, p(\mathbf{w})\bigr]
- \mathbb{E}_{q(\mathbf{w})}\bigl[\log p(\mathcal{D} \mid \mathbf{w})\bigr]
\end{equation}
where $p(\mathbf{w}) = \mathcal{N}(\mathbf{0}, \sigma_p^2 \mathbf{I})$
is the isotropic Gaussian prior. The KL divergence admits a closed form;
the data-fit term is approximated by a single Monte Carlo sample per
mini-batch (Bayes-by-Backprop\textsuperscript{10}).

BNN 的权重后验由均场变分分布近似。变分参数通过最小化负证据下界来优化。
重参数化技巧使梯度计算成为可能：$w = \mu + \sigma \cdot \epsilon$，
其中 $\epsilon \sim \mathcal{N}(0, \mathbf{I})$。方差参数
$\sigma = \log(1 + \exp(\rho))$ 以确保正性，$\rho_\mathrm{abs,min}$
钳制 $\rho \geq \rho_\mathrm{abs,min}$ 以防止后验坍缩。

\subsubsection*{A.2.~Heteroscedastic observation noise / 异方差观测噪声}

The network outputs both a mean $\mu(\boldsymbol{\theta})$ and a
log-variance $\log \sigma^2_\mathrm{obs}(\boldsymbol{\theta})$ per
output channel:
\begin{equation}
\log p(y_j \mid \boldsymbol{\theta}, \mathbf{w})
= -\tfrac{1}{2}\bigl[\log \sigma^2_{\mathrm{obs},j}
+ (y_j - \mu_j)^2 / \sigma^2_{\mathrm{obs},j}\bigr]
\end{equation}

该异方差公式允许 BNN 建模输入依赖的观测噪声，将其与认知不确定性分离。

\subsubsection*{A.3.~KL annealing schedule / KL 退火策略}

The KL weight $\beta$ is annealed from 0 to its target value
$\mathrm{kl\_weight} = 1.93\times10^{-4}$ over the first 100 epochs:
$\beta(t) = \min(t/100, 1) \cdot \mathrm{kl\_weight}$.

KL 权重通过线性策略在前 100 轮退火，防止 KL 项在数据拟合项找到合理
权重空间区域之前主导早期训练。

\subsubsection*{A.4.~Physics-prior monotonicity constraints / 物理先验单调性约束}

The physics-regularized BNN adds an auxiliary loss:
\begin{equation}
\mathcal{L}_\mathrm{mono}
= \frac{1}{K}\sum_{k=1}^{K}\max\bigl(0,\;
  -\mathrm{sign}\cdot\partial\mu/\partial\theta_k\bigr)
\end{equation}
weighted by $w_\mathrm{mono} = 0.212$. Sign constraints are derived
from constitutive physics (e.g.\ stress increases with \Eint{}).

物理正则化 BNN 添加辅助损失项，惩罚违反从本构律推导的已知单调性关系。

\subsubsection*{A.5.~Gradient clipping and initialisation / 梯度裁剪与初始化}

Gradient norms are clipped at 0.674. Weight means $\mu$ are initialised
with Xavier uniform; $\rho$ values are initialised uniformly in
$[-5, -4]$.

梯度范数按参数组裁剪至 0.674。权重均值以 Xavier 均匀初始化；$\rho$ 值
在 $[-5, -4]$ 上均匀初始化。


\subsection{Note B: Sobol sensitivity methodology / Sobol 敏感性方法}

\subsubsection*{B.1.~Sobol variance decomposition / 方差分解}

For a square-integrable function $f(\theta_1, \ldots, \theta_d)$ with
independent inputs:
\begin{equation}
V = \sum_i V_i + \sum_{i<j} V_{ij} + \cdots + V_{1,\ldots,d}
\end{equation}
The first-order Sobol index is $S_i = V_i / V$; the total-order index
is $S_{T,i} = 1 - V_{\sim i} / V$.

一阶 Sobol 指标 $S_i = V_i / V$；全阶指标 $S_{T,i} = 1 - V_{\sim i} / V$。

\subsubsection*{B.2.~Saltelli cross-matrix scheme / 交叉矩阵方案}

Two independent $N \times d$ matrices $\mathbf{A}$ and $\mathbf{B}$ are
drawn from the joint prior. For each input $i$, a cross-matrix
$\mathbf{A}_\mathbf{B}^{(i)}$ is constructed. The model is evaluated at
all $N(d+2)$ points.

从联合先验中抽取两个独立矩阵，对每个输入构造交叉矩阵，在所有
$N(d+2)$ 个点上评估模型。

\subsubsection*{B.3.~Jansen estimator / Jansen 估计量}

\begin{align}
S_{1,i} &= 1 - \frac{1}{2N}\sum_{j=1}^{N}
  \bigl[f(\mathbf{A}_\mathbf{B}^{(i)})_j - f(\mathbf{B})_j\bigr]^2 / V \\
S_{T,i} &= \frac{1}{2N}\sum_{j=1}^{N}
  \bigl[f(\mathbf{A})_j - f(\mathbf{A}_\mathbf{B}^{(i)})_j\bigr]^2 / V
\end{align}

\subsubsection*{B.4.~Replication-based confidence intervals / 基于重复的置信区间}

The entire Sobol analysis is independently repeated $R = 50$ times. The
90\% CI is constructed from the 5th and 95th percentiles. The BNN
posterior predictive mean ($M = 30$ weight samples) serves as $f$.

完整 Sobol 分析独立重复 $R = 50$ 次。BNN 后验预测均值（$M = 30$）
充当 $f$。


\subsection{Note C: MCMC posterior calibration details / MCMC 后验标定细节}

\subsubsection*{C.1.~Likelihood formulation / 似然公式}

\begin{equation}
\log p(\mathbf{y}_\mathrm{obs} \mid \boldsymbol{\theta})
= \sum_{j=1}^{J} \log \mathcal{N}\bigl(y_{\mathrm{obs},j} \mid
  \mu_j(\boldsymbol{\theta}),\;
  \sigma_{\mathrm{obs},j}^2 + \sigma_{\mathrm{BNN},j}^2(\boldsymbol{\theta})\bigr)
\end{equation}

where $\mu_j$ is the BNN predictive mean ($M = 20$ samples) and
$\sigma_\mathrm{obs} = 2\%$ of the true value. $J = 5$ primary outputs.

其中 $\mu_j$ 为 BNN 预测均值，$\sigma_\mathrm{obs}$ 为真实值的 2\%。

\subsubsection*{C.2.~Proposal distribution / 提议分布}

Symmetric Gaussian random-walk:
$\boldsymbol{\theta}^* = \boldsymbol{\theta}_\mathrm{current} +
\boldsymbol{\epsilon}$,
$\boldsymbol{\epsilon} \sim \mathcal{N}(\mathbf{0},
\boldsymbol{\Sigma}_\mathrm{proposal})$.

对称高斯随机游走提议，尺度通过 1000 次迭代的短试探运行校准。

\subsubsection*{C.3.~Chain configuration / 链配置}

Per benchmark case: 4 independent chains, $N_\mathrm{total} = 8000$
iterations, burn-in 2000, thinning 5. Effective posterior: 1200 samples
per chain, 4800 total. Calibrated parameters: \Eint{}, \abase{},
\aslope{}, \kref{}.

每个基准算例：4 条独立链，8000 次迭代，预热 2000 次，稀疏化步长 5。

\subsubsection*{C.4.~Convergence diagnostics / 收敛诊断}

Rank-normalised split-$\hat{R}$: $\hat{R}_\mathrm{max} = 1.017$ (all
72 parameter--case combinations). $\mathrm{ESS}_\mathrm{min} = 352$.
Acceptance rates: 0.58--0.62 (mean 0.605).

秩归一化 split-$\hat{R}$：$\hat{R}_\mathrm{max} = 1.017$，
$\mathrm{ESS}_\mathrm{min} = 352$，接受率 0.58--0.62。

\subsubsection*{C.5.~Benchmark case selection / 基准算例选取}

18 cases from the 435-sample test split: 6 low (102--119\,MPa), 6 near
(123--129\,MPa), 6 high (143--198\,MPa). Threshold: 131\,MPa.
Observations: true HF outputs $+ 2\%$ multiplicative Gaussian noise.

18 个基准算例取自测试集，覆盖低、阈值附近和高应力区间。


\subsection{Note D: Forward UQ extensions / 前向 UQ 扩展}

\subsubsection*{D.1.~Predictive mean vs.\ full draw / 预测均值与完整抽样}

The main-text forward UQ propagates through the BNN posterior predictive
mean $y = \mathbb{E}_{\mathbf{w}}[\mu_{\mathbf{w}}(\boldsymbol{\theta})]$.
As a consistency check, full predictive draws (sampling both weight
uncertainty and observation noise) produce wider distributions but
consistent means and percentile rankings.

正文前向 UQ 通过 BNN 后验预测均值传播。完整预测抽样分布更宽，但均值和
百分位排名一致。

\subsubsection*{D.2.~Latin hypercube sampling / 拉丁超立方采样}

LHS ensures more uniform coverage of the input space than simple random
sampling. $N = 20{,}000$ samples, $d = 8$ inputs.

LHS 确保比简单随机采样更均匀的输入空间覆盖。


\subsection{Note E: Coupled simulation and material model /
耦合仿真与材料模型}

\subsubsection*{E.1.~Material constitutive models / 材料本构模型}

SS316 monolith parameters grouped by physical function:
\begin{itemize}
\item Structural mechanics: $E(T) = \Eslope{} \cdot T + \Eint{}$;
  $\nu$ (Poisson's ratio). Stress: $\sigma = \mathbf{C}(E, \nu) \cdot
  \boldsymbol{\epsilon}$.
\item Thermal deformation: $\alpha(T) = \abase{} + \aslope{} \cdot T$.
  Dual role: thermal strain $\epsilon_\mathrm{th} = \alpha \cdot \Delta T$
  and geometric expansion.
\item Thermal transport: $k(T) = \kref{} + \kslope{} \cdot (T - \Tref{})$.
\end{itemize}

SS316 一体化结构参数按物理功能分组：结构力学、热变形、热输运。

\subsubsection*{E.2.~Picard iteration / Picard 迭代}

OpenMC--FEniCS coupling: (1)~initialise; (2)~OpenMC computes \keff{} and
fission power; (3)~FEniCS solves temperature, displacement, stress;
(4)~feedback updated geometry; (5)~repeat until $\Delta T_\mathrm{max}
< 1$\,K. Under-relaxation factor: 0.1.

OpenMC--FEniCS 耦合迭代，收敛准则：$\Delta T_\mathrm{max} < 1$\,K，
欠松弛因子 0.1。

\subsubsection*{E.3.~Output definitions / 输出定义}

Each HF evaluation produces 15 scalar outputs: 7 decoupled (before
coupling convergence) and 8 coupled (after full convergence, including
\keff{}). The posterior likelihood uses 5 primary outputs: \keff{},
peak fuel temperature, peak monolith temperature, peak global stress,
and wall expansion.

每次高保真评估产生 15 个标量输出：7 个解耦和 8 个耦合。后验似然使用 5 个
主要输出。

\subsubsection*{E.4.~Dataset generation / 数据集生成}

$n = 2900$ samples by LHS; split (seed $= 2026$): training 2029,
validation 436, test 435.

通过拉丁超立方采样生成 2900 个样本；固定划分复用以确保可重复性。
